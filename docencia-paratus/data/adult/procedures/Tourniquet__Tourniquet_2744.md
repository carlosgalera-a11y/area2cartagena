## 4
    Pass the band through the buckle Place the tourniquet directly on the skin, 5 to 7 cm (2-3 inches) above the injury  Do NOT put the tourniquet over a joint (elbow, wrist, knee, ankle)
## 5
    Firmly tighten the strap to eliminate any space between the skin and tourniquet It should be impossible to place your fingers under the tourniquet at this point

## 8
    Clearly note the exact application time directly on the tourniquet or on the skin next to it
## 9
    Transfer the patient as soon as possible for definitive treatment Tourniquets left in place for longer than 2 hours risk significant ischemic injury
