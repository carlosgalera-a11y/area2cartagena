# Urinary catheterization — Male

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2773


## Tab: Preparation
## Indications
  ### Indications
    Acute urinary retention Precise monitoring of urinary flow (critical condition, shock, acute renal failure) Unconscious patient or in a state of altered consciousness
## Contraindications
    Suspected urethral trauma (macroscopic hematuria associated with pelvic trauma)

## Tab: Execution
## 1
    Confirm the presence of a bladder globe with a Bladder Scan Confirm the absence of allergy to iodine or latex Encourage the patient to urinate before the procedure
## 2
    Ensure patient privacy Position the patient in a supine position with the lower limbs extended, slightly abducted
## 3
    
    Hand washing Open the packaging on a clean surface, make the opening by holding the edges, without contaminating the inside Open the catheter in a sterile manner and place it on the sterile field Apply the fenestrated field, making sure the opening is over the genital area
## 4
    Hand washing Wear sterile gloves Lubricate the catheter from 12.5 to 17.5 cm Place the catheter in the container and place it on the non-fenestrated field
## 5
    
    With the non-dominant hand, gently retract and hold the foreskin back throughout the procedure With the sterile forceps and the antiseptic-soaked compresses, thoroughly disinfect the glans in circular movements from the meatus outwards. Repeat 3 times with new compresses each time Let dry
## 6
    Slowly introduce 10 mL of anesthetic lubricating gel into the urethra while holding the penis in a vertical position Gently compress the tip of the glans for a few seconds to prevent immediate gel flow Wait 5 minutes for optimal anesthetic effect
## 7
    
    Hold the penis perpendicular to the pelvis and gently pull it upwards Maintain the sterile probe with the dominant hand, avoiding any non-sterile contact Slowly and gently insert the catheter into the urethra 15 to 20 cm until urine appears Lower the penis and check the urine flow in the container
## Indwelling urinary catheter (Foley)
## 8
    Gently inflate the balloon with the syringe pre-filled with 10 mL of sterile water Check the correct attachment by gently pulling on the probe until you feel a slight resistance
## 9
    Always reposition the foreskin correctly after the procedure to avoid paraphimosis
## 10
    Immediately and aseptically connect the catheter to the sterile collection bag placed below the level of the bladder
## 11
    Carefully attach the probe to the patient's thigh or abdomen using the sterile device provided, without applying tension
