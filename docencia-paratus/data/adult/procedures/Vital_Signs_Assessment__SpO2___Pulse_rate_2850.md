# Vital Signs Assessment — SpO2 & Pulse rate

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2850


## Tab: Execution
## Indications
    Initial assessment of any patient Continuous or intermittent monitoring during care/transport Rapid reassessment post-intervention
## Contraindications
    None Adapt placement if trauma or injury at measurement site
## 1
    
    Wash hands (alcohol-based solution or soap and water) Wear appropriate protective equipment
    [PROCEDURE] Hand hygiene (hydroalcoholic gel) — Hand hygiene (hydroalcoholic gel)
## 2
  ### Measure blood oxygen saturation and pulse rate
    Prefer a warm, well‑perfused finger Alternative:
## 3
    Clean and dry Remove dark nail polish/acrylics (or pick another site) Keep the limb still
## 4
    Power ON the pulse oximeter Place on the site
## 5
    Allow several seconds Confirm pleth/waveform or signal bar is strong and consistent; without a pulse signal, readings are meaningless Record your findings
## Interpretation
    Respiratory rate: 12-20/min Heart rate: 60-100/min Blood pressure: ~120/80 mmHg (normal > 90/60 and < 130/85 mmHg) Saturation SpO₂: 95–100 % (≥ 94 % acceptable) Temperature:
