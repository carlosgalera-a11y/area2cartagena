# Basic Airway Opening Maneuvers — Jaw Thrust

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Airway & Ventilation
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2823


## Tab: Default
## Indications
  ### =
    Unconscious or semi-conscious patient with airway obstruction  Need to open airway before definitive airway management (BVM, OPA/NPA, intubation). Need to open the airway without moving the cervical spine.
## 1
    
    Place the hands on each side of the patient's head Position the fingers under the angles of the mandible (back part of the jaw)
## 2
    
    Maintain the head in a neutral position (no tilt) Lift the mandible upwards and forwards, which moves the tongue and clears the airways Check the airway patency (chest rise, effective ventilation)
## 3
    Monitor chest rise, ventilation effectiveness, and ensure there are no signs of obstruction.
