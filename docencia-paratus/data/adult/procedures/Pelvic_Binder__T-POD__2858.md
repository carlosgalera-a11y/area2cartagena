- Tipo: procedure

- Entorno: adult

## Tab: Default
  ### Indications
    Presumed or confirmed pelvic fracture Pelvic instability (Open-book) Polytraumatized with suspected pelvic origin hemorrhagic shock
  ### Contraindications
    None

## 7
    Apply regular and symmetrical traction on the tightening straps until a firm and homogeneous tension is obtained around the pelvis
## 8
    Wrap the cord around the Pulley System hooks, then fasten the Velcro-backed Pull Tab securely onto the Pulley System fabric
## 9
    Internal rotation of the patient’s legs improves pelvic reduction Use tape to maintain the rotation in place
## 10
    Check the symmetry and alignment of the binder on the hips Regularly check the neurovascular signs (peripheral pulses, color, sensitivity, and motor function of the lower limbs)
