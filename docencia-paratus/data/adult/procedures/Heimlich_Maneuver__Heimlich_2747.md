# Heimlich Maneuver — Heimlich

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2747


## Tab: Default
## Indications
    Obstruction of the airways by a foreign body (choking) Patient unable to speak, cough, or breathe effectively Absence of visible air circulation (no respiratory noise, possible cyanosis)
    If the person is unconscious, start CPR
    [PROCEDURE] CPR - Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation — Manual
## 1
    Position behind the patient
    Encircle the waist with the arms
    Advance one leg between the patient's legs
## 2
    Close the fist and place it above the umbilicus (under the xiphoid process of the sternum)
    Grab the fist with the other hand
## Caution
    Place your hands on the sternum instead of under the xiphoid if:
## 4
    Give rapid inward and upward thrusts (J-shaped movement) Repeat until the object is expelled or the victim loses consciousness
