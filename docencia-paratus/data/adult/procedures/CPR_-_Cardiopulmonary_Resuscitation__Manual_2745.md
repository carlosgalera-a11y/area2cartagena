# CPR - Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation — Manual

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2745


## Tab: Execution
## Indications
    Cardiac arrest (unresponsive, no normal breathing, no pulse)
## 1
    Make sure the scene is secure for you and the patient If not, the priority becomes obtaining help and/or moving the patient if this can be safely achieved
## 2
    Immediately call emergency services or instruct someone nearby to call Obtain an AED or send someone to get it or send someone to get it
## 3
    
    Turn on AED and follow voice instructions Place electrode pads on bare chest as indicated (upper right chest, lower left side) Ensure no one touches the patient during analysis and before shock delivery Immediately resume CPR after shock, starting with compressions
    [PROCEDURE] Defibrillation — AED (Automatic External Defibrillator)
## 4
    
    Place the patient on a firm, flat and dry surface Kneel beside the chest, shoulders aligned over patient's sternum
## 5
    
    Place heel of one hand on center of chest, interlock fingers of second hand on top Keep arms straight
## 6
    
    Compress the chest to a depth of at least 5 cm (2 inches) at a rate of 100 to 120 compressions per minute
## 7
    
    [PROCEDURE] Basic Airway Opening Maneuvers — Basic Airway Opening Maneuvers
    [PROCEDURE] Bag-Valve-Mask (BVM) Ventilation — Bag-Valve-Mask (BVM) Ventilation
