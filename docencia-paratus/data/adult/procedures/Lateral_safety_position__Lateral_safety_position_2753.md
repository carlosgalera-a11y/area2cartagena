# Lateral safety position — Lateral safety position

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2753


## Tab: Default
## Indications
    Unconscious person but breathing normally Prevention of airway obstruction by the tongue or vomiting
## Contraindications
  ### Absolute
    Cardiac arrest
  ### Relative
    Suspicion of spinal injury Obvious fractures making positioning difficult or dangerous
## 1
    Call the person and gently encourage them Check breathing by bringing your ear close to their mouth (observe the chest, listen to the respiratory sounds, feel the exhaled air)
    [PROCEDURE] CPR - Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation — CPR
## 2
    Lay the person on their back, legs straight and aligned Remove glasses or bulky objects
## 3
    Place the person's arm located on your side at a right angle to the body, elbow bent with palm facing up
## 4
    Place the back of the hand of the opposite arm against the cheek close to you Keep this hand in position
## 5
    Catch the opposite leg at the knee and bend it while keeping the foot flat on the ground
## 6
    Gently pull the bent knee towards you to slowly rotate the person onto their side towards you Keep the person's hand against their cheek to stabilize the head during rotation
## 7
    Tilt the head slightly back to keep the airways open Check that the mouth is open and directed downwards to facilitate the flow of fluids
## 8
    Check that the person is stable (knee on the ground serving as support) Regularly check breathing until help arrives
