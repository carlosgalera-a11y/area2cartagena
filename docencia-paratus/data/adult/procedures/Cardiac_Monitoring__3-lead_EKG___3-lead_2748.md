# Cardiac Monitoring (3-lead EKG) — 3-lead

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2748


## Tab: Default
## 1
    Turn on the cardiac monitor and verify it is functioning Confirm correct cable (3‑lead) is attached
## 2
    Clean skin with alcohol swab to remove oils/sweat Shave hair if needed for good electrode adhesion Allow skin to dry fully before applying electrodes
## 3
    
    RA – Right Arm (White) LA – Left Arm (Black) LL – Left Leg (Red)
## 4
    Attach the leads to the electrodes ensuring firm connection Ensure cables are untangled and secured to prevent pulling
## 5
    Check the monitor for proper waveform and heart rate display Adjust electrode placement or cable connection if artifact or noise is present
