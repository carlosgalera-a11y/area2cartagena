# Vagal maneuvers — Valsalva maneuver (REVERT method)

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2766


## Tab: Default
  ### Indications
    Stable supraventricular tachycardia Clarify the type and origin of a narrow complex tachycardia
  ### Contraindications
    Hemodynamic instability (hypotension, loss of consciousness) Suspicion of acute myocardial infarction
## 1
    Patient Patient in a semi-sitting position at a 45 degree angle Continuous cardiac monitoring (ECG) and SpO₂ Environment Make sure you can quickly lower the head of the bed and raise the patient's legs
## 2
    
    Ask the patient to take a deep breath and blow forcefully for 15 seconds into a 10 mL syringe The movement of the syringe piston confirms a pressure of 30-50 mmHg
## 3
    
    As soon as the 15 seconds are up, immediately tilt the backrest backwards to quickly place the patient in a supine position Immediately raise the patient's legs to 45 degrees and hold for 15 seconds
## 4
    Close monitoring of heart rate Gently bring the patient back to the initial semi-sitting position Can be repeated once if the first attempt is ineffective (43% success rate in REVERT study)
