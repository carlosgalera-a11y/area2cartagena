# EKG Procedure — ECG

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2749


## Tab: Default
## 1
    Supine position, arms relaxed at the sides Bare chest
## 2
    Clean the skin with a compress soaked in alcohol to remove oils/sweat Shave if necessary for better electrode adhesion Let the skin dry completely before applying the electrodes
## 3
  ### 12-lead ECG
    
    RA: right arm, distal (wrist or forearm) LA: left arm, distal RL: right leg (neutral electrode) LL: left leg
## 4
    
    V1-V2 = 4th intercostal space right/left of sternum V4 = 5th intercostal space, left mid-clavicular line V3 = between V2 and V4 V5 = anterior axillary line V6 = mid-axillary line
## Additional Leads
    
    V7: 5th intercostal space, left posterior axillary line V8: 5th intercostal space, left scapular tip V9: 5th intercostal space, left paravertebral line
    
    V4R: Symmetrical to V4 but on the right hemithorax (5th intercostal space, right mid-clavicular line)
## 5
    Associate each cable with the corresponding electrode (color code/letter) Make sure the cables are untangled and secured to avoid any pulling
## 6
    Ask the patient to remain still and relaxed Launch the 12-lead ECG on the device Check the quality of the graph (absence of artifacts)
