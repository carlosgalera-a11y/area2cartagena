# Thoracostomy — Chest Drainage System (Pleur-evac™)

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Breathing
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2954


## Tab: Preparation and execution
## 1
    
    Open the package without contaminating the tubing connector Turn the drainage system stand to stabilize it to the ground
## 2
    Inject the recommended volume of sterile water through the back port into the suction port at the top Usually there is a bottle on the system for this purpose
## 3
    Connect the system to the wall suction with suction tubing Confirm that the float is lifted Adjust the suction level prescribed by the clinician (usually –10 to –20 cmH₂O) by turning the regulator Keep the drainage tubing clamp closed (it will be opened when the chest drain is connected).
## 4
    
    Connect the chest drain to the drainage tubing Secure the connection with waterproof tape Make sure to keep the chest drainage system below the patient at all times
 Ideally ensure the drainage tubing remains above the system The drainage system may also be fixed to the bed rails with it's hangers
## 5
    Open the clamp on the drainage tubing Turn on the wall suction device Gradually adjust suction to at least  -80 mmHg or more until the suction float (usually orange) appears, without exceeding -120 mmHg
