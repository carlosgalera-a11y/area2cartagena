## 2
    
    Expose the patient's chest Quickly dry any moisture on the skin Shave the chest if necessary to ensure proper electrode adhesion
## 3
    
    Remove the electrode protection and apply them firmly according to the visual instructions on the device: Connect the cables to the defibrillator, unless they are already pre-connected
## 3
    Turn on the monitor-defibrillator Confirm the defibrillable rhythm
## 4
    Biphasique: 200 J Monophasic (rare): 360 J
## 5
    
    Announce clearly and loudly: "Preparing to defibrillate. Nobody touch the patient!" Press the "Charge" button
## 6
    
    Once loaded, announce: "Clear out everyone!" Confirm visually again that no one is touching the patient Administer the shock by pressing the "Shock" button
## 7
    
    Immediately after the shock, resume cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) for 2 minutes At the end of the 2-minute cycle, briefly stop to check the rhythm Repeat the procedure as needed
