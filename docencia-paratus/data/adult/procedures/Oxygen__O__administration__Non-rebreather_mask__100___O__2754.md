# Oxygen (O₂) administration — Non-rebreather mask ("100 % O₂")

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Airway & Ventilation
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2754


## Tab: Execution
## Indications
    Severe hypoxemia (SpO₂ < 90 % despite nasal O₂ or simple mask) Acute respiratory distress Pre-oxygenation before intubation
## 1
    Non-rebreather mask (100 % O2)
    Check the integrity of the mask, valves, and reservoir Connect the tubing to the mask and flowmeter
## 2
    Adjust the O₂ flow rate between 10-15 L/min Always make sure that the bag remains inflated during use; otherwise increase the flow rate
## 3
    
    Place the mask on the face (nose and mouth) Adjust the elastic strap around the head for a firm yet comfortable fit
## 4
    Maintain ≥ 10 L/min to avoid collapse of the reservoir Check that the bag remains partially inflated at all times
## 5
    Immediately check the patient's comfort Monitor oxygen saturation (SpO₂) after 1 to 2 minutes to confirm the effectiveness of the device
