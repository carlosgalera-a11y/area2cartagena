# Defibrillation — AED (Automatic External Defibrillator)

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2752


## Tab: Default
## Indications
    Confirmed or suspected cardiac arrest Unconscious person, without normal breathing and without signs of circulation
## 1
    Check your safety and that of the environment Make sure the patient is on a dry and safe surface
## 2
    
    Open or activate the device immediately next to the patient
## 3
    
    Expose the patient's chest Quickly dry any moisture on the skin Shave the chest if necessary to ensure proper electrode adhesion
## 4
    
    Remove the electrode protection and apply them firmly according to the visual instructions on the device: Connect the AED cables to the device, unless they are already pre-connected
## 5
    
    Make sure no one touches the patient (shout clearly: "Do not touch the patient!") Let the AED automatically analyze the heart rate The AED will indicate whether a shock is advised
## 6
    
    If a shock is indicated, make sure that no one touches the patient Shout: "Move away, shock in progress!" Press the "shock" button when the device prompts you
## 7
    
    Immediately after the shock, resume chest compressions and ventilations according to established protocols Follow the AED instructions to continue CPR and for the next cycles of analysis/shock
    [PROCEDURE] CPR - Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation — CPR
