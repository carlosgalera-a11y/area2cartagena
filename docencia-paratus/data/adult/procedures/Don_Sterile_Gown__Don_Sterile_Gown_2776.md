# Don Sterile Gown — Don Sterile Gown

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2776


## Tab: Default
## 1
    
    Thoroughly wash hands with antiseptic solution
    [PROCEDURE] Hand hygiene (hydroalcoholic gel) — -
## 2
    
    Identify the opening of the folded sterile gown neck Firmly grasp the blouse at the neck/shoulder seam from the inside, being careful not to touch the outer surface Lift the gown directly upwards and away from the sterile field/packaging
## 3
    
    Let the gown unfold without touching the ground or any non-sterile object Do not shake the sterile gown
## 4
    
    Gently slide both arms into the armholes simultaneously, keeping the hands inside the cuffs of the blouse Do not let the hands go beyond the wrists at this stage
## 5
    
    Ask someone to grasp the ties and secure them at the back of your neck and hips
## 6
    
    With hands still inside the gown cuffs: Repeat the process for the second hand
## 7
    
    Locate the outer waist tie (often a long strap with a sterile paper tab) Hand the sterile paper tab to the circulating nurse, being careful not to touch their hand or any non-sterile surface Perform a 360-degree turn (pirouette) in place, allowing the assistant to hold the end of the tie and effectively wrap it around your back Once you complete the turn, retrieve the tie from the assistant and tie it securely to the other end of the waist tie at the front of the gown
