# Urinary catheterization — Female

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2855


## Tab: Preparation
## Indications
  ### Indications
    Acute urinary retention Precise monitoring of urinary flow (critical condition, shock, acute renal failure) Unconscious patient or in a state of altered consciousness
## Contraindications
    Suspected urethral trauma (macroscopic hematuria associated with pelvic trauma)

## Equipment Preparation

    Foley catheter
    Sterile gloves
    Foley Insertion Tray

    Normal Saline Syringe 10 mL

    Transpore Medical tape
    Lidocaine gel

## Tab: Execution
## 1
    Confirm the presence of a bladder globe with a Bladder Scan Confirm the absence of allergy to iodine or latex Encourage the patient to urinate before the procedure
## 2
    
    Ensure patient privacy Gynecological position
## 3
    
    Hand washing Open the packaging on a clean surface, make the opening by holding the edges, without contaminating the inside Open the catheter in a sterile manner and place it on the sterile field Apply the fenestrated field, making sure the opening is over the genital area
## 4
    Hand washing Wear sterile gloves Lubricate the catheter from 2.5-5 cm Place the catheter in the container and place it on the non-fenestrated field
## 5
    
    With the non-dominant hand, gently separate the labia majora to expose the urethral meatus The non-dominant hand is now considered contaminated and must remain in position throughout the procedure Using sterile forceps and antiseptic-soaked swabs, cleanse from top to bottom in the following order:
## 6
    
    Insert the catheter into the urethra 5-7.5 cm until urine appears.
## Indwelling urinary catheter (Foley)
## 7
    Gently inflate the balloon with the syringe pre-filled with 10 mL of sterile water Check the correct attachment by gently pulling on the probe until you feel a slight resistance
## 8
    Immediately and aseptically connect the catheter to the sterile collection bag placed below the level of the bladder
## 9
    Carefully attach the probe to the patient's thigh or abdomen using the sterile device provided, without applying tension
