# EpiPen™ Administration — EpiPen™ Administration

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2781


## Tab: Default
## Indications
    Severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis)
## 1
    Call emergency services (911) immediately or ask someone to call.
## 2
    Grasp the EpiPen™ firmly in your dominant hand, with the orange tip pointing downwards
## 3
    Remove the blue safety cap by pulling it straight out
## 4
    
    Position the EpiPen™ against the outer part of the thigh (middle of the thigh) Apply strong pressure until you hear or feel the click indicating that the injection has started Hold in place for 3 seconds Remove EpiPen™
## 5
    Stay with the victim until help arrives Inform the rescuers of the exact time of administration of the EpiPen™ If no improvement after 5 to 10 minutes, possible administration of a second EpiPen™ if available
