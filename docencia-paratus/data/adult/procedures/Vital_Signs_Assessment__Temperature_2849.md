## 5. Oral (buccal) measure
## 5a
    Check that the patient has not drunk, eaten, or smoked in the last 15 minutes
## 5b
    Ask the patient to open their mouth Place the thermometer under the tongue, on each side of the lingual frenulum Ask the patient to gently close their mouth without biting

## 6. Rectal measure
## 6a
    Put on gloves Install the patient in a lateral decubitus position, with slightly bent knees Lubricate the tip of the thermometer
## 6b
    Gently spread the buttocks and insert the thermometer 2-3 cm deep
