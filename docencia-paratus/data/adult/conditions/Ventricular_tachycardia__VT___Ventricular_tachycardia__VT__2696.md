## Refractory
    Correct hypokalemia and hypomagnesemia
    Consider intubation and sedation/analgesia for sympatholysis
    [PROCEDURE] Intubation — Intubation checklist
    Consider myocardial ischemia and reperfusion therapy
    [PROCEDURE] Acute coronary syndrome (ACS) - Myocardial infarction (MI) — Acute coronary syndrome (ACS) - myocardial infarction (MI)
  ### Unstable or cardiomyopathy
    [DRUG] Esmolol (Step 1: bolus) — Refractory VF/VT
    [DRUG] Esmolol (Step 2: drip) — Refractory VF/VT
  ### Stable
    [DRUG] Propranolol (N/A) — Recurring ventricular tachycardia
