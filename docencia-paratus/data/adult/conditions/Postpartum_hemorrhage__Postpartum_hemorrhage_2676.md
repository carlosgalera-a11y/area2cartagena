# Postpartum hemorrhage — Postpartum hemorrhage

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: OB/GYN
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2676


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Evaluate and address causes of bleeding (atony, trauma, retained placenta, coagulopathy) Consider physical (bimanual compressions) +/- surgical treatments as necessary Empty bladder Bakri balloon Vaginal packing if lacerations
## Hemorrhagic shock
    Rapid administration of blood products: consider massive hemorrhage protocol Consider fibrinogen repletion (target: ≥ 2.0g/L) : cryoprecipitate or fibrinogen concentrate
