# Spontaneous bacterial peritonitis (SBP) — Spontaneous bacterial peritonitis (SBP)

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Gastroenterology
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2687


## Tab: Default
## Antibiotics
  ### Consider large spectrum treatment for severely ill patients or patients at risk of nosocomial/drug resistant infections
    [DRUG] Piperacillin-tazobactam (N/A) — Usual dosing
    [DRUG] Meropenem (N/A) — Usual dosage
    [DRUG] Vancomycin (N/A) — Loading dose, severe infection
    [DRUG] Ceftriaxone (N/A) — Severe infection
    [DRUG] Cefotaxime (N/A) — Severe infection
## Albumin
  ### Prevention of hepatorenal syndrome
    [DRUG] Albumin 25% (Day 1) — Spontaneous bacterial peritonitis
    [DRUG] Albumin 25% (Day 3) — Spontaneous bacterial peritonitis
## General measures
    Consider discontinuing or decreasing beta blockers in case of acute kidney injury or hypotension
