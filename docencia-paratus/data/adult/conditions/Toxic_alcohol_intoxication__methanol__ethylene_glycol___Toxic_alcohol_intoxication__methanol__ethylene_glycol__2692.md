## Specific antidotes
    [DRUG] Fomepizole (Loading dose) — Toxic alcohol intoxication
    [DRUG] Fomepizole (Maintenance dose) — Toxic alcohol intoxication
    Consider hemodialysis
## Fomepizole not available
  ### IV Ethanol
    [DRUG] Ethanol IV (Loading dose) — Toxic alcohol intoxication
    [DRUG] Ethanol IV (Maintenance dose) — Toxic alcohol intoxication
  ### PO Ethanol
    [DRUG] Ethanol PO (Loading dose) — Toxic alcohol intoxication
    [DRUG] Ethanol PO (Maintenance dose) — Toxic alcohol intoxication
## Adjuvants in methanol toxicity
  ### Without metabolic acidosis
    [DRUG] Leucovorin IV (1mg/kg) — Methanol intoxication
    If leucovorin unavailable:
    [DRUG] Folic acid (1 mg/kg) — Adjuvant in methanol intoxication
      Alternative
  ### With metabolic acidosis
    [DRUG] Leucovorin IV (2mg/kg) — Methanol intoxication
    If leucovorin unavailable:
    [DRUG] Folic acid (2 mg/kg) — Adjuvant in methanol intoxication
      Alternative
## Adjuvants in ethylene glycol toxicity
    [DRUG] Pyridoxine (N/A) — Ethylene glycol intoxication
    [DRUG] Thiamine (N/A) — Ethylene glycol intoxication
