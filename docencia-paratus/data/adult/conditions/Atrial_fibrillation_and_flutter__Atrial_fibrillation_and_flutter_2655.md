# Atrial fibrillation and flutter — Atrial fibrillation and flutter

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Cardiovascular
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2655


## Tab: Default
## Unstable
    Consider other causes for hypotension: sepsis, pulmonary embolism, hemorrhage, etc.
    [PROCEDURE] Cardioversion — Electrical cardioversion
  ### Rate and/or rhythm control
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (150mg) — VT with pulse or AFib (bolus)
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (Infusion: step 1) — VT with pulse or AFib (drip)
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (Infusion: step 2) — VT with pulse or AFib (drip)
    [DRUG] Digoxin (N/A) — Atrial fibrillation
    [DRUG] Esmolol (Step 1: bolus) — Targeted control for BP and/or HR
    [DRUG] Esmolol (Step 2: drip) — Targeted control for BP and/or HR
  ### Hemodynamic support
    [DRUG] Vasopressin (N/A) — Titratable dose in shock
    [DRUG] Norepinephrine (mcg/kg/min) — Shock
    [DRUG] Norepinephrine (mcg/min) — Shock
    [DRUG] Phenylephrine (N/A) — Shock
## Stable, rate control
    [DRUG] Diltiazem (1st Dose) — AFib/Flutter/PSVT (bolus)
    [DRUG] Diltiazem (2nd Dose) — AFib/Flutter/PSVT (bolus)
    [DRUG] Diltiazem (N/A) — AFib/Flutter/PSVT (drip)
      Infusion
    [DRUG] Metoprolol (N/A) — Afib/Flutter/PSVT
## Stable, rhythm control
    **Criteria for proceeding directly to cardioversion**
      Therapeutic anticoagulation for ≥ 3 weeks Non valvular AF, Duration < 12 hours and No recent Stroke/TIA Non valvular AF Duration <48 hours and CHADS2 0-1 TEE negative for LAA clot
    [PROCEDURE] Cardioversion — Electrical cardioversion
    [DRUG] Procainamide (N/A) — Atrial Fibrillation
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (150mg) — VT with pulse or AFib (bolus)
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (Infusion: step 1) — VT with pulse or AFib (drip)
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (Infusion: step 2) — VT with pulse or AFib (drip)
## Other considerations
    Address the cause
    Correct hypokalemia and hypomagnesemia
    Address high sympathetic output (pain, agitation, fever, etc.)
## Anticoagulation
    Favor direct oral anticoagulants (DOAC) depending on the clinical situation
    [DRUG] Enoxaparin (1mg/kg SC BID) — Venous thromboembolism, treatment
    [DRUG] Enoxaparin (1.5mg/kg SC once daily) — Venous thromboembolism, treatment
    [DRUG] Dalteparin (100units/kg SC BID) — Venous thromboembolism, treatment
    [DRUG] Dalteparin (200units/kg SC once daily) — Venous thromboembolism, treatment
    [DRUG] Tinzaparin (86 units/kg SC BID) — Venous thromboembolism, treatment
    [DRUG] Tinzaparin (175 units/kg SC once daily) — Venous thromboembolism, treatment
    [DRUG] Heparin (Bolus) — Acute coronary syndrome and atrial fibrillation
    [DRUG] Heparin (Infusion) — Acute coronary syndrome and atrial fibrillation
## Post anticoagulation hemorrhage
    [PROCEDURE] Anticoagulation reversal — Anticoagulation reversal
