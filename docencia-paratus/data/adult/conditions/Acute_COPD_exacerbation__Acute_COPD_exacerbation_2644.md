## Antibiotics
    Consider PO antibiotics depending on the clinical situation
    [DRUG] Ceftriaxone (N/A) — Severe infection
    [DRUG] Piperacillin-tazobactam (N/A) — Severe infection
    [DRUG] Amoxicillin/clavulanic acid IV (2000mg (2g)) — Severe infection
    [DRUG] Azithromycin IV (N/A) — Pneumonia
    [DRUG] Ciprofloxacin (N/A) — Severe infection
    [DRUG] Levofloxacin (N/A) — Severe infection
## Corticosteroids
    [DRUG] Methylprednisolone (N/A) — Acute severe asthma / acute COPD exacerbation
## Bronchodilators
  ### Nebulization
    [DRUG] Salbutamol (Nebulization) — Bronchospasm
    [DRUG] Ipratropium (Nebulization) — Bronchospasm
  ### Inhalation
    [DRUG] Salbutamol (Inhalation) — Bronchospasm
    [DRUG] Ipratropium (Inhalation) — Bronchospasm
