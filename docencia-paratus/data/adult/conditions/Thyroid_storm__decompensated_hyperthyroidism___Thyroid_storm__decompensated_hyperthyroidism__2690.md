  ### Normal cardiac function
    [DRUG] Propranolol (N/A) — Thyroid storm
  ### Suspicion for heart failure

    [DRUG] Hydrocortisone (N/A) — Thyroid storm
## Thionamide
  ### Preferred
    [DRUG] Methimazole PO (N/A) — Thyroid storm
  ### 1st trimester of pregnancy
    [DRUG] Propylthiouracil PO (N/A) — Thyroid storm
## Iodine
  ### Wait at least one hour after thionamide administration
    [DRUG] Potassium iodide and iodine 5% PO (N/A) — Thyroid storm
