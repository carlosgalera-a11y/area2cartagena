# Severe agitation — Severe agitation

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Neurological
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2681


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Attempt verbal de-escalation Evaluate for organic cause (hypoglycemia, hypoxia, etc.) Ensure adequate monitoring (as for procedural sedation)
## Benzodiazepines
  ### No IV access
    [DRUG] Midazolam (N/A) — Severe agitation (IM)
  ### IV access
    [DRUG] Midazolam (N/A) — Severe agitation and sympathomimetic toxidrome (IV)
## Ketamine
  ### No IV access
    [DRUG] Ketamine (N/A) — Severe agitation (IM)
## Antipsychotics
    Onset of action is within 30-60 minutes when used alone, therefore monotherapy may be less appropriate in severely agitated or violent patient
  ### No IV access
    [DRUG] Haloperidol (IM) — Severe agitation
  ### IV access
    [DRUG] Haloperidol (IV) — Severe agitation
