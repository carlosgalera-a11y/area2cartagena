# Local anesthetic systemic toxicity — Local anesthetic systemic toxicity

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Toxicology
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2669


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Stop all local anesthetic administration Consider contacting your poison center
## Specific antidote
    [DRUG] Lipid emulsion 20% (N/A) — Local anesthetic systemic toxicity
## Seizures
  ### No IV access
    [DRUG] Midazolam (IM) — Status epilepticus
  ### IV access
    [DRUG] Lorazepam (N/A) — Status epilepticus
    Phenytoin not recommended
## Tachyarrhythmias
    [PROCEDURE] Cardioversion — Electrical cardioversion
    [DRUG] Sodium bicarbonate (N/A) — Sodium channel blocker toxicity
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (150mg) — VT with pulse or AFib (bolus)
    Lidocaine and Procainamide not recommended Beta Blockers and Calcium Channel Blockers not recommended
## Cardiac arrest or hypotension
  ### Ventricular Tachycardia (VT) or Ventricular Fibrillation (VF)
    [PROCEDURE] Defibrillation — Manual
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (1st Dose) — Pulseless VF/VT
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (2nd Dose) — Pulseless VF/VT
    Lidocaine not recommended
  ### Non shockable rhythm
    Epinephrine 1 mcg/kg IV Epinephrine 1 mg IV not recommended
## Other considerations
    Consider ExtraCorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO)
    Consider methemoglobinemia
    [PROCEDURE] Methemoglobinemia — Methemoglobinemia
