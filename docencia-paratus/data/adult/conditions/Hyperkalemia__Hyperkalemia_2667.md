# Hyperkalemia — Hyperkalemia

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Metabolic
- Entorno: adult
- Indication ID: 2667


## Tab: Default
## ECG changes or severe hyperkalemia (> 6.5 mmol/L)
  ### Peripheral venous access
    [DRUG] Calcium (Gluconate) (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
  ### Central venous access or unstable patient
    [DRUG] Calcium (Chloride) (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
## Bradycardia with hemodynamic instability
    [DRUG] Epinephrine (N/A) — Bradycardia
## Intracellular potassium shifting
  ### Insulin and 
dextrose (if glycemia < 14 mmol/L (250 mg/dL))
    [DRUG] Insulin regular (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
    [DRUG] D50% (N/A) — Adjuvant to IV insulin in hyperkalemia
    [DRUG] D10% (N/A) — Adjuvant to IV insulin in hyperkalemia
  ### Salbutamol
    [DRUG] Salbutamol (Nebulization) — Hyperkalemia
## Hypovolemia
  ### Metabolic acidosis
    [DRUG] Sodium bicarbonate (N/A) — Hyperkaliemia
  ### No metabolic acidosis
## Hypervolemia
    [DRUG] Furosemide (N/A) — Bolus (20 to 100mg)
    [DRUG] Furosemide (160mg) — Bolus (120 to 200mg)
    Consider dialysis
