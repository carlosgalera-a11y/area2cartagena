# Liothyronine PO (T3)
- Drug ID: 239
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Decompensated hypothyroidism (myxedema coma)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [Administer PO]
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Increases the risk of arrhythmias. Adjust the dose: lower doses chosen for smaller or older patients and those with a history of coronary artery disease or arrhythmia.

Loading dose of 5 to 20mcg.
Subsequent doses of 2.5 to 10mcg q8h.
