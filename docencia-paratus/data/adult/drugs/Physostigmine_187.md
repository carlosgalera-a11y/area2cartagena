# Physostigmine (Anticholium®)
- Drug ID: 187
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Anticholinergic delirium
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer without dilution
      concentration: 0.4 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Repeat the dose every 20 minutes PRN for a maximum of 2 mg in the first hour.

Do not administer if QRS > 100 msec. Do not administer in mixed intoxications or intoxication with a tricyclic agent.

Discuss with your poison control center. An infusion may be indicated in certain clinical situations.
