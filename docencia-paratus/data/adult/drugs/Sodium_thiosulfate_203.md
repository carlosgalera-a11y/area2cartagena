# Sodium thiosulfate (Seacalphyx®)
- Drug ID: 203
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Cyanide intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Syringe driver  Administer without dilution
      concentration: 250 mg/mL
      administration: 12500 mg = 12.5 g
Administer undiluted over 10 minutes on a syringe driver.

Repeat as needed for an additional ½ dose (6250 mg) if signs of cyanide toxicity reappear.
      particularity: The antidote of choice in the treatment of cyanide poisoning is hydroxocobalamin. Sodium thiosulfate is an adjuvant treatment when there is a partial response to hydroxocobalamin.

Discontinue nitroprusside if in use.

  - STEP: Volumetric pump
      concentration: 250 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Add 12.5 g (12500 mg = 50 mL) to a 100 mL bag of 0.9% NaCl.
2) Final concentration = 83.3 mg/mL.
3) Administer IV over 10 minutes.
4) Repeat as needed for additional ½ dose (6250 mg) if signs of cyanide toxicity reappear.
      particularity: The antidote of choice in the treatment of cyanide poisoning is hydroxocobalamin. Sodium thiosulfate is an adjuvant treatment when there is a partial response to hydroxocobalamin.
