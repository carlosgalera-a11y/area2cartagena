# Tenecteplase (TNKase®)
- Drug ID: 201
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Acute ischemic stroke
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      concentration note: Make sure there are no contraindications
      administration: Incompatible with D5%
Refer to the monograph for detailed reconstitution.

Presentation:
- Vial of Tenecteplase 50mg
- 10mL Sterile water for injection (SWFI)
- 10mL syringe
- Transfer device

1) Take 10mL of sterile water
2) Inject the sterile water into the vial of Tenecteplase
3) Gently swirl until contents are completely dissolved; DO NOT shake
4) Final concentration 5mg/mL
5) Draw the desired dose
6) Administer IV push
      particularity: Dose according to weight:
<60 kg: 15 mg
60 to <70 kg: 17.5 mg
70 to <80 kg: 20 mg
80 to <90 kg: 22.5 mg
>90 kg: 25 mg

*** This list is not exhaustive, see monography ***

Contraindications :
- Symptoms suggestive of SAH
- Active internal bleeding
- BP > 185/115 despite treatment
- CT shows hypodensity > 1/3 cerebral hemisphere
- Current use of anticoagulants
- INR > 1.7, Plt < 100K, aPPT > 40s, PT > 15s
- Heparin within 48 hr with abnormal pTT
- LMWH within 24h
- Postpartum < 14 days
- Intracranial aneurysm, AVM, neoplasm
- Within last 7 days : 
      - Arterial puncture at a non-compressible site
- Within last 21 days : 
      -GI / GU bleed
- Within last 3 months : 
      - Intracranial or intra-spinal surgery
      - Severe head trauma
      - Intracranial hemorrhage 
      - Prior ischemic stroke

- Active internal bleeding
- Within last 3 months : 
      - Intracranial or intra-spinal surgery

      - Prior ischemic stroke
- Intracranial aneurysm, AVM, neoplasm
- Known bleeding diathesis
- Suspected aortic dissection

Relative Contraindications :
- >75 years
- Current use of anticoagulants
- Pregnancy
- Traumatic CPR
- Internal bleed within 4 weeks
- Severe hypertension (> 180/110)
- Dementia
- Major surgery within 3 weeks
- Ischemic stroke > 3 months old
