# Rocuronium (Zemuron®)
- Drug ID: 63
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Neuromuscular blocker
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: -  [IV push]
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Ensure adequate analgo-sedation is in place immediately after intubation to prevent a paralyzed patient not receiving analgesia and sedation.

There is no consensus in the literature on the maximum dose of rocuronium. The maximum dose chosen is an editorial choice: use of total body weight to a maximum of 100kg. Higher doses (>1.4mg/kg) possibly give better intubation conditions. However, the use of ideal versus total body weight remains controversial and higher doses lead to longer periods of paralysis.

Keep refrigerated
