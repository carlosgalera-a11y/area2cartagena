# Methylprednisolone (Solumedrol®)
- Drug ID: 165
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 2-3 min]
      administration: Presentation: 125mg vial
1) Dilute each vial with 2mL of sterile water
2) Final concentration 62.5mg/mL
3) Administer IV over 2min

Presentation: 40mg vial
1) Dilute each vial with 1mL of sterile water
2) Final concentration 40mg/mL
3) Administer IV over 2min
      particularity: There is lack of evidence for the use of steroids in the acute management of anaphylaxis. Their use should not be systematic and could be reserved for certain cases (eg bronchospasm, upper airway obstruction, shock). The main treatment remains epinephrine.

Solumedrol Act-o-vial 40mg is contraindicated in patients with a known or suspected hypersensitivity to cow's milk
      doses:
        • 125mg IV [weight_min=40, weight=0, min=0, max=125]

Anaphylaxis--a practice parameter update 2015. Ann Allergy Asthma Immunol. 2015 Nov;115(5):341-84. doi: 10.1016/j.anai.2015.07.019. PubMed PMID: 26505932.
