# Dantrolene (Dantrium®)
- Drug ID: 117
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Malignant hyperthermia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  [IV push]
      concentration: 0.3333 mg/mL
      administration: 20 mg vials to be reconstituted with 60 mL sterile water.
Concentration after reconstitution = 0.333 mg/mL.
Administer IV push after reconstitution.

This concentration is the only one available on the Canadian market and involves a significant quantity of liquid. Administer IV push as rapidly as possible.
      particularity: The loading dose may be repeated every 5 to 10 minutes until stiffness subsides or for a cumulative dose of up to 10 mg/kg. 
Some patients may require a higher total dose. Discuss with you Poison Control Center.

  - STEP: Maintenance dose  [IV push]
      concentration: 0.3333 mg/mL
      administration: 20 mg vials to be reconstituted with 60 mL sterile water.
Concentration after reconstitution = 0.333 mg/mL.
Administer IV push after reconstitution.

This concentration is the only one available on the Canadian market and involves a significant quantity of liquid. Administer IV push as rapidly as possible.
      particularity: Continue the treatment every 6 hours.

Consult your poison control center for discontinuation of therapy.
