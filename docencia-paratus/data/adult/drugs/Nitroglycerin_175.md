# Nitroglycerin (Nitroject®)
- Drug ID: 175
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Ischemic chest pain
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Use with polyolefin bag. Do not use with PVC bag for the drip.

Presentation: 5mg/mL (10mL vial)
1) Add 50mg (10mL) to 250mL of D5%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 0.19mg/mL
4) Start infusion at 5mcg/min.
5) Titrate by increments of 5-10mcg/min q3-5min according to clinical response and blood pressure (expert opinion)
6) Usual maximum dose 400mcg/min

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability.

Can also be administered more concentrated at 100mg in 250mL of D5%.
      particularity: Use with caution in patients taking phosphodiesterase type 5 inhibitors and in patients with severe aortic stenosis. 
Avoid hypotension.

Strongly consider urgent revascularization in patients with refractory angina.

## Indicación: Hypertensive acute pulmonary edema
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Use with polyolefin bag. Do not use with PVC bag for the drip.

Presentation: 5mg/mL (10mL vial)
1) Add 50mg (10mL) to 250mL of D5%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 0.19mg/mL
4) Start infusion at 20mcg/min or higher (see  particularities)
5) Titrate by increments of 10mcg/min q3-5min (or faster) according to clinical response and blood pressure (expert opinion)

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability (see particularities).

 Can also be administered more concentrated at 100mg in 250mL of D5%.
      particularity: Multiples sources describe the possibility of initiating the infusion at much higher doses (ex. 400mcg/min over 2 min then titrating down). The administration of higher bolus-doses is also probably safe. Consider these practices in the critically ill patient who is severely hypertensive (SCAPE).

Use with caution in patients taking phosphodiesterase type 5 inhibitors and in patients with severe aortic stenosis.
