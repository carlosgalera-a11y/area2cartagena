## Indicación: Bleeding with unfractionated heparin
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: The 50mg dose of protamine is for patients with major bleeding and with ongoing or recent administration of therapeutic dose heparin only if it is impossible to calculate the exact dose of protamine (see below). In case of less severe bleeding or if the administration of the anticoagulant dates more than a few hours, calculate the appropriate dose of protamine (see below) or consider administering a dose of 25mg and repeating the dose only if necessary depending on clinical response (maximum 50mg total).
Overcorrection with protamine can lead to paradoxical bleeding.

Calculation based on dose of heparin:
1. Calculate the amount of heparin to neutralize: all the units administered in the last hour + half the units administered 2 hrs ago + a quarter of the units administered 3 hours ago.
2. Determine the amount of protamine to administer: quantity of heparin to neutralize / 100.
(1mg of protamine neutralizes 100 units of heparin). Maximum 50mg per dose.
3. Presentation: 10mg/mL (5mL vial).
4. Add the calculated dose to 50mL of NaCl 0.9%.
5. Do NOT remove volume.
6. Administer IV over 10min.
      particularity: Rapid administration may cause hypotension or a non-IgE mediated anaphylactic reaction.
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=40, weight=25, min=0, max=0, dilution_volume=50]
        •  [weight_min=40, weight=50, min=0, max=0, dilution_volume=50]
