# Enalaprilat (Vasotec®)
- Drug ID: 232
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 1.25 mg/mL
      administration: Must be administered over at least 5 minutes according to product monograph.
May be diluted in D5 or 0.9% NaCl for administration.
      particularity: Usual interval q6h.

Reduce dose to 0.625mg for patients with renal impairment and CrCl less than 30mL/min.

Response may be exaggerated in the presence of hypovolemia and/or concomitant administration of diuretics. Consider reducing dose or using an alternative agent.

In acute conditions, short acting medications are preferable.
