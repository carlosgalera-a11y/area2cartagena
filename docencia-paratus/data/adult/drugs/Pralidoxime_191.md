# Pralidoxime (Protopam®)
- Drug ID: 191
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Organophosphate intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: In case of fluid restriction or when the situation requires rapid administration of the antidote, administer the 50 mg/mL concentrated solution directly IV at a maximum rate of 200 mg/min.
      particularity: Administer the loading dose as soon as possible after poisoning.
Follow the loading dose by the maintenance infusion.
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=40, weight=0, min=0, max=2000, dilution_volume=100]

  - STEP: Maintenance infusion < 65 kg
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Remove 40 mL from a 250 mL bag of 0.9% NaCl = 210 mL.
2) Add 2000 mg (40 mL) in 210 mL of 0.9% NaCl.
3) Final concentration = 8 mg/mL.
3) Start the infusion at the indicated rate.

Dose = 10 mg/kg/h with a maximum rate of 650 mg/h.
      particularity: Consult your poison control center.

The duration of treatment depends on the symptomatology of the patient.

  - STEP: Maintenance infusion ≥ 65 kg
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Dose = 10 mg/kg/h with a maximum rate of 650 mg/h, reached at a patient weight of 65 kg.

1) Remove 40 mL from a 250 mL bag of 0.9% NaCl = 210 mL.
2) Add 2000 mg (40 mL) in 210 mL of 0.9% NaCl.
3) Final concentration = 8 mg/mL.
3) Start the infusion at the indicated rate.
      particularity: Consult your poison control center.

The duration of treatment depends on the symptomatology of the patient.
