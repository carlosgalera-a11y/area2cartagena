## Indicación: Procedural sedation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 30-60 sec]
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Administration may be irritating. Administer in a proximal vein when possible.

If using the lipid formulation, shake the ampoule before withdrawing the dose.
      particularity: Dose reduction is recommended for elderly patients or patients with renal or hepatic impairment.
Subsequent doses of 0.05mg/kg q3-5 min can be administered to obtain desired sedation depth.

Maximum doses are an editorial choice based on the fact that etomidate should be dosed based on ideal body weight.

Can cause myoclonus.
