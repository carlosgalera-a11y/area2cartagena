# Hydroxocobalamin (Cyanokit® or Hydroxy-Cobal®)
- Drug ID: 144
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Cyanide poisoning
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 1 g
      administration: Presentation:
- Vial: 5 g hydroxocobalamin
- Sterile transfer device
- Cardboard box to hang the vial

1) Remove 50 mL from a 250 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
2) Reconstitute the vial of hydroxocobalamin with 200 mL of NaCl 0.9% using the sterile transfer device provided
3) Swing or flip the flask for at least 60 seconds to mix the solution (DO NOT shake)
4) Final concentration: 25 mg/mL
5) Hang the vial using the provided box
6) Use tubing and filter provided in the box
7) Administer via pump or gravity over 15 minutes

If the supplied filter is not compatible with your institution's pumps, administer by gravity.
      particularity: The vial should not be shaken as this may cause foaming and make it difficult to control reconstitution. Since the reconstituted solution is dark red, some insoluble particles may not be seen.

Interference with certain laboratory analyses using colorimetry (creatinine, bilirubin, etc.) may last 24 to 72h. In addition, blood samples can be falsely interpreted as hemolyzed.
Alteration (lower) of carboxyhemoglobin has also been reported.
