# Labetalol (Trandate®)
- Drug ID: 151
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control (bolus)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Administer IV non diluted at a rate of 10mg/min.
      particularity: Single dose can be doubled to a maximum of 80mg/dose if lack of clinical response.

Although monograph recommends to limit cumulative dose to 300 mg over 24h, it appears safe to exceed this dose in certain clinical situations.

Do not give faster than 10mg/min.
Watch for bradycardia. 
The maximal hypotensive effect occurs 5-15 min after administration.

## Indicación: Targeted BP control (infusion)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 5mg/mL (20mL vial)
1) Add 300mg (60mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 1.9mg/mL
4) Start the infusion at 0.5mg/min
5) Titrate by 0.25-0.5mg/min q30min PRN (expert opinion)
6) Usual maximum dose 2mg/min

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability
      particularity: High risk of accumulation due to a half-life of 5.5 hours. Titrate with caution.
Some sources recommend doses up to 10mg/min.

Although monograph recommends to limit cumulative dose to 300mg over 24h, it appears safe to exceed this dose in certain clinical situations.
