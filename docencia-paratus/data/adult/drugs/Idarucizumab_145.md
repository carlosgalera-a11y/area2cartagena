# Idarucizumab (Praxbind®)
- Drug ID: 145
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Life-threatening bleeding with use of Dabigatran
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Rinse the tubing before the first dose with 0.9% NaCl
2) Administer undiluted via syringe driver or by suspending the vial with tubing
3) Administer 2.5g IV over 5 minutes
4) Immediately administer the second dose of 2.5g IV over 5 minutes
5) Rinse the tubing after the second dose with 0.9% NaCl
Total dose: 5g
      particularity: No other medication can be administered in the same tubing concomitantly.

Risk of serious adverse events in patients with hereditary fructose intolerance attributable to the excipient sorbitol.

The literature also supports the use of Idarucizumab to reverse Dabigatran for surgery and/or emergency procedure.

Keep refrigerated.
