# Dexamethasone IV (Decadron®)
- Drug ID: 120
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: COVID + with hypoxemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: N/A  [IV push]  Administer without dilution
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Administer IV push.
Doses of dexamethasone up to 20mg can be administered IV push without dilution.

Can also be administered PO at the same dose.
      particularity: Recommended dose: 6 mg daily for 10 days (RECOVERY study). Reduced mortality in patients requiring oxygen therapy or invasive mechanical ventilation.

Use with Tocilizumab if possible. If Tocilizumab (or other similar agent) is not available, consider higher doses of dexamethasone (10 to 20mg daily). In patients with very severe hypoxemia, some experts recommend the use of higher doses of dexamethasone (10 to 20 mg die) regardless of other therapies.

In the event of a dexamethasone shortage, consider the use of other corticosteroids. Approximate equivalence: dexamethasone 0.75mg = methylprednisolone 4mg = prednisone 5mg.
