# Phenobarbital (Luminal®)
- Drug ID: 183
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Alcohol withdrawal, high dose
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 3

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 120 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: The concomitant administration of other sedative/hypnotic agents increases the risk of respiratory depression.
The use of a standardized tool for the evaluation of sedation / withdrawal is strongly recommended (e.g.: CIWA or RASS). 

This dose can be repeated ever 30 to 60 minutes PRN. It is generally recommended to limit the total cumulative dose to 20-25 mg/kg. Cumulative doses up to 30 mg/kg and more are reported in the literature.

## Indicación: Status epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 3

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 120 mg/mL
      administration: Administer at 60mg/min as calculated above (rate recommended by monograph).

The "Neurocritical Care Society" recommends administration between 50 and 100mg/min. Consider adjusting according to clinical situation.
      particularity: Phenobarbital is a second line agent in status epilepticus, but should be strongly considered as a first line agent in patients with seizures secondary to alcohol withdrawal.

If necessary, additional doses of 5-10mg/kg IV over 10 minutes can be administered every 30 minutes for a maximum total dose of 40mg/kg.

## Indicación: Alcohol withdrawal, low dose
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 3

  - STEP: 120 mg/mL  Administer without dilution
      concentration: 120 mg/mL
      administration: Administer at a maximum rate of 60 mg/min.

Can also be administered IM at the same dose.
      particularity: Repeat dose every 15 to 30 minutes as needed.
The use of a standardized tool for the evaluation of sedation / withdrawal is strongly recommended (e.g.: CIWA or RASS).

The concomitant administration of other sedative / hypnotic agents increases the risk of respiratory depression.
