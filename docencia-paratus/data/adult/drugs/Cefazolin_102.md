# Cefazolin (Ancef®)
- Drug ID: 102
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to 50 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      administration: Nil
      particularity: *** Off-label ***
With the unstable patient, can be administered IV over 3-5 min in a minimum final volume of 10mL.

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual interval q8h.
Expert advice: 3g IV q8h for patients who weigh more than 120kg.
