# Propylthiouracil PO (Halycil®)
- Drug ID: 241
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Thyroid storm
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: For patients who cannot tolerate the PO route, consider placing and administering via nasogastric tube. Alternatively, intrarectal administration is possible (see references Yeung et al. and Jongjaroenprasert et al.). If this is still not possible, some sources describe how to prepare a solution for intravenous administration (see reference Giles et al.).
      particularity: The loading dose is optional, depending on the clinical situation. Some sources recommend starting with maintenance dosing.
Subsequent doses: 250mg q4h.

May cause hepatotoxicity and liver failure. Prefer methimazole in less severe cases. Reserve propylthiouracil for fulminant cases or pregnant women in the 1st trimester.

Wait at least one hour after propylthiouracil before administering iodine.
