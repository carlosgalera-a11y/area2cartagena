# Lidocaine IV (Xylocaine®)
- Drug ID: 160
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Pulseless VT/VF
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP:   [IV push]
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: Use the syringe of cardiac lidocaïne 2%.
      particularity: A dose of 0.5 to 0.75mg/kg can be repeated q5-10min PRN.
Maximum cumulative dose: 3mg/kg or 300mg for patients over 100kg.
