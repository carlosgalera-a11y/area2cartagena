# Lacosamide (Vimpat®)
- Drug ID: 152
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Status epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Some sources support the administration of doses up to 400mg over 5 minutes.
      particularity: Avoid in patients with symptomatic bradycardia or with 2nd or 3rd degree AV block.

Subsequent doses of 200 mg IV q12h should be considered according to clinical evolution. These doses can also be administered orally given the bioavailability close to 100%.
