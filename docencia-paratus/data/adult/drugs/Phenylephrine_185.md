# Phenylephrine (NeoSynephrine®)
- Drug ID: 185
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Push-dose pressor
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 100 mcg/mL  [IV push]  q 3-5 min PRN
      concentration: 100 mcg/mL
      administration: Caution: multiple concentrations available. The 100mcg/mL concentration should be prepared as follows.

Presentation: 10mg/mL (1mL vial)
1) Add 10mg (1mL) to a bag of 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Final concentration: 0.1mg/mL = 100mcg/mL
3) Draw the required dose using a syringe
4) Clearly identify syringes with the correct concentration
5) Administer 0.1-0.2mg = 100-200mcg (1-2mL) IV push q3-5min PRN to a target blood pressure
      particularity: Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

## Indicación: Shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: *** Not recommended as a first choice in septic shock ***

Presentation: 10mg/mL (1mL vial)
1) Add 10mg (1mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 0.1mg/mL
4) Start infusion at 0.5mcg/kg/min
5) Titrate by increments of 0.1-0.3mcg/kg/min q3-5min until target blood pressure is achieved. (Expert opinion)

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability.
      particularity: Maximal reported dose up to 10mcg/kg/min.

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

## Indicación: Priapism
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [Intracavernous]  q 3-5 min
      concentration: 0.1 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 10mg/mL (1mL vial)
1) Add 1mL (10mg) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 0.1mg/mL
4) Inject 0.1-0.5mg (1-5mL) in the cavernous body of the penis q3-5min PRN
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
        • 0.1-0.5 mg (1-5 mL) [weight_min=40, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
