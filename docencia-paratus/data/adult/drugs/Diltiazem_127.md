# Diltiazem (Cardizem®)
- Drug ID: 127
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: AFib/Flutter/PSVT (bolus)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1st Dose
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: 1) 1st dose 0.25mg/kg (max 20mg).
2) If ineffective after 15 minutes, administer a 2nd dose at 0.35mg/kg (max 25mg).
3) If ineffective after 15 minutes, a 3rd dose at 0.35mg/kg (max 25mg) may be considered, to be individualized according to the patient and the clinical presentation.
4) Consider a diltiazem infusion based on clinical response.
      particularity: A strategy using 10mg boluses instead of higher doses based on weight might be appropriate for some patients (older patients and/or lower blood pressure).

Avoid in patients with hypotension and in case of acute heart failure or known LV dysfunction. 
Use with caution in patients with sepsis or hemodynamic compromise.

Keep refrigerated.

## Indicación: AFib/Flutter/PSVT (drip)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 5mg/mL (10mL vial).
1) Add 125mg (25mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%.
2) Do NOT remove volume.
3) Final concentration: 1mg/mL.
4) Start the infusion at 5mg/h.
5) Titrate infusion by increments of 2.5mg/h every 15 minutes as needed.
6) Do not exceed the maximum dose of 15mg/h.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Continuous infusion for >24h is not recommended.

Avoid in patients with hypotension and in case of acute heart failure or known LV dysfunction. 
Use with caution in patients with sepsis or hemodynamic compromise.

IV drip rate conversion to PO daily dose:
• 5 mg/h -> 180 mg/day
• 7.5 mg/h -> 260 mg/day
• 10 mg/h -> 330 mg/day
• 15 mg/h -> 480 mg/day
