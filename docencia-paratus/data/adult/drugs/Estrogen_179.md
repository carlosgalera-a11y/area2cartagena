# Estrogen (Premarin®)
- Drug ID: 179
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Acute uterine bleeding
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: Presentation: 25mg vial
1) Reconstitute a 25mg vial with 5mL sterile water.
2) The diluent should be added slowly, allowing it to slide over the edges of the vial. Shake gently. Do not stir violently. Use immediately after reconstitution.
3) Final concentration 5mg/mL.
4) Administer IV over 5 minutes without dilution.
      particularity: Contraindications include: patients with breast cancer, venous or arterial thromboembolic disease, liver disease.

Dose can be repeated after 4-6 hours PRN.

Can be administered IM, but IV route is preferred because of faster onset of action.

Keep refrigerated.
