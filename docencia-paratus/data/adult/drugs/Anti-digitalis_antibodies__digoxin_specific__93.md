# Anti-digitalis antibodies (digoxin specific) (DigiFab®)
- Drug ID: 93
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Cardiac arrest imminent
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [Total dose IV over 2-3 min]  Administer wIthout redilution
      administration: Presentation: 40 mg/vial
1) Reconstitute each vial with 4 mL sterile water.
2) Mix gently, roll between hands until completely dissolved to avoid bubble formation. The final solution should be clear and colorless.
3) Final concentration 10 mg/mL.
4) Administer 10 vials IV over 2-3 minutes.
5) Repeat 5-10 vials 3 to 5 minutes later if needed.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Maximum total dose: 20 vials.

## Indicación: Unstable patient, digoxin level unavailable
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to 50 mL NaCl 0.9%
      administration: This dose is applicable to hemodynamically unstable patients

Presentation: 40 mg/vial
1) Reconstitute each vial with 4 mL sterile water.
2) Mix gently, roll between hands until completely dissolved to avoid bubble formation. The final solution should be clear and colorless. 
3) Final concentration 10 mg/mL.
4) Administer the total dose (2-4 vials) non diluted with a syringe driver or add to 50 mL of NaCl 0.9%.
5) Administer IV over 30 minutes.
6) Once the bag is empty, rinse the tubing with a new NaCl 0.9% bag using the infusion pump at the same rate of administration than the Digoxin-specific antibodies.
7) Repeat 2 vials 15-30 minutes later in the absence of clinical response and after discussion with your poison center.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated. Stable 4 hours in the fridge after reconstitution.

Measure digoxin level before administration of the antibodies as the level measured post administration is no longer reliable for at least 24 hours.
