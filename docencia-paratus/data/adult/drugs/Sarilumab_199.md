# Sarilumab (Kevzara®)
- Drug ID: 199
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: COVID + with hypoxemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: N/A
      concentration: 175 mg/mL
      concentration note: Syringes of 200mg in 1.14ml
      administration: *** Off-label ***

1) Add 400mg to a 100mL bag of 0.9% NaCl.
2) To mix, gently invert the bag to avoid foaming.
3) Administer IV over 1 hour.
4) Finish by rinsing with 20mL of 0.9% NaCl.
      particularity: Indications:
In case of a shortage of Tocilizumab
COVID-19 + with hypoxemia under oxygen therapy with CRP >/= 75 mg/L or high-flow oxygen therapy, non-invasive or invasive mechanical ventilation.

Contraindications:
Allergy, septic shock, immunosuppression, liver enzymes more than 5 times upper limit of normal, platelets < 50 x 10e9/L, neutrophils < 0.5 x 10e9/L.

Keep refrigerated.
