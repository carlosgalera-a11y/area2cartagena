# Lorazepam (Ativan®)
- Drug ID: 161
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Status epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Dilute with an equal volume of NaCl 0.9%
2) Administer IV over 2 minutes
      particularity: Administer a 2nd dose after 5 minutes if seizures persist.
Note that this dosage applies only to a patient in status epilepticus.
If seizures persist, switch to a 2nd line agent.

Keep refrigerated.
