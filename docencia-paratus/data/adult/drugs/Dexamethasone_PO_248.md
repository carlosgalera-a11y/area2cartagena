# Dexamethasone PO (Decadron® PO)
- Drug ID: 248
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: Nil
      particularity: There is lack of evidence for the use of steroids in the acute management of anaphylaxis. Their use should not be systematic and could be reserved for certain cases (eg bronchospasm, upper airway obstruction, shock). The main treatment remains epinephrine.
