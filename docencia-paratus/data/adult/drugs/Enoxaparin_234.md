Where possible, round to the nearest pre-prepared syringe dose:
40mg, 50mg, 60mg, 70mg, 80mg, 100mg, 120mg, 150mg.
      particularity: Adjust dose for patients with ClCr <30 mL/minute: 1mg/kg daily. Contraindicated for patients on renal replacement therapy.

The maximum dose of 150mg is an editorial choice, but supported by guidelines, since the population over 150kg is poorly represented in the studies. Although some guidelines and organizations recommend dosing enoxaparin using total weight at 1mg/kg BID with no upper limit, this may increase bleeding risk and potentially be of no benefit. Indeed, some studies show that doses of 0.7-0.8mg/kg BID are probably as effective as doses of 1mg/kg BID for the morbidly obese population. To be considered according to certain patient-specific factors: hemorrhagic risk, clot burden, age, renal function, BMI. Dosing anti-Xa levels should be considered in order to ensure the effectiveness of the treatment. Alternatively, another drug may be used such as unfractionated heparin or a vitamin K antagonist or a DOAC, depending on the clinical situation.

## Indicación: Non-occlusion acute myocardial infarction and atrial fibrillation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1 mg/kg SC BID  [Administer subcutaneously]
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: There are few data to support the use of enoxaparin for patients over 150kg. Choose another molecule or administer a dose of 150mg depending on the risk of bleeding and the clinical situation.

Where possible, round to the nearest pre-prepared syringe dose:
40mg, 50mg, 60mg, 70mg, 80mg, 100mg, 120mg, 150mg.
      particularity: Subsequent doses BID.
Adjust dose for patients with ClCr <30 mL/minute: 1mg/kg daily. Contraindicated for patients on renal replacement therapy.

For patients in whom invasive therapy (coronary angiography) is being considered, the use of unfractionated heparin is preferable.

- Weight type: 1

      particularity: Maximum dose 75mg. Subsequent doses BID.
