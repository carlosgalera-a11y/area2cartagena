# Hydromorphone (Dilaudid®)
- Drug ID: 215
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Post-intubation analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 2 min]
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Use the minimum dose required for adequate analgesia. The use of a standardized scale for pain assessment in the intubated patient is recommended (eg: CPOT).
For intubated patients, doses can be repeated up to q10min PRN depending on the clinical situation (peak action 10-20 minutes IV). Usual interval q1h PRN.

Higher doses may be required in patients with chronic opioid use.
Use smaller doses in elderly patients or patients at risk of respiratory depression.

## Indicación: Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Dilute with 10mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Higher doses may be required in patients with chronic opioid use.
Use smaller doses in elderly patients or patients at risk of respiratory depression.
Close monitoring of vital signs is required.

Initial doses can be administered q10min PRN (peak action 10-20 minutes IV).
