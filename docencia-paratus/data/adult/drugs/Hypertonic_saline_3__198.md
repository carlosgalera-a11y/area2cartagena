## Indicación: Severe hyponatremia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 10 min]
      administration: Administer preferably through a proximal IV access (hyperosmolar solution).
      particularity: Administer in case of severe hyponatraemia with symptoms (seizures, altered level of consciousness, confusion, vomiting, tremors, movement disorders, etc.)

If there is no clinical improvement 5-10 minutes after administration, dose can be repeated up to a total of 300mL. The biochemical objective is to increase serum sodium by 4-6 mmol/L in the first hours of treatment.

Monitor serum sodium closely thereafter, according to usual guidelines and with specialist consultation if necessary. Serum sodium should not increase by more than 6-8 mmol/L per 24 hour period. Risk factors for osmotic demyelination syndrome: serum Na <105 at presentation, chronic/subacute presentation, hypokalemia, malnutrition, advanced liver failure or alcoholism (or history of).

If possible, before administration of 3% saline, measure serum and urine electrolytes/osmolarity.
