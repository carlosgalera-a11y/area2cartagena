# Clevidipine (Cleviprex®)
- Drug ID: 111
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 0.5 mg/mL
      concentration note: No preparation: use the bottle directly
      administration: Presentation: 0.5mg/mL (50 or 100mL vial)
No preparation necessary.

Start the infusion at 1-2 mg/h.
Double the infusion rate every 90 seconds initially.
As BP approaches desired target, increase dose with smaller intervals (1-2mg/h) and every 5-10 minutes.

A majority of patients will require a dose of 16mg/h or less (4-6mg/h on average).
Although some patients may require doses up to 32mg/h, doses of more than 21mg/h over 24 hours or more are not recommended due to the risks associated with the lipid load.
      particularity: Watch for the risks associated with the lipid emulsion (hypertriglyceridemia, metabolic acidosis, rhabdomyolysis, etc.) especially when used with propofol.

Keep refrigerated.
Change tubing and vial every 12 hours.
