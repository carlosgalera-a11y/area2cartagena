# Levothyroxine (T4)
- Drug ID: 157
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Decompensated hypothyroidism (myxedema coma)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  Administer without dilution
      concentration: 40 mcg/mL
      administration: 1) Reconstitute each vial with 5 mL of 0.9% NaCl
• vial of 200 mcg reconstituted: 40 mcg/mL
• vial of 500 mcg reconstituted: 100 mcg/mL
2) Administer the desired dose IV at 100 mcg/min
• 200 mcg over 2 minutes
• 300 mcg over 3 minutes
• 400 mcg over 4 minutes
      particularity: Loading dose: 
• Administer 200 mcg to patients of low body weight, older age or at greater risk of cardiac complications. 
• Administer 400 mcg in younger patients, with higher body weight and without comorbidities.

If the IV form is not available, consider PO administration with a loading dose between 300 mcg and 500 mcg.

Consider the concomitant administration of T3 according to the clinical situation and with specialized advice. Administration of T3 may increase the risk of cardiac complications.

Administer with hydrocortisone in view of the high prevalence of concomitant adrenal insufficiency in these patients.

  - STEP: Maintenance dose  Administer without dilution
      concentration: 40 mcg/mL
      administration: 1) Reconstitute each vial with 5 mL of 0.9% NaCl
• vial of 200 mcg reconstituted: 40 mcg/mL
• vial of 500 mcg reconstituted: 100 mcg/mL
2) Administer the desired dose IV at 100 mcg/min
• 50 mcg over 30 seconds
• 100 mcg over 1 minute
      particularity: Maintenance dose: 
• Administer 50 mcg daily to patients of low body weight, older age or at greater risk of cardiac complications. 
• Administer 100 mcg daily in younger patients, with higher body weight and without comorbidities.

If the IV form is not available, consider PO administration with a maintenance dose between 62.5 mcg and 125 mcg,

Consider the concomitant administration of T3 according to the clinical situation and with specialized advice. Administration of T3 may increase the risk of cardiac complications.

Administer with hydrocortisone in view of the high prevalence of concomitant adrenal insufficiency in these patients.
