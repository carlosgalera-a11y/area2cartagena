# Amoxicillin / clavulanic acid IV (Clavulin®)
- Drug ID: 90
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1000 mg (1 g)  Add to 100 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration note: Dose expressed in mg of amoxicillin
      administration: *** Presentation: vial of amoxicillin 1000 mg / clavulanic acid 200mg or amoxicillin 500 mg / clavulanic acid 100mg = 5:1 ***

Reconstitute according to the manufacturer's information.
The primary dilution (of the vial) is stable 15 minutes after dilution.
The primary dilution may be slightly pink, this coloration should disappear after shaking.
      particularity: Unstable when diluted in dextrose solution. However, may be compatible with dextrose solution as secondary infusion.

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dose: 1000mg IV q8h 

The 2g and 1g doses are not interchangeable since they contain different ratios of amoxicillin and clavulanic acid (10:1 vs 5:1)
