# Activated charcoal (aqueous) (Charac® or Charactol®)
- Drug ID: 109
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Toxicology
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 200 mg/mL
      administration: 1 g/kg, maximum of 50 g per dose.

Shake vigorously before administration.
Administer PO over 15 minutes or via NG tube.

For administration via NG tube, cut the tip of the bottle and insert the end of the probe inside. Gently squeeze the bottle to empty to content into the gastric tube. 
After administration, rinse the content of the tube with 5 to 10 mL of sterile water for injection, then clamp the tube for a period of 1 to 2 hours.

Can be mixed with other liquids to improve the taste:
Chocolate milk, volume ratio 1: 1.
Coca-cola, volume ratio 1: 1.
Cherry syrup, volume ratio 6: 1 (charcoal: syrup).
      particularity: Discuss with your poison control center.

The use of activated charcoal in sorbitol suspension is no longer recommended.

Consider activated charcoal in a potentially toxic ingestion of less than an hour and if there are no contraindications (including airway protection). Discuss with your poison control center before administering via NG tube in an unintubated patient.
