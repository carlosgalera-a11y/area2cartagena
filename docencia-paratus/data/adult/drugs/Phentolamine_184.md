# Phentolamine (Rogitine®)
- Drug ID: 184
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  [IV over 1 minute]
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Take 5 mg of phentolamine.
2) Administer IV bolus over 1 minute.
3) Repeat the loading dose every 5 to 10 minutes PRN for a maximum of 4 doses (20 mg).
4) Maximum effect reached in 2 minutes, duration of action 15 to 30 minutes.
5) Always follow the loading dose with the infusion.
      particularity: Consider the use of this agent for targeted blood pressure control in cases of pheochromocytoma.

For intoxications with a sympathomimetic (e.g.: cocaine), phentolamine is rarely a first line treatment and should only be considered if inadequate response to initial treatments (e.g.: benzodiazepines).

  - STEP: Infusion
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Add 60 mg (12 mL) of phentolamine to a 100 mL bag of 0.9% NaCl.
2) Start the infusion at 1 mg/h.
3) Titrate from 1 to 5 mg/h every 15 minutes PRN for a blood pressure target.
4) Maximum dose of 40 mg/h.

There are no studies on the stability of the chosen dilution, but other dilutions (10 mg in 500 mL) result in too much solute flow at the highest doses. Recommended dilution based on reported cases. Use immediately after preparation and discard after 24 hours.
      particularity: Consider the use of this agent for targeted blood pressure control in cases of pheochromocytoma or intoxication with a sympathomimetic (eg: cocaine).

## Indicación: Extravasation of vasoconstrictor agents
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: Before you begin: with the infiltrated catheter in place, remove as much fluid as possible by pulling back with an empty syringe. Then, remove the catheter.

Presentation: 5 mg/mL (1 mL vial)
1) Add 10 mg (2 mL) to 8mL 0.9% NaCl.
2) Final concentration: 1 mg/mL.
4) Infiltrate the entire affected area with a 25 or 27 gauge needle, 0.5 to 1 mL at a time.
5) Do not exceed the maximum total dose : 10 mg (10 mL).
      particularity: Must be given within 12 hours of extravasation.
