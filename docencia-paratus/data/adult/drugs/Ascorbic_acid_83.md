# Ascorbic acid (Vitamin C)
- Drug ID: 83
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Vitamin C deficiency (scurvy)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 500 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 500 mg/mL or 250 mg/mL.
Dilute 500 mg in 50 mL of 0.9% NaCl.
Administer IV over 30 minutes.
      particularity: Usual interval: daily.
Doses of 300 mg to 1000 mg can be used.
Oral administration should be preferred when possible and clinically appropriate.
Can be administered IM undiluted.
Protect from light.
