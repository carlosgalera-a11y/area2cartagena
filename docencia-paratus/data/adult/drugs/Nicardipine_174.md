# Nicardipine (Cardene®)
- Drug ID: 174
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 0.2 mg/mL
      administration: If pre-prepared bag not available:
Presentation: ampoules of 10 mg in 10 mL.
1) Remove 90 mL from a 250 mL bag of 0.9% NaCl = 160 mL.
2) Add 40 mg of nicardipine (4 vials = 40 mL) to the bag.
3) Final concentration = 40 mg in 200 mL = 0.2 mg/mL. 

Titration information:
1) Start the infusion at 5 mg/h.
2) Titrate by 2.5 mg/h every 5-15 minutes until target BP is achieved (onset of action 5-10 min; rapid titration q5 min or gradual titration q15 min).
3) Maximum dose of 15mg/h.
*** Caution: BP can drop precipitously after the initial titration ***
4) Once target BP is achieved, decrease the infusion to 3mg/h, then titrate again gradually as needed.
5) If BP falls below target, decrease the infusion to minimum rate or hold entirely, then titrate again gradually as needed.
