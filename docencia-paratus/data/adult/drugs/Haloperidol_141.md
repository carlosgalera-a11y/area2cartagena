## Indicación: Severe agitation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IM  [IM in the thigh]
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Withdraw directly from vial without diluting.
Inject desired dose IM in the thigh.
      particularity: For elderly patients in delirium without severe agitation, reduce the dose significantly (0.5-1 mg). Cumulative and higher doses of antipsychotics are associated with risk of falls and mortality.

Depending on the clinical situation, consider the concomitant use of a benzodiazepine. Decrease the dose of haloperidol accordingly because of increased risk of respiratory depression with this association.

  - STEP: IV  [IV over 1 min]  Administer undiluted or diluted up to 5mL of D5%
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Administer undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of D5%.
Administer IV over 1 min.
      particularity: The injectable formulation of haloperidol is not officially approved for IV administration, but is safe according to extensive clinical experience and large studies.

Dose can be repeated every 15 to 30 minutes as needed until the targeted level of sedation is achieved.

Watch for QTc prolongation, especially with doses of more than 20 mg over 24 hours.

For elderly patients in delirium without severe agitation, reduce the dose significantly (0.5-1 mg). Cumulative and higher doses of antipsychotics are associated with risk of falls and mortality.

Depending on the clinical situation, consider the concomitant use of a benzodiazepine. Decrease the dose of haloperidol accordingly because of increased risk of respiratory depression with this association.
