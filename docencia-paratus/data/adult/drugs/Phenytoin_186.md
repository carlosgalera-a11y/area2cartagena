# Phenytoin (Dilantin®)
- Drug ID: 186
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 25mg/min
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Determine the rate of administration according to the clinical situation:
50mg/min for:
- Stable patient < 75 years

25mg/min for:
- Hemodynamic instability
- Patient > 75 years
- Cardiovascular comorbidities

Use a 0.22 micron filter for administration.
Do not administer through a PICC (catheter can block).
Rince tubing with NaCl 0.9% (at same rate) before and after administering medication.
Not compatible with D5%.
      particularity: The 20mg/kg dose calculated with total body weight with a cap at 2000mg (2g) is an editorial choice based on recent literature.
      doses:
