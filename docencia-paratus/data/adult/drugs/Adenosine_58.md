# Adenosine (Adenocard®)
- Drug ID: 58
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Paroxysmal supraventricular tachycardia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1st dose  [IV push]
      concentration: 3 mg/mL
      administration: Use a proximal peripheral venous access.

Two administration options:
1) Administer the dose of adenosine IV push and flush immediately with 10 to 20mL of NaCl 0.9%.
OR
2) Dilute the dose of adenosine with NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 20mL and administer IV push with a single syringe.
      particularity: In a stable patient, consider vagal maneuvers first.

Reduce the dose by 50% if: 
• Administered via central venous access
• Transplanted heart
• Concomitant carbamazepine use
      doses:
