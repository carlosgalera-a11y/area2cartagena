# Hypertonic saline 23.4% (NaCl 23.4%)
- Drug ID: 289
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Intracranial hypertension
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: Although administration via a central venous access is preferable (hyperosmolar solution), administration via peripheral venous access has been shown to be safe.

May be administered over 3-5 minutes in case of severe intracranial hypertension.
      particularity: Measure serum sodium and osmolarity post treatment and avoid Na > 155mmol/L and osmolarity > 320mOsm/kg.
      doses:
        • 30 mL [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
