# Methylene blue (Methylthioninium chloride)
- Drug ID: 97
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: Bolus
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: *** Off-label ***

Can also be administered undiluted via syringe pump, then rinse with 0.9% NaCl.
      particularity: Last-line treatment in refractory anaphylactic shock.

In the presence of a clinical response and refractory anaphylactic shock, consider starting the infusion 1-2 hours after the bolus/test dose.

Pay attention to interactions with serotonergic agents since severe toxicity can occur.

Pulse oximetry may no longer be reliable after administration of methylene blue.

  - STEP: Infusion
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: *** Off-label ***

In the presence of a clinical response to the bolus dose, consider starting the infusion 1-2 hours after the bolus dose.

1) Add 250mg (25mL) to 100mL of D5%.
2) Final concentration = 2mg/mL.
3) Start the infusion at 0.5 mg/kg/h.
4) Increase by 0.25mg/kg/h q1h depending on the clinical response.
5) Maximum dose of 1mg/kg/h.
      particularity: The infusion is optional for anaphylaxis and was only used in a minority of case reports. When used, the treatment time was usually 1-2 hours.

The usual maximum dose of methylene blue is 7 mg/kg of ideal weight in 24 hours.

Pay attention to interactions with serotonergic agents since severe toxicity can occur.

Pulse oximetry may no longer be reliable after administration of methylene blue.

If an infusion is started, administration by central route or intraosseous catheter is preferred (vesicant agent).

## Indicación: Methemoglobinemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer without dilution
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Rinse the tubing before and after each injection with 2.5 to 30mL of 0.9% NaCl to reduce the risk of thrombophlebitis.

Possibility of diluting to facilitate administration and especially to reduce irritation and local pain. Dilute in 50 or 100mL of 0.9% NaCl or D5% and infuse over 10 to 30 minutes.
      particularity: ndications:
Methemoglobinemia greater than 0.20 (20%).
Methemoglobinemia less than 0.20 (20%), but symptomatic patient (angina, confusion, agitation, anemia).

Maximum of 100mg for each dose.

The dose can be repeated 30 to 60 minutes later depending on the clinical response.

Maximum dose: 7 mg/kg of ideal body weight per 24 hours.

Pay attention to interactions with serotonergic agents since severe toxicity can occur.

Pulse oximetry may no longer be reliable after administration of methylene blue.
