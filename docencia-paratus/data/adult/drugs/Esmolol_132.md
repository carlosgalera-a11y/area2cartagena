# Esmolol (Brevibloc®)
- Drug ID: 132
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted control for BP and/or HR
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Step 1: bolus
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: IV push over 1min
      particularity: Short half life (9min)
Possibility of repeating the bolus: 500mcg/kg IV q5min x 3 doses total
Max dose for the bolus: 60,000mcg (expert opinion)

Avoid in patients with hypotension, and use with caution in patients with sepsis, hemodynamic compromise, or cardiomyopathy.
