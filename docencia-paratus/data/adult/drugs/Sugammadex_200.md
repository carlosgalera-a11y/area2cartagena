# Sugammadex (Bridion®)
- Drug ID: 200
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Urgent reversal of rocuronium
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer without dilution
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: For reversal of rocuronium and vecuronium only. Does not reverse the effect of depolarizing agents (succinylcholine).

The 16 mg/kg dose is associated with an increased frequency of anaphylactic and non-IgE mediated anaphylactic reactions. Monitor closely.

A partial reversal can be performed with a dose of 4 mg/kg; usually used for reversal of paralysis at the end of an operative procedure.
