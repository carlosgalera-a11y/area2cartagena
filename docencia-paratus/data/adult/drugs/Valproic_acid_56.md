# Valproic acid (Epiject®)
- Drug ID: 56
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Status epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading Dose
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      concentration note: Vials of 500mg (5mL)
      administration: Add calculated dose to a bag of 50mL NaCl 0.9% without removing volume.
Administer IV over 10 minutes.
      particularity: Maximum speed of administration of 6mg/kg/min.

A second dose of 20mg/kg can be administered after the initial loading dose if needed.

Potentially ineffective in patients receiving carbapenems; concurrent use not recommended.
