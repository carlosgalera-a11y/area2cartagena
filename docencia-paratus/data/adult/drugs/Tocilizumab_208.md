# Tocilizumab (Actemra®)
- Drug ID: 208
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: COVID + with hypoxemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: N/A
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: Calculate the dose by body weight:
91kg and more: 800 mg
66 to 90kg: 600 mg
40 to 65kg: 400 mg
Less than 40kg: 8 mg/kg

1) Choose the vials corresponding to the desired dose and according to availability.
2) Add the total dose to a 100mL bag of 0.9% NaCl.
3) To mix the solution, gently invert the bag to avoid foaming.
4) Administer IV over 1 hour.
5) Finish by rinsing with 20mL of 0.9% NaCl.
      particularity: Indications:
COVID-19 + with hypoxemia under oxygen therapy with CRP >/= 75 mg/L or high-flow oxygen therapy, non-invasive or invasive mechanical ventilation.

Contraindications:
Allergy, septic shock, immunosuppression, liver enzymes more than 5 times upper limit of normal, platelets < 50 x 10e9/L, neutrophils < 0.5 x 10e9/L.

Keep refrigerated.

In case of a tocilizumab shortage, consider using sarilumab.
