# Diphenhydramine (Benadryl®)
- Drug ID: 128
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Pruritus secondary to anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 10 mL
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Dilute with NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 10mL.
2) Administer IV over 2 minutes.
      particularity: Subsequent doses q4-6h PRN.
      doses:
        • 50 mg (1 mL) [weight_min=40, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
