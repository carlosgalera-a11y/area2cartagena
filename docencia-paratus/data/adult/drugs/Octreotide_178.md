# Octreotide (Sandostatin®)
- Drug ID: 178
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Esophageal variceal bleed
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Step 1: bolus
      concentration: 500 mcg/mL
      administration: *** High concentration ***
Presentation: 500mcg/mL

1) Add 500mcg (1mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 5mcg/mL
4) If smart pump available, administer the 50mcg bolus over 10 minutes from the infusion. Alternatively, withdraw 50mcg (10mL) from the prepared bag and administer IV over 3 minutes.
5) Start the infusion at 50mcg/h
      particularity: Keep refrigerated
      doses:
        • 50 mcg [weight_min=40, weight=0, min=0, max=0]

## Indicación: Sulfonylurea intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Subcutaneous
      concentration: 50 mcg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Repeat the dose q6h as needed.
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=40, weight=50, min=0, max=0]

  - STEP: IV
      concentration: 50 mcg/mL
      administration: Favor the subcutaneous route.
Consider administering IV if severe intoxication or peripheral hypoperfusion.

Can also be administered IV without dilution over 3 minutes. However, this can cause more cardiovascular side effects.
      particularity: Repeat the dose q4h if necessary (expert opinion: shorten interval to q4h when the IV route is used).
