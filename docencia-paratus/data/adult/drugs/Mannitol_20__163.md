# Mannitol 20% (Osmitrol®)
- Drug ID: 163
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Intracranial hypertension
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 0.2 g/mL
      administration: Use the prepared bag of 100g/500mL (20%).
Administer on volume/time pump over 20 minutes.
Use a 0.22 micron filter for administration.
      particularity: Monitor for hypotension.
Installing a foley catheter is recommended in order to follow induced diuresis.
NaCl 3% is a preferred first line treatment for intracranial hypertension.
Contraindicated in severe renal failure.
