# Diazepam (Valium®)
- Drug ID: 125
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Severe alcohol withdrawal
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer without dilution
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Administer at 5mg/min IV bolus.
Administer the 40mg dose by syringe-driver.
      particularity: It is recommended to use a standardized assessment scale for alcohol withdrawal (CIWA or RASS). Use the smallest effective dose and increase as needed and based on clinical response. Very high doses can sometimes be necessary

Usual interval q20-60 min PRN. Depending on the clinical situation, the initial doses can be titrated rapidly upwards and given more closely (q5-10 min) under continuous monitoring.

Rapid sedation after the first doses of benzodiazepines should lead to questioning of the diagnosis of alcohol withdrawal.
