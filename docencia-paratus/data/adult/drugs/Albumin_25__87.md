# Albumin 25% ([])
- Drug ID: 87
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Spontaneous bacterial peritonitis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Day 1
      concentration: 1 g
      administration: The dose is rounded to the nearest full vial to facilitate administration.

Administer with ventilated tubing.
Administer according to local protocols. Usually, one vial of 25g (100mL) over 1 hour. The large amounts of volume can lead to circulatory overload.
      particularity: Dose of albumin in SBP:
1.5g/kg IV day 1, maximum of 150g.
1g/kg IV day 3, maximum of 100g.

Administration of albumin reduces mortality and the development of hepatorenal syndrome, especially in patients with acute renal failure and hyperbilirubinemia.

## Indicación: Paracentesis; acording to quantity drained in Liters
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 5 to 8 Liters
      concentration note: 100 mL vials (25 g)
      administration: The dose is rounded to the nearest full vial to facilitate administration.

Administer with ventilated tubing.
Administer according to local protocols. Usually, one vial of 25g (100mL) over 1 hour. The large amounts of volume can lead to circulatory overload.
      particularity: It is recommended to administer 8g of albumin for every liter of ascites drained, if drainage of more than 5L. In patients at high risk of hemodynamic or renal decompensation, a replacement can be administered for drainage of less than 5L with the same ratio of 8g for every liter drained.
