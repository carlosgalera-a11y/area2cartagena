# Dopamine (Intropin®)
- Drug ID: 65
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Shock/unstable bradycardia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 1.6 mg/mL
      administration: Dopamine is not a first line agent in septic shock; see "De Backer, D. et al (2010)"

Pre-prepared commercial bag:
1) Start infusion at the minimal dose of 2mcg/kg/min.
2) Titrate by increments of 1-2mcg/kg/min q10-15min as needed (t1/2: 2-20min).
      particularity: Dopamine is not a first line agent in septic shock; see "De Backer, D. et al (2010)"

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.
