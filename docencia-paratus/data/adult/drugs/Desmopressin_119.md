# Desmopressin (DDAVP®)
- Drug ID: 119
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Bleeding associated with Hemophilia A or von Willebrand disease
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 4 mcg/mL
      administration: Dilute in 50mL of NaCl 0.9%.
Administer IV over 20 minutes.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
May cause hyponatremia.

Indicated to treat minor bleeding only.
Doses may be repeated thereafter (8 to 12h later, then daily), in which case free water intake should be limited and serum sodium monitored.

Some studies support the use of DDAVP in intracranial bleeding in patients on antiplatelet agents (0.4mcg/kg).
