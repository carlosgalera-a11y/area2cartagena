# Potassium iodide and iodine 5% PO (Lugol)
- Drug ID: 240
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Thyroid storm
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: Caution: multiple concentrations available.
Proposed dose is for the 5% solution.

Administer at least one hour after the antithyroid medication (propylthiouracil, methimazole).

May cause gastric irritation. If the situation allows, administer with food or liquids, ideally a sweet juice (apple juice) given the sour taste.
      particularity: Subsequent doses q6-8h.
      doses:
        • 5 to 10 drops (0.25 to 0.5mL) PO [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
