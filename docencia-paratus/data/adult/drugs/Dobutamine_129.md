# Dobutamine (Dobutrex®)
- Drug ID: 129
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Cardiogenic shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 12.5 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 12.5mg/mL (20mL vial)
1) Add 250mg (20mL) or 500mg (40mL) to 250ml of NaCl 0.9%.
2) Do NOT remove volume.
3) Final concentration: 0.9mg/mL or 1.7mg/mL.
4) Start infusion at the minimal dose of 2mcg/kg/min.
5) Titrate by increments of 1-2mcg/kg/min every 5 to 10 minutes as needed (t1/2: 2-3min) (expert opinion).

N.B. Dobutamine is an inotrope. Therefore, its efficacy cannot be evaluated with blood pressure changes, but rather by measuring cardiac output or by evaluating indirect signs of cardiac output.
      particularity: No literature guides the type of weight to use in obesity. Ideal body weight dosing was an editorial decision by our experts in obesity pharmacology.

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.
