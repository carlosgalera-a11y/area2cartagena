# Phosphate ({"fr":null})
- Drug ID: 226
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Hypophosphatemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Low serum potassium (<3.5mmol/L)
      concentration: 3 mmol/mL
      concentration note: POTASSIUM or SODIUM phosphate
      administration: If the POTASSIUM level is low (<3.5mmol/L), use POTASSIUM phosphate preferably, or SODIUM phosphate if not available.

This administration method is for peripheral venous access. For central venous access, a dose of 30mmol can be administered in 100mL of NaCl 0.9%.

Also compatible with D5%.
      particularity: Use IV phosphate in patients with: 
• Severe hypophosphatemia (< 0.32mmol/L (1mg/dL))
• Moderate hypophosphatemia (0.32 to 0.64mmol/L (1 to 2mg/dL)) AND symptoms (muscle weakness, respiratory failure, neurological symptoms, etc.). 
Otherwise, treat with PO phosphate.

Adjust dose in renal insufficiency (ClCr < 30mL/min). Consult a specialist if necessary.

If the phosphate level is below 0.32mmol/L (1mg/dL), consider completing repletion with a second dose of 15mmol in 250mL of 0.9% NaCl over 3 hours.

POTASSIUM phosphate contains 4.4mmol of potassium for every 3mmol of phosphate.

Absolute values ​​are approximate. Rely on values ​​from your local laboratory or local protocols.
