# Hydralazine (Apresoline®)
- Drug ID: 142
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Dilute with NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 10 mL
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: Administer at 5mg/min (10mg dose over 2 minutes; 20mg dose over 4 minutes).
Can be diluted in 50mL of NaCl 0.9% and administered over 15 to 30 minutes.
      particularity: Hydralazine is not a first choice agent in critical situations due to its unpredictable and sometimes prolonged effect on blood pressure.

Maximum effect 10-80 minutes after administration.

Usual dose: 10 to 20mg IV q6h PRN for blood pressure target.
