# Pantoprazole (Pantoloc®)
- Drug ID: 181
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Upper GI bleeding (bolus - infusion)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: powder (40mg/vial)
1) Reconstitute with 10mL NaCl 0.9%
2) Final concentration: 4mg/mL
3) Add 80mg (20mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
4) Do NOT remove volume
5) Administer IV over 15min

The 80mg bolus can also be administered undiluted IV (after reconstitution) over 2-3 minutes.

  - STEP: Infusion
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: powder (40mg/vial)
1) Reconstitute with 10mL NaCl 0.9% (4mg/mL)
2) Remove 10mL from a bag of 100mL NaCl 0.9%
3) Add 40mg (10mL) to the remaining 90mL of NaCl 0.9%
4) Final concentration: 0.4mg/mL
5) Start the infusion at 8mg/h
      particularity: Alternative to bolus and infusion: intermittent boluses (ex: bolus of 80 mg followed by 40 mg IV q12h); JAMA Intern Med. 2014;174(11):1755-1762

## Indicación: Upper GI bleeding (bolus strategy)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: powder (40mg/vial)
1) Reconstitute with 10mL NaCl 0.9%
2) Final concentration : 4mg/mL
3) Add 80mg (20mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
4) Do NOT remove volume
5) Administer IV over 15min

The 80 mg bolus can also be administered undiluted IV (after reconstitution) over 2-3 minutes.
      particularity: dminister maintenance dose 12h after loading dose.

  - STEP: Maintenance dose
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: powder (40mg/vial)
1) Reconstitute with 10mL NaCl 0.9%
2) Final concentration: 4mg/mL
3) Add 40mg (10mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
4) Do NOT remove volume
5) Administer IV over 15min

The 40mg doses can also be administered undiluted IV (after reconstitution) over 2-3 minutes or on a syringe driver over 10 minutes.
      particularity: Start 12h after the loading dose and administer q12h thereafter.
According to some sources, the maintenance dose can also be administered q6h.
According to the literature, intermittent bolus pantoprazole is comparable to the infusion in terms of effectiveness.
