# Levocarnitine (Carnitor®)
- Drug ID: 155
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Valproic acid intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 200 mg/mL
      administration: More rapid administration can lead to serious side effects (visual changes, headache, seizures). It is therefore recommended to give the dose in 30 minutes.

The loading dose can be given diluted, but the concentration cannot exceed 8 mg/mL and the 6000 mg dose should therefore be diluted in 1 L of solution and administered over 30 minutes, which is not possible with most volumetric pumps. For this reason, the loading dose should be given via syringe driver without dilution.

If a syringe driver is not available, empty a 25 or 50 mL bag, introduce the dose into the empty bag and administer over 30 minutes.
      particularity: To be considered as an adjuvant in acute valproic acid poisoning in a symptomatic patient (encephalopathy or presence of hepatoxicity), not undergoing hemodialysis, with hyperammonemia.

Maximum dose of 6 g.

Follow the loading dose with maintenance doses; start 4 hours after the loading dose.

  - STEP: Maintenance dose
      concentration: 200 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Give the first maintenance dose 4 hours after the loading dose and repeat the maintenance dose every 4 hours.

Maximum dose of 12 g per 24 hours.

Discuss with your poison control center for the next treatment doses in order to respect the maximum dose over 24 hours.
