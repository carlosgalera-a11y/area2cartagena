# Fomepizole (Antizol®)
- Drug ID: 135
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Toxic alcohol intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 1000 mg/mL
      administration: If available, enter patient height.
For obese patients, it is best to determine the dose using ideal body weight, up to a maximum of 1500 mg/dose. 

Loading dose of 15 mg/kg.
Add to 100 mL of 0.9% NaCl.
Administer IV over 30 minutes.
      particularity: Discuss with your local toxicology center to establish the need for maintenance doses.

  - STEP: Maintenance dose
      concentration: 1000 mg/mL
      administration: If available, enter patient height.
 For obese patients, it is best to determine the dose using ideal weight, up to a maximum of 1500 mg/dose. 

Maintenance dose of 10 mg/kg.
Add to 100 mL of 0.9% NaCl.
Administer IV over 30 minutes.
      particularity: Discuss with your local toxicology center to establish the need for maintenance doses.
Administer the first maintenance dose 12 hours after the loading dose, then every 12 hours as needed for a maximum of 4 maintenance doses.
If treatment is continued beyond 48 hours, the dose must be increased because fomepizole self-induces its metabolism.
Administer the maintenance dose every 4 hours during hemodialysis.
