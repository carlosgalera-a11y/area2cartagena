# Procainamide (Pronestyl®)
- Drug ID: 192
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Ventricular tachycardia with pulse
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 100mg/mL (10mL vial)
1) Add the calculated dose to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Administer IV over 20 minutes

Stop the infusion if the patient converts to sinus rhythm.
      particularity: Continuously monitor BP for hypotension and ECG for prolongation of QT interval and heart block. 

Avoid in patients initially treated with other anti-arrhythmics to avoid excessive QT interval prolongation and torsade de pointes.

## Indicación: Atrial Fibrillation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 100mg/mL (10mL vial)
1) Add the calculated dose to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Administer IV over 30min

Stop the infusion if the patient converts to sinus rhythm.
      particularity: Avoid if systolic BP < 100 mmHg or QTc > 500 msec.
Stop infusion if BP drops or QRS lengthens.
Verifiy QTc after conversion.

Avoid in patients initially treated with other anti-arrhythmics to avoid excessive QT interval prolongation and torsade de pointes.
