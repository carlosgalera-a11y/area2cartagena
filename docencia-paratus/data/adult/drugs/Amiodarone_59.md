- Type: drug

## Indicación: VT with pulse or AFib (bolus)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 150 mg
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 50mg/mL
1) Add the desired dose to 100mL of D5%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Administer IV over 10 minutes
Can be administered over 30 minutes for less urgent situations.

Must be administered with a 0.22 micron IV tubing filter. This can be omitted for the bolus in emergency situations.
      particularity: May be administered more quickly depending on the clinical situation (maximum speed: 30mg/min).

Additional doses of 150mg can be administered every 10 minutes as needed for a maximum of 2200mg (including the infusion) for the first 24 hours.

The rate of cardioversion of AF/flutter with amiodarone is rather low and this agent can be used for rate control in hemodynamically unstable or precarious patients.

## Indicación: VT with pulse or AFib (drip)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion: step 1  Administer at 1 mg/min for 6 h
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 50mg/mL (3mL vial)
1) Add 450mg (9mL) to 250mL of D5%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 1.7mg/mL
4) Administer at 1mg/min for the first 6 hours
5) Decrease to 0.5mg/min for the next 18 hours
6) Continue drip at 0.5mg/min or stop drip depending on clinical situation
      particularity: Use with polyolefin bag. Do not use with PVC bag for the drip.
Must be administered with a 0.22 micron IV tubing filter.

Must be administered via central access when concentration > 2 mg/mL.

The rate of cardioversion of AF/flutter with amiodarone is rather low and this agent can be used for rate control in hemodynamically unstable or precarious patients.
