# Trimethoprim / sulfamethoxazole IV (Septra® or Bactrim®)
- Drug ID: 207
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Pneumocystis pneumonia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 6

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 16 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 16mg/mL trimethoprim and 80mg/mL sulfamethoxazole.

Dose 5mg/kg expressed in trimethroprim.

Doses of less than 400mg are administered in 250mL of D5% over 60 minutes.
Doses of 400mg and more are administered in 500mL of D5% over 90 minutes.

In case of fluid restriction, dilute every 1mL (16mg TMP/ 80mg SMX) in 10mL of solute.
      particularity: The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual interval q6-8h.

Expert advice: Adjusted body weight is used for all weights (when inferior to total body weight) to ensure a logical progression in the doses with increasing patient weights. Some sources recommend using adjusted body weight only in patients with obesity (total body weight > 120% of ideal body weight or BMI > 30).
