# Digoxin (Lanoxin®)
- Drug ID: 126
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Atrial fibrillation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 10 mL
      concentration: 0.25 mg/mL
      administration: Choose an initial bolus of 0.25mg or 0.5mg.
Dilute with NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 10mL.
Administer IV over 5 minutes.
      particularity: After the initial bolus, repeat 0.25mg IV q6h for a maximum of 1.5mg total for the first 24h. Then, initiate the maintenance PO dose as clinically indicated.

In patients with older age, smaller weight and/or reduced CrCl, use smaller doses and consider a maximum dose of 0.75mg total for the first 24h.

Some sources recommend an IV loading dose of 8-12mcg/kg ideal body weight (6-10mcg/kg ideal body weight in renal insufficiency) given 50% as a loading dose, than 25% given twice every 6 hours.

Less effective in case of high sympathetic activity.
Contraindicated in case of pre-excitation.
