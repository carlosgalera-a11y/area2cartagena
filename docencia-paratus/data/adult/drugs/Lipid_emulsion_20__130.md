# Lipid emulsion 20% (Intralipid®)
- Drug ID: 130
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Local anesthetic systemic toxicity
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 3

  - STEP: 
      administration: The administration of a lipid emulsion infusion is not supported by evidence. The loading dose can be repeated every 5 minutes (max 3 doses total) if absence of clinical response.

Consult your local poison center if no response to boluses.

May be infused via peripheral or central lines. DEHP-free tubing is not required and no in-line filter is required. 

Can be used in asystole or refractory shock secondary to intoxication with a sodium channel blocker or a liposoluble drug. Consider its use only if all the usual therapies have failed.
      particularity: Contraindicated by monograph if allergy to eggs, soy or peanuts.
