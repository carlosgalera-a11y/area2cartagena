# Potassium chloride (KCl)
- Drug ID: 281
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Hypokalemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 10 mmol in 100 mL
      concentration: 0.1 mmol/mL
      administration: Pre-prepared bag of 10 mmol in 100 mL.

Via peripheral venous access, administer IV with a maximum rate of 20 mmol over 1 hour.
Via central venous access, administer IV with a maximum rate of 40 mmol over 1 hour. 

In case of pain at the infusion site, reduce infusion rate or administer in "Y" with another infusion.
      particularity: PO potassium is usually well absorbed. Reserve IV potassium for refractory or severe cases or if PO administration is impossible.

In severe hypokalemia, potassium deficit can be in the order of 100-200 mmol/L or more.
Consider concomitant PO potassium administration as higher doses can be given by the PO route. 
Consider serum magnesium measurement and/or magnesium repletion.

Avoid dextrose and insulin administration if possible.
Follow potassium levels closely in patients with kidney injury.
