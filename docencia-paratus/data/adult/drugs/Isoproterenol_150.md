# Isoproterenol (Isuprel®)
- Drug ID: 150
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Bradyarrhythmia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 0.2 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Add 1mg (5mL) or 2mg (10mL) or 4mg (20mL) to 250mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 4mcg/mL or 8mcg/mL or 16mcg/mL
4) Start infusion at 0.5-1mcg/min
5) Titrate by 0.5-1mcg/min q5min to desired heart rate (expert opinion)
6) Usual maximum dose 10mcg/min

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability
      particularity: Doses as high as 30mcg/min have been reported in the literature.
Monitor for hypotension.
Consider the installation of an endovenous pacemaker if needed.
Protect ampoules from light.
Also compatible with D5W for dilution.

## Indicación: Medical chronotropy in torsade de pointes
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 0.2 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Add 1mg (5mL) or 2mg (10mL) or 4mg (20mL) to 250mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 4mcg/mL or 8cmg/mL or 16mcg/mL
4) Start infusion at 2mcg/min
5) Titrate by 0.5-1mcg/min q5min to desired heart rate (expert opinion)
6) Usual maximum dose 10mcg/min
7) Aim for heart rate 100-110 bpm

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability
      particularity: Doses as high as 30mcg/min have been reported in the literature.
Monitor for hypotension.
Consider electrical pacing as needed.
Protect ampoules from light.
Also compatible with D5W for dilution.
