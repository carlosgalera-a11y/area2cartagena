# Prothrombin complex concentrate (4-factor) (Beriplex® or Kcentra®)
- Drug ID: 116
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: DOAC (Xa inhibitor) reversal in patients with acute major bleeding
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 25 unités/mL
      administration: Presentation:
- Vial of prothrombin complex
- Vial of diluant (sterile water) 
- Mix2Vial® transfer device

Refer to the monograph for detailed reconstitution.
1) Mix the vial with the diluent using the device provided. DO NOT shake the bottle.
2) Gently swirl the bottle to make sure the product is completely dissolved
3) Draw the required dose with syringe
      particularity: May be administered more quickly depending on the clinical situation (maximum speed: 3 units/kg/min)

Contraindications:
- Prior heparin-induced thrombocytopenia (HIT)
- Heparin allergy

## Indicación: Vitamin K antagonist (VKA) reversal in patients with acute major bleeding
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Fixed dose  [IV over 15 min]
      concentration: 25 unités
      administration: Must be administered with Vitamin K (10mg IV) in case of major bleeding.

Presentation:
- Vial of prothrombin complex
- Vial of diluant (sterile water) 
- Mix2Vial® transfer device

Refer to the monograph for detailed reconstitution.
1) Mix the vial with the diluent using the device provided. DO NOT shake the bottle.
2) Gently swirl the bottle to make sure the product is completely dissolved.
3) Draw the required dose with syringe.
      particularity: May be administered more quickly depending on the clinical situation (maximum speed: 3 units/kg/min)

The dose of 1500 units is an editorial choice. Some societies (ex: Thrombosis Canada) recommend a dose of 2000 units. According to some literature, non-intracranial bleeding could be treated with a dose of 1000 units. 

Contraindications:
- Prior heparin-induced thrombocytopenia (HIT)
- Heparin allergy
