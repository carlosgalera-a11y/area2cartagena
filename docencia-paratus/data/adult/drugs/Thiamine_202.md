## Indicación: Treatment for Wernicke's Encephalopathy
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Usual dosing:
500 mg IV every 8 hours for 48 hours, then 250 mg IV daily for 5 days.

The 500 mg IV every 8 hours dose can be continued for a longer period depending on patient symptoms.

Expert opinion: slower administration increases absorption. Can be administered over 2 hours.

Preferably administer thiamine before carbohydrates.
