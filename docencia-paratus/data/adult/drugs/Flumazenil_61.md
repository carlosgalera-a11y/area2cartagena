# Flumazenil (Anexate® or Romazicon®)
- Drug ID: 61
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Iatrogenic benzodiazepine intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  [IV over 30 seconds]
      concentration: 0.1 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Administer 0.2 mg IV over 30 seconds.
2) If no clinical response after 60 seconds, administer 0.3 mg IV over 30 seconds.
3) If no clinical response after 60 seconds, administer 0.5 mg IV over 30 seconds.
4) Repeat the dose of 0.5 mg IV every 60 seconds as needed.
5) Maximum cumulative dose of 5 mg. Consider other etiologies for the clinical presentation if no clinical response after a cumulative dose of 3 mg.
6) Start the infusion if necessary.
      particularity: Contraindicated:
- Coma of unknown origin
- Co-ingestion of another toxic substance
- Epilepsy
- Benzodiazepine dependence
- Sodium channel blockade on EKG
- Intubated patient

  - STEP: Drip
      concentration: 0.1 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 0.1 mg/mL (5 mL vial)
1) Remove 20 mL from a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%.
2) Add 2 mg (20 mL) to the remaining 80 mL of NaCl 0.9%.
3) Final concentration : 0.02 mg/mL.
4) Start the infusion at 0.2 mg/h and titrate according to clinical response.
      particularity: Contraindicated:
