# Ceftriaxone (Rocephin®)
- Drug ID: 107
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to 50 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      administration: Nil
      particularity: *** Off-label ***
With the unstable patient, can be administered IV over 3-5 min in a minimum final volume of 20mL.

No adjustment needed in renal failure.

Usual interval: q24h
Meningitis: q12h

Incompatible with calcium containing solutions (ex: Lactated Ringer's)
