# Acyclovir (Zovirax®)
- Drug ID: 86
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Encephalitis, HSV or VZV infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 6

  - STEP:   [IV over 1h]  Add to 250mL of NaCl 0.9%
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Doses up to 15mg/kg have been reported in herpes zoster encephalitis.

Maintain adequate IV hydration during administration.

Fixed dose. Adjust the dosing interval according to renal fonction.
Usual interval q8h.

Expert advice: Adjusted body weight is used for all weights (when inferior to total body weight) to ensure a logical progression in the doses with increasing patient weights. Some sources recommend using adjusted body weight only in patients with obesity (total body weight > 120% of ideal body weight or BMI > 30).
