# Deferoxamin (Desferal®)
- Drug ID: 118
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Iron intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Remove 20mL from a 100mL bag of 0.9% NaCl.
2) Add 2000mg of deferoxamine (4 vials of 500mg or 1 vial of 2000mg).
3) Start the infusion at 15mg/kg/h or a maximum of 1000mg/h as the initial dose (50mL/h).
4) Higher doses may be needed depending on the clinical situation and if tolerated by the patient. Discuss with your local toxicology center before increasing the infusion and to determine the total duration and discontinuation of treatment (administration of deferoxamin causes an underestimation of circulating iron levels; dose iron levels only after stopping the treatment).
5) Usual maximum dose of 6g per 24 hours.
      particularity: Indications:
- Ingestion of 40mg/kg or more of elemental iron with symptoms of toxicity.
- Serum concentration greater than 90 mcmol/L (500mcg/dL) in acute intoxication.
- Serum concentration greater than 63 mcmol/L (350mcg/dL) and cardiovascular symptoms or lactic acidosis.
