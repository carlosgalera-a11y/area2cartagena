# Nitroprusside (Nipride®)
- Drug ID: 176
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 25 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 25mg/mL (2mL vial)
1) Add 50mg (2mL) to 250mL of D5%
2) Do NOT remove volume 
3) Final concentration: 0.2mg/mL
4) Start infusion at 0.5mcg/kg/min
5) Titrate by increments of 0.25-0.5mcg/kg/min q5min to target blood pressure (expert opinion)
6) Maximum usual dose 2mcg/kg/min

N.B. Increase starting dose and titrate faster depending on degree of instability.
      particularity: The risk of cyanide toxicity increases with doses greater than 2mcg/kg/min if used over many hours. Doses up to 10mcg/kg/min are described in the literature, but for short durations only (maximum 10 minutes).

Avoid in the presence of intracranial hypertension. Instead, use other agents (e.g. nicardipine, labetalol, clevidipine).

Protect solution from light at all times.
