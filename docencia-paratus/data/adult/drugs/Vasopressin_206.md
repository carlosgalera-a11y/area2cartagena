# Vasopressin (Pressyn®)
- Drug ID: 206
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Fixed dose in septic shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 20 unités/mL
      administration: Presentation: 20units/mL (1mL vial)
1) Add 40units (2mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 0.4units/mL
2) Start at 0.03units/min and keep fixed dose (see particularities)
      particularity: Doses ranging from 0.01-0.06 units/min have been reported in the literature.
The Surviving Sepsis Campaign suggests a fixed dose at 0.03units/min (1.8units/hr).

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

## Indicación: Titratable dose in shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 20 unités/mL
      administration: Presentation: 20units/mL (1mL vial)
1) Add 40units (2mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 0.4units/mL
4) Start infusion at 0.01units/min
5) Titrate by increments of 0.01units/min q15min as needed (t1/2 10min) (Expert opinion)
      particularity: There is a possible risk of tissue necrosis, coronary and mesenteric ischemia, especially at doses higher than 0.04units/min; use with caution.
There may be rebound hypotension with weaning and withdrawal. Consider slowly tapering by 0.01units/min every 30-60 minutes.

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.
