# Propranolol (Inderal®)
- Drug ID: 194
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Thyroid storm
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Dilute with 10 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: Can be administered without dilution.
Administer a first dose of 0.5 to 1mg IV.
A dose of 1 to 2mg can be repeated 10 minutes later depending on clinical response.
Make the transition to PO therapy as soon as possible.
      particularity: Administer with caution in patients with heart failure. Esmolol may potentially be a better option given its short duration of action in those patients at risk for cardiovascular decompensation.

Use the oral route if possible at doses of 20-80mg q4-6h. If the IV route is used, transition to the PO route as soon as possible.

In addition to decreasing the cardiovascular effects of hyperthyroidism, propranolol decreases the conversion of T4 to T3.

## Indicación: Recurring ventricular tachycardia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to NaCl 0.9% for a total volume of 10 mL
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: Can be administered without dilution.
Administer no faster than 1mg/min.
      particularity: Repeat the dose q5min PRN to a maximum of 5mg total. Some sources recommend doses up to 10mg.

Administer with caution in patients with heart failure or hemodynamic instability. Esmolol may potentially be a better option given its short duration of action in those patients at risk for cardiovascular decompensation.

Use the oral route if possible at doses of 40mg q6h. If the IV route is used, transition to the PO route as soon as possible.
