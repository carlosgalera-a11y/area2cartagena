# Vancomycin (Vancocin®)
- Drug ID: 209
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Loading dose, severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Reconstitute every 500mg dose with 10mL of NaCl 0.9% for a final concentration of 50mg/mL.
2) Add to the recommended volume of NaCl 0.9%.
3) Administer every 500mg dose in 30 minutes using a volumetric pump. A faster administration can lead to hypersensibility reactions (vancomycin infusion syndrome a.k.a. "red man syndrome").

Maximal concentration for peripheral IV use = 5mg/mL.
      particularity: Loading dose in severe infection.
Adjust the doses and the interval of subsequent doses according to renal function and indication.
Max dose = 3g.
