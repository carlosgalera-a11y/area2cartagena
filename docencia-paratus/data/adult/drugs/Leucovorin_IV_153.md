# Leucovorin IV (Folinic acid (IV))
- Drug ID: 153
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Methanol intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1mg/kg  Administer IV without dilution over 3 minutes
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Discuss with your local poison center.

Administer with NaCl 0.9% or D5% with "Y" derivation.

If anionic gap without metabolic acidosis: 1 mg/kg.
Repeat the dose every 6 hours until the intoxication resolves.

If anionic gap with metabolic acidosis: 2 mg/kg.
Repeat the dose every 4-6 hours until the intoxication resolves and the metabolic acidosis is corrected.
Adjust the dose and the interval according to the severity of the acidosis and history (denutrition, alcoholism, etc.).
      particularity: Expert advice:
Maximum dose for 1mg/kg: 50mg
Maximum dose for 2mg/kg: 100mg
