# Maintenance fluids (IV fluids)
- Drug ID: 250
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Burns
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: The calculated rate is an initial rate based on the modified Brooke/Parkland formula:
2 mL/kg of IV fluids per percentage body surface area burned second or third degree over the first 24 hours, the first half being administered in the first 8 hours.

Adjust rate every hour according to volume status, urine output and hemodynamics.
Hypovolemic patients may require initial IV fluid boluses and more aggressive fluid resuscitation.
      particularity: Applicable only for patients with 20 % or greater of TBSA burned second or third degree.

Favor balanced fluids (e.g. LR).
Consider the addition of albumin for refractory patients.
Consider other causes of hypoperfusion and avoid volume overload.
