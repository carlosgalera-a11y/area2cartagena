# Folic acid (Vitamin B9)
- Drug ID: 84
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Adjuvant in methanol intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1 mg/kg  [IV at 5 mg/min]  Administer without dilution with syringe driver
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Administer undiluted with syringe driver at a rate of 5 mg/min = 1 mL/min (50 mg dose over 10 minutes; 100 mg dose over 20 minutes).
If a syringe driver is not available, the dose can be introduced into an empty bag and administered on pump at the same rate.

In case of elevated anion gap, but without metabolic acidosis: 1 mg/kg.
Repeat the dose every 6 hours until the intoxication resolves.

In case of elevated anion gap with metabolic acidosis: 2 mg/kg.
Repeat the dose every 4-6 hours until the intoxication resolves and the metabolic acidosis is corrected.
Adjust the dose and the interval according to the severity of the acidosis and patient characteristics (denutrition, alcoholism, etc.).
      particularity: Expert advice:
Maximum dose for 1 mg/kg : 50 mg
Maximum dose for 2 mg/kg : 100 mg
