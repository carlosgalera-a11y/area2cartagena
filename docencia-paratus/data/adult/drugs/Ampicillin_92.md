# Ampicillin (Penbritine®)
- Drug ID: 92
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Meningitis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Add to 50 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      administration: nil
      particularity: The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual interval q4h.
Adjust the interval according to renal function.

        • 2 g [weight_min=40, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
