# Levetiracetam (Keppra®)
- Drug ID: 154
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Status epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: For doses less than 3000mg:
1) Add calculated dose to a bag of 50mL of NaCl 0.9%.
2) Do not remove volume.
3) Administer IV over 5 minutes.

For doses of 3000mg or more:
1) Add calculated dose to a bag of 100mL of NaCl 0.9%.
2) Do not remove volume.
3) Administer IV over 10 minutes.
      particularity: Loading dose in status epilepticus.

Subsequent doses of 1-1.5g IV q12h can be administered if required. Adjust according to renal function. These doses can also be administered orally as bioavailability is close to 100%.
