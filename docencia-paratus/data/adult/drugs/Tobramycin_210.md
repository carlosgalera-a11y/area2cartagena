# Tobramycin (Nebcin®)
- Drug ID: 210
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Once daily dosing
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 6

  - STEP:   Add to 50mL of NaCl 0.9%
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Doses up to 7mg/kg can be used for sicker patients.

Some experts recommend decreasing the first dose in patients with renal insufficiency.

Because of the risk of renal impairment, consider another class of antibiotics in patients with CrCl less than 30ml/min.

Expert advice: Adjusted body weight is used for all weights (when inferior to total body weight) to ensure a logical progression in the doses with increasing patient weights. Some sources recommend using adjusted body weight only in patients with obesity (total body weight > 120% of ideal body weight or BMI > 30).
