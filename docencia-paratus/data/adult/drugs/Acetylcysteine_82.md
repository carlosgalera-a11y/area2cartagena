# Acetylcysteine (Mucomyst®)
- Drug ID: 82
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Acetaminophen intoxication, Quebec poison control protocol
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: First hour
      concentration: 200 mg/mL
      administration: This is the protocol of the Quebec Poison Control Center

Weight less than or equal to 50 kg:
1) Use a 500 mL bag of D5%.
2) Do not remove solute.
3) Add 24,000 mg (120 mL) of NAC 200 mg/ml.
4) Administer 150 mg/kg over 1 hour (first hour) then 15 mg/kg/h for at least 20 hours or as recommended by the poison control center (see "infusion" step).

Weight greater than or equal to 51 kg (maximum weight 100 kg):
1) Use a 1000 mL bag of D5%.
2) Do not remove solute.
3) Add 48,000 mg (240 mL) of NAC 200 mg/ml.
4) Administer 150 mg kg over 1 hour (first hour) then 15 mg/kg/h for at least 20 hours or as recommended by the poison control center (see "infusion" step).
      particularity: To be started and stopped according to the criteria of your poison control center.

Watch for the risk of an anaphylactoid reaction.

Calculate the dose based on actual weight up to a maximum of 100 kg.

Also compatible with NaCl 0.9% and 0.45%.
