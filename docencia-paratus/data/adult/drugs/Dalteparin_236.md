      administration: There are few data to support the use of dalteparin for patients over 150kg. Choose another molecule or administer a dose of 15,000 units +/- monitor anti-Xa levels or dose according to the actual body weight according to hemorrhagic risk and clinical situation.

Where possible, round to the nearest pre-prepared syringe dose:
5,000 units, 7,500 units, 10,000 units, 12,500 units, 15,000 units, 18,000 units.
      particularity: Use with caution for patients with ClCr <30 mL/minute, monitoring anti-Xa levels as needed.

The maximum dose of 15,000 units BID is an editorial choice, but supported by guidelines, since the population over 150 kg is poorly represented in the studies. Although some guidelines and organizations recommend dosing dalteparin using a total body weight up to 190kg, this may increase the risk of bleeding and potentially be of no benefit. Also, the monograph recommends a maximum dose of 18,000 units per day, recognizing however that higher doses are probably safe in obesity. Dosing anti-Xa levels should be considered in order to ensure the effectiveness of the treatment. Alternatively, another drug may be used such as unfractionated heparin or a vitamin K antagonist or a DOAC, depending on the clinical situation.
