# Dexmedetomidine (Precedex®)
- Drug ID: 121
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Post-intubation sedation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 2

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 4 mcg/mL
      administration: If 4 mcg/mL pre-prepared vials not available: 
1) Use vials of 100 mcg/mL.
2) Remove 4 mL from a 100 mL bag of  0.9% NaCl.
3) Add 400 mcg (4 mL) of dexmedetomidine to 96 mL of 0.9% NaCl.
4) Final concentration = 4 mcg/mL.

 Titration information: 
1) Start the infusion at 0.4 mcg/kg/h for frail patients and at 0.7 mcg/kg/h for others.
2) Increase the infusion by 0.1 mcg/kg/h every 15-30 minutes until the target level of sedation is obtained (use a sedation scale, e.g. RASS).
3) Maximum recommended dose 1.4 mcg/kg/h.
      particularity: Contraindicated in patients with severe heart failure, AV bloc or bradycardia. Monitor for the risk of hypotension and bradycardia.

Reduce the dose in patients with renal impairment, hepatic insufficiency.

In case of agitation, provide boluses of another agent (ex: propofol). Dexmedetomidine boluses are not recommended for this indication.

Off-label: can be used for other indications at the same doses (eg: sedation for non-invasive ventilation, adjunctive treatment in alcohol withdrawal, etc.).
