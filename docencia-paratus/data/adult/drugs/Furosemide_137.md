# Furosemide (Lasix®)
- Drug ID: 137
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Bolus (20 to 100 mg)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Administer IV over 2 minutes
      particularity: Administer 40mg IV for the patient in acute pulmonary edema who does not take furosemide on a regular basis.
Give at least the equivalent IV dose to a patient already taking furosemide (ex: patient takes furosemide 40mg PO BID, administer at least 40mg IV).

- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 120 mg
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: 1) Dilute in 50mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Administer at rate of 4mg/min (30 min for 120mg, 40 min for 160mg, 50 min for 200mg)
      particularity: Administer at least the equivalent IV dose to a patient already taking furosemide (ex: patient takes furosemide 40mg PO BID, administer at least 40mg IV).
Administer doses greater than 100mg at a maximum of 4mg/min.

## Indicación: Infusion
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: vial of 25mL (10mg/mL, total 250mg)
1) Add 250mg (25mL) to 100mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Do NOT remove volume
3) Final concentration: 2mg/mL
4) Start infusion at desired rate and adjust according to diuresis
      particularity: Ideally, a bolus should be administered prior to the infusion to obtain sufficient plasma concentrations.
