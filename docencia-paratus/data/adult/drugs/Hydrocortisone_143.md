# Hydrocortisone (Solucortef®)
- Drug ID: 143
- Type: drug
- Entorno: adult

## Indicación: Septic shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 30 seconds]
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 100 mg vial
1) Reconstitute with 1.8 mL of sterile water
2) Final concentration: 50 mg/mL
3) Administer 50 mg (1 mL) IV over 30 seconds
      particularity: Some experts recommend a loading dose of 100 mg.

Usual interval every 6 hours (total daily dose of 200 mg).

If possible, measure a cortisol level before administration.

## Indicación: Decompensated hypothyroidism (myxedema coma)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 30sec]
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 100mg vial
1) Reconstitute with 1.8mL of sterile water
2) Final concentration: 50mg/mL
3) Administer IV over 30sec 
4) Usual dosage: 100mg IV q8h
      particularity: Empirical treatment for myxedematous coma in view of the high incidence of concomitant adrenal insufficiency.
Measure a cortisol level before administration.
Stop therapy if adrenal insufficiency is excluded.
Usual interval q8h.

## Indicación: Thyroid storm
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Presentation: 100mg vial
1) Reconstitute with 1.8mL of sterile water.
2) Final concentration: 50mg/mL.
3) Add 300mg (6mL) in a bag of 50mL of 0.9% NaCl.
4) Administer IV over 20 minutes.
5) Follow the loading dose with doses of 100mg (2mL) IV q8h. Each 100mg dose can be given IV over 30 seconds without dilution.
      particularity: Reduces the conversion of T4 to T3.

Subsequent doses 100mg q8h.
