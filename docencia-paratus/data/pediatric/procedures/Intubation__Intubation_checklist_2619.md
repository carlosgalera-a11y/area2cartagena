## Tab: Patient
## Resuscitate before you intubate
    Perform physiological and hemodynamic stabilization prior to intubation whenever possible
## Position
    
    Head at the level of the intubator's sternum Ear-to sternal notch alignment
## Preoxygenation
    Non-rebreather mask aiming 100 % FiO₂ (Flow rate ≥ 15 L/min) Consider apneic oxygenation (nasal cannula under mask) Hypoxemia despite optimal preoxygenation: Add PEEP
