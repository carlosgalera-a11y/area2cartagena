# Don Sterile Gloves — Don Sterile Gloves

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Essentials
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 3013


## Tab: Default
## 1
    
    Thoroughly wash hands with water and soap or hydroalcoholic friction until completely dry
    [PROCEDURE] Hand hygiene (hydroalcoholic gel) — Hand hygiene (hydroalcoholic gel)
## 2
    
    Open the outer packaging without touching the inside of the gloves Unfold the inner packaging on a clean surface The gloves must remain oriented palm up
## 3
    
    With the non-dominant hand, grab the first glove by the inner back of the wrist (this area will be against the skin and can be touched) Put on the glove on the dominant hand, without touching the outside of the glove
## 4
    
    Keep your thumb extended and away at all times to avoid contaminating the glove when donning the second one Slide the gloved fingers of the dominant hand under the cuff of the second glove Put on the glove on the non-dominant hand, avoiding touching the outside of the glove with bare skin
## 5
    
    Adjust the gloves only by touching the already sterile parts (outer against outer) Avoid all contact with a non-sterile surface
