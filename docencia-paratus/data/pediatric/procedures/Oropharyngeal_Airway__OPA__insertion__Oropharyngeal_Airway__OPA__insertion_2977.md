# Oropharyngeal Airway (OPA) insertion — Oropharyngeal Airway (OPA) insertion

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Airway & Ventilation
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2977


## Tab: Preparation
## Indication
    Unconscious patient without gag reflex requiring airway management.
## Contraindication
  ### Absolute
    Conscious patient or intact gag reflex
  ### Relative
    Severe oral trauma or obstruction Known oral pathology or recent surgery

## Equipment preparation

## Tab: Execution
## 1
    Measure from the corner of the patient’s mouth to the angle of the jaw
## 2
    Use head-tilt/chin-lift or jaw-thrust maneuver if spinal injury suspected
## 3
    Insert the oropharyngeal airway under direct vision (concave surface on the tongue)
## 4
    
    Flange should rest securely against lips without protrusion Observe chest rise, assess breath sounds, and monitor for improved airflow
