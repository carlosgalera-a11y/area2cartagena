# Basic Airway Opening Maneuvers — Head Tilt - Chin Lift

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Airway & Ventilation
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2983


## Tab: Default
## Indications
    Unconscious or semi-conscious patient with airway obstruction Need to open the airways before definitive airway management (Bag-mask ventilation, insertion of an oropharyngeal/nasopharyngeal airway, intubation)
## Contraindications
    Avoid head tilt with chin lift for suspected cervical spine injury Perform jaw thrust instead
## 1
    
    Place one hand on the forehead and apply gentle pressure backwards (tilting the head) Place the fingers of the other hand under the chin bone (avoiding soft tissues)
## 2
    
    Lift the chin upwards and forwards to clear the airways
## 3
    Monitor chest rise, ventilation effectiveness, and ensure there are no signs of obstruction
