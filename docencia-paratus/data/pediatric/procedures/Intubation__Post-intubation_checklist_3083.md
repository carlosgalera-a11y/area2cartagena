# Intubation — Post-intubation checklist

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Airway & Ventilation
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 3083


## Tab: Ventilation
## Confirm tube position
    Use waveform EtC0₂ (gold standard), ultrasound or esophageal detector device to confirm tube placement Auscultation or lung ultrasound at the costophrenic angle
## Secure the endotracheal tube
    Secure the endotracheal tube with tape or a commercial holder Order a chest X-ray Establish cuff pressure: 20–30 cmH₂O (check with manometer) Reassess ETT depth if repositioning or with changes in lung compliance
## General measures
    Head of bed elevation 15–30 degrees Assess pCO₂ / pH through venous or arterial blood gas analysis Humidify inspired gases (active system or HME filter) Assess need for bronchodilators if obstructive component
## Initial ventilator settings
    Set a lung-protective ventilation strategy to maintain a plateau pressure < 30 cmH₂0
