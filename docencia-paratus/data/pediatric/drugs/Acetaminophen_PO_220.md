# Acetaminophen PO (Tylenol® PO)
- Drug ID: 220
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Pain or fever
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 15 mg/Kg q6h  [PO]  q6h reg or PRN
      concentration: 80 mg/mL
      administration: • Intra-rectal acetaminophen should only be used as a last resort, as absorption is erratic and bioavailability can be as low as 33%.
• Contraindicated in severe hepatic impairment with impairment of function (INR, glycemia or albumin)
      particularity: • The dosage of 15 mg/Kg/dose q4h PRN, maximum 5 doses/day is also possible, but may cause an interval of 8 hours without a dose.
• Maximum dose of 75 mg/Kg/day to a maximum of 4 g/day
