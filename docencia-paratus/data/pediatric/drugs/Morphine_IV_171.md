# Morphine IV (Duramorph®)
- Drug ID: 171
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Use the minimum required dose for adequate analgesia and repeat as needed.

If 2 mg/mL concentration is unavailable:

Presentation : Vial of Morphine 10 mg/mL
1) Withdraw 1 mL (1 vial) of Morphine 10 mg/mL with a 5 mL syringe
2) Add to 4 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 10 mg in 5 mL= 2 mg/mL
 
OR

Presentation : Vial of Morphine 15 mg/mL
1) Withdraw 1 mL (1 vial) of Morphine 15 mg/mL with a 10 mL syringe
2) Add to 6.5 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 15 mg in 7.5 mL= 2 mg/mL
      particularity: Maximum effect after 20 minutes
Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Adjustment necessary in renal failure if repeated dose (favor hydromorphone)

## Indicación: TET spell in Tetralogy of Fallot
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Intravenous  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
• Opinion of Pediatric Intensive Care recommended
• Use in agitated or painful patient with hypoxic Tetralogy of Fallot.
• The decrease in heart rate and respiratory rate due to morphine reduces the right-to-left shunt and improves pulmonary blood flow in the tetralogy of Fallot.

• For initial titration (patient on continuous infusion of morphine or q3h), repeat every 5-10 minutes as needed. 
• If morphine IV q3h regular or PRN, start at 0.05 or 0.1 mg/Kg/dose.
• If breakthrough dose of IV morphine (q1h PRN), administer 1/2 the dose of morphine q3h or the same dose as the hourly dose of the continuous IV infusion. (e.g.: IV morphine at 0.05 mg/Kg/h => breakthrough dose of IV morphine at 0.05 mg/Kg q1h PRN)

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: The use of morphine 2 mg/mL to make a morphine infusion is not recommended, as it requires too much handling.

• Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration. 

1) Start the infusion at a rate ranging from 0.03-0.05 mg/Kg/h
2) Titrate by increments of 0.01 mg/Kg/h q1h until the desired level of analgesia is achieved. (Expert opinion) 

• Before starting the continuous infusion, consider an initial titration with IV morphine boluses repeated every 5-10 minutes as needed.
