# Metronidazole IV (Flagyl®)
- Drug ID: 167
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual Dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infection (Loading dose, less than 1 month)  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Caution:
• Before the administration of the 2nd dose, validate the dose and dosage interval according to gestational age and postnatal age with a specialist in pediatrics or neonatology.
• Usual dose for infants (< 1 month): 7.5 mg/Kg/dose IV q12-24h (0-7 days: q24h, 8-28 days: q12h), excluding patients < 37 weeks.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
No adjustment required in renal failure, but adjustment in dialysis
      doses:

  - STEP: Infection (≥ 1 month)  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Usual dosage: 30-40 mg/Kg/day divided q6-8h up to a maximum of 500 mg IV q6h excluding patients < 1 month and premature.
No adjustment required in renal failure, but adjustment in dialysis
Can be given q6h in necrotizing fasciitis or in the presence of cerebral abscess.
