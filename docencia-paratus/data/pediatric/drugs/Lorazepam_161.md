# Lorazepam (Ativan®)
- Drug ID: 161
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV  over 30-60 seconds]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted with equal volume of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: • Repeat a 2nd dose of benzodiazepine after 5 min then switch to a 2nd line agent if status epilepticus persists. Administration of > 2 doses of benzodiazepines is associated with a greater incidence of respiratory depression.
• Lorazepam is not the first line of treatment for neonatal Status Epilepticus - consider the use of Phenobarbital as the first line
      particularity: Stable for 6 months at room temperature.
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=2, weight=0, min=0, max=4]
