# Thiamine (Vitamine B1)
- Drug ID: 202
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Ethylene glycol intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 5 min]  Administer IV diluted in 10 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: • Administer only in the presence of metabolic acidosis.
• Usual interval: every 8 hours.
• Contact your local poison control center if hemodialysis, as recommendations on continuing or stopping thiamine during renal replacement therapy are unclear in the literature.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR
• Keep refrigerated
• Protect from light
      doses:
        • 50 mg (0,5 mL) [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
