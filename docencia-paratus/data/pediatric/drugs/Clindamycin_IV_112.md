# Clindamycin IV (Dalacin® or Cleocin®)
- Drug ID: 112
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosing
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 150 mg/mL
      administration: Administer dose ≤ 300 mg over 10 minutes, dose 301 to 600 mg over 20 minutes and dose 600 to 900 mg over 30 minutes.
It is possible to administer the drug over a longer period according to existing hospital protocols.
Can be administered IM if undiluted (150 mg/mL)

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag.
2) Add the indicated dose.
3) Administer at the indicated rate.

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
If using as an adjunctive protein synthesis inhibitor for toxin removal, always combine with a penicillin or cephalosporin.
Usual dosage: 30-40 mg/Kg/day divided q8h up to a maximum of 900 mg IV q8h excluding patients <1 month and premature.
No adjustment in renal insufficiency.
