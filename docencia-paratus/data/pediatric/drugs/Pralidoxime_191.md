# Pralidoxime (Protopam®)
- Drug ID: 191
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Organophosphate intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: • Consider during poisoning by an organophosphate insecticide causing the presence of nicotine signs (e.g.: fasciculations, muscle weakness) or damage to the central nervous system (e.g.: coma). Does not replace the use of atropine in the treatment of muscarinic signs (e.g. bronchorrhea).
• Exceptionally, in pulmonary edema or significant fluid restriction, pralidoxime can be administered undiluted over 5 minutes. The need for fluid restriction is rare in cholinergic intoxication, because insensible losses are increased and rapid IV administration can result in: tachycardia, laryngospasm, apnea, muscle rigidity and transient neuromuscular block. Consult your poison control center.
• Follow the loading dose by the maintenance infusion. 

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Pralidoxime 1000 mg
1) Add 20 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 50 mg/mL
      particularity: • Y-site compatible only with NaCl. 
• Antidote available in Canada only through Health Canada's Special Access Program.
• Administer the loading dose as soon as possible after poisoning.
