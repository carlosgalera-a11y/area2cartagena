# Protamine ([])
- Drug ID: 195
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Bleeding with unfractionated heparin (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Unknown dose/time
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      concentration note: Opinion of pediatric hematology recommended
      administration: In most cases of non life-threatening bleeding due to a heparin infusion, discontinuing the infusion alone is adequate.

Overcorrection with protamine can lead to paradoxical bleeding by inhibition of factor V.

The 0.5 mg/Kg up to maximal dose of 50 mg of protamine (expert opinion) is for patients with life-threatening bleeding and with ongoing or recent administration of therapeutic dose heparin only if it is impossible to calculate the exact dose of protamine (see below). 

In case of less severe bleeding or if the administration of the anticoagulant dates more than a few hours, calculate the appropriate dose of protamine (see other specific tab) or consider administering a dose of 0.25 mg/Kg up to a maximum of 25 mg and repeating the dose only if necessary depending on clinical response (maximum of 1 mg/Kg up to 50 mg total. Experts opinion). 

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: Rapid administration may cause hypotension, a non-IgE mediated anaphylactic reaction or pulmonary hypertension.

Monitor aPTT/anti-Xa 10 min after protamine is given, then again in 2-8 hours.
