# Nitroglycerin (Nitroject®)
- Drug ID: 175
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Heart failure with coronary artery disease
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Premixed bag)
      concentration: 200 mcg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag/vial at 50 mg/250 mL D5W (200 mcg/mL)
      administration: Suggested titration:
1) Start the infusion at 0.3-0.5 mcg/Kg/min
2) Increase by 0.3-0.5 mcg/Kg/min q15min if hypertension up to a maximum of 2 mcg/Kg/min. (Expert opinion)

Additional information:
• Doses > 2 mcg/Kg/min may cause arterial vasodilation. Use other agents for this indication. (Nicardipine, Nitroprusside, Milrinone or Phentolamine)
• The use of bags without DEHP is recommended to reduce the release of plasticizers. For this reason, administration via syringe pump is not advised, as syringes are typically made of PVC.
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
Monitor methemoglobinemia if dose > 2 mcg/Kg/min or combination with NO.
Avoid in patients with Intracranial Hypertension.

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: If pre-mixed bag not available:

Presentation: Vial of Nitroglycerin 5 mg/mL (50 mg in 10 mL)
1) Use 250 mL bag of D5W WITHOUT DEHP for preparation of the infusion
2) Add 10 mL (1 vial) of Nitroglycerin 5 mg/mL to the bag.
3) Final concentration = 50 mg in 260 mL = 0.192 mg/mL = 192 mcg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.

Suggested titration:
1) Start the infusion at 0.3-0.5 mcg/Kg/min
