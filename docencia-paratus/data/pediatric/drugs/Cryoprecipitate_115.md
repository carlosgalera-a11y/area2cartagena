# Cryoprecipitate ([])
- Drug ID: 115
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Massive hemorrhage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Rate of administration varies depending on the clinical indication
      administration: Additional informations:
• Repeat according to clinical response
• In early massive hemorrhage, favor transfusing blood products at a ratio of 1:1:1 (Plasma: Platelets: RBCs: Cryo)
• Fibrinogen may be especially important in neonatal and obstetric haemorrhages
• If low fibrinogen AND absence of bleeding, pediatric hematology opinion recommended
• 1 bag of cryoprecipitate = 1 unit (approximately 15 mL)
      particularity: Dose for Massive Transfusion Protocol
      doses:
        • 1 unit [weight_min=2, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 2 units [weight_min=15, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 3 units [weight_min=25, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 4 units [weight_min=35, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 5 units [weight_min=45, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 6 units [weight_min=55, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
