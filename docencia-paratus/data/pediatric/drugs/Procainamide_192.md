# Procainamide (Pronestyl®)
- Drug ID: 192
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: VT with pulse/SVT (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV DILUTED
      concentration: 3.85 mg/mL
      administration: Caution: 
• Opinion of Pediatric Intensive Care or Pediatric Cardiology recommended
• If the patient is hypotensive, it is possible to administer the bolus over 10-30 minutes.
• Administration of bolus procainamide < 5 min increases the risk of severe hypotension. 
• If still in arrhythmia, additional boluses up to a total maximum of 15 mg/Kg or 1000 mg is possible.

DILUTED 3.85 mg/mL solution:

Further dilution for administration via syringe pump:
Presentation: Vial of Procainamide 100 mg/mL (1000 mg in 10 mL)
1) Use a 250 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
2) Add 10 mL (1 vial) of Procainamide 100 mg/mL to the bag, WITHOUT removing volume
3) Final concentration = 1000 mg in 260 mL = 3.85 mg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
5) For a dose of 3-6 mg/Kg, administer 0.78-1.56 mL/Kg (max 26 mL) of the solution diluted to 3.85 mg/mL over 5 min

Additional information:
The DILUTED solution at 3.85 mg/mL allows the administration of boluses and continuous infusion via the same solution. The volume provided by multiple boluses is adequate even for fluid restricted patients.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Use with caution in patients with heart, liver or kidney failure. Adjust the dose in hepatic or renal impairment.
Do not use if AV block of the 2nd or 3rd or if presence of lupus syndrome.
Do not use if in doubt about Torsade de Pointes and assess risks/benefits with cardiology if QTc> 500 msec.
Consider monitoring serum procainamide and N-Acetyl-Procainamide (NAPA) levels in patients < 5 kg or with renal failure. If monitoring of serum levels impossible, close follow-up of the ECG for measurement of QRS and QTc.

  - STEP: Drip
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
Opinion of a pediatric cardiologist with expertise in electrophysiology recommended if total maximum bolus dose reached or > 50 mcg/Kg/min.

Further dilution for administration via pump:
1) Prepare a DILUTED 3,85 mg/mL solution
2) Do NOT remove volume during preparation
3) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
4) After the bolus, start the continuous infusion at 20 mcg/Kg/min

Additional information:
If still in arrhythmia, additional boluses up to a total maximum of 15 mg/Kg or 1000 mg is possible.
