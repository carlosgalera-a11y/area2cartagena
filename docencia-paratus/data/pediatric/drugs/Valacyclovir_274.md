# Valacyclovir (Valtrex®)
- Drug ID: 274
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Immunocompetent
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: HSV (BID)
      administration: • Do not use valacyclovir in infants or patients with suspected encephalitis. Consult an infectious diseases specialist if immunosuppressed child.
• Use valacyclovir tablets or tablet fractions of 500 mg if compound oral solution not available.
• There is a compound oral solution of valacyclovir 50 mg/mL
• Duration of the treatment:• Primary infection: 7-10 days
• Recurring episode: 5 days

• Alternative dosage if herpes labialis in 12 years: Valacyclovir 2000 mg (4 co) PO q12h x 2 doses from the appearance of symptoms
• Favor valacyclovir for compliance; acyclovir is administered 3-5 times/day in this indication.

      administration: • Do not use < 3 months of life and favor IV acyclovir. Consult an infectious diseases specialist if immunosuppressed child.
• Treatment of VZV in immunocompetent children should not be used routinely. Consider in unvaccinated children, with chronic pulmonary or dermatological conditions, on chronic aspirin or chronic corticosteroids or in frequent intermittent treatment.
• Use valacyclovir tablets or tablet fractions of 500 mg if compound oral solution not available.
• There is a compound oral solution of valacyclovir 50 mg/mL
• Duration of the treatment: 5 days
• Favor valacyclovir for compliance; acyclovir is administered 4-5 times/day in this indication.
