# Cefixime (Suprax®)
- Drug ID: 266
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Urinary tract infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Daily  [PO]  x 1 dose STAT then start once daily 12 hours after the dose
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: • Exclusion criteria for empiric PO treatment• < 2 months of life
• Complicated or non-isolated urinary tract infection (exclude systemic, neurological involvement, dehydration)
• Renal function impairment
• History of resistant infection
• Anatomical abnormality or surgical history at the nephro-urinary level
• Double J or other urinary device
• Uropathy under investigation

• The loading dose (8 mg/Kg/dose PO q12h on day 1) comes from a consensus of local experts (pediatric infectiologists and paediatricians); this allows adequate urinary cefixime concentrations to be achieved more quickly with a low risk of adverse effects.
