# Caspofungin (Cancidas®)
- Drug ID: 101
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection (≥ 3 months)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 7

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Caspofungin 250 mg
1) Add 10.8 mL of NaCl 0.9% or sterile water
2) Shake gently, avoiding foaming the solution
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 5 mg/mL
4) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%

Additional information:
• Final concentration after further dilution < 0.5 mg/mL
      particularity: Incompatible with D5W. Y-site compatible with NaCl, LR. 
Usual dose for ≥ 3 months of life: Loading dose of 70 mg/m2, max 70 mg IV followed by 50 mg/m2, max 50 mg IV q24h.
No adjustment required in renal failure
Body surface area in m2, without measuring height, obtained by Costeff's formula: m2= [(4 x Kg)+ 7]/(90+ Kg).

## Indicación: Severe infection (Less than 3 months)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 7

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Caspofungin 250 mg
1) Add 10.8 mL of NaCl 0.9% or sterile water
2) Shake gently, avoiding foaming the solution
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 5 mg/mL
4) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%

Additional information:
• Final concentration after further dilution < 0.5 mg/mL

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag.
2) Add the indicated dose.
3) Administer at the indicated rate.

Consider that the use of a bag of 25 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation
      particularity: Incompatible with D5W. Y-site compatible with NaCl, LR. 
Usual dose for < 3 months of life: 25 mg/m2 IV q24h
No adjustment required in renal failure
