# Fosfomycin (Monuril®)
- Drug ID: 279
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Acute cystitis (uncomplicated) in teenage girl
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Acute cystitis (uncomplicated)
      administration: Woman aged 14 and over who presents, among the following, at least two of the symptoms or signs of recent appearance, suggestive of cystitis:

• Dysuria
• Urgency to void (urgenturia)
• Frequent urge to urinate (pollakiuria)
• Suprapubic pain or discomfort
• Hematuria
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
        • 3 g PO x 1 dose [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
