# Dimenhydrinate IV (Gravol®)
- Drug ID: 228
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Nausea/Travel sickness
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 2 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: • Favor ondansetron so as not to alter the patient's state of consciousness with the anti-nausea treatment
• Data limited between 6 months and 2 years; single dose use for motion sickness is acceptable. (Expert's opinion) For other indications, do not use and favor a proven effective treatment such as ondansetron.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with NaCl 0.9%, D5W, Lactate Ringer and KCl
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=50]
