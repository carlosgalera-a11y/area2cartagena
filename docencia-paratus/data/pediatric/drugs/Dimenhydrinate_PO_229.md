# Dimenhydrinate PO (Gravol®)
- Drug ID: 229
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Nausea/Travel sickness
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [PO or via nasogastric tube]
      concentration: 3 mg/mL
      administration: • If oral solution not available, dilute 1/2 of a 50 mg tablet in 8.4 mL of water (3 mg/mL) for administration.
• Favor ondansetron so as not to alter the patient's state of consciousness with the anti-nausea treatment
• Data limited between 6 months and 2 years; single dose use for motion sickness is acceptable. (Expert's opinion) For other indications, do not use and favor a proven effective treatment such as ondansetron.
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
