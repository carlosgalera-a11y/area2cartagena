# Desmopressin (DDAVP®)
- Drug ID: 119
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Intracranial hemorrhage associated with antiplatelet agents
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 4 mcg/mL
      administration: IV administration without further dilution over 1 min should not be used for hematological indications of desmopressin, but only in diabetes insipidus.

Can be administered subcutaneously as a last resort, as subcutaneous absorption is erratic in patients in shock.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Monitor for hyponatremia as well as IV intake and urine output. Desmopressin can have potent antidiuretic effect.
