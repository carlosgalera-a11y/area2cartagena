# Sodium thiosulfate (Seacalphyx®)
- Drug ID: 203
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Cyanide intoxication (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 250 mg/mL
      administration: • Repeat as needed for an additional ½ dose (125 mg/Kg) if signs of cyanide toxicity reappear. 
• 12500 mg = 12.5 g
• Consider reducing the rate of administration if hypotension

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. Incompatible with hydroxocobalamin and LR (no data with LR)
• Protect from light
• The antidote of choice in the treatment of cyanide poisoning is hydroxocobalamin. Sodium thiosulfate is an adjuvant treatment when there is a partial response to hydroxocobalamin. If hydroxocobalamin is not available, administer sodium thiosulfate in combination with sodium nitrite. 
• Discontinue nitroprusside if in use.
