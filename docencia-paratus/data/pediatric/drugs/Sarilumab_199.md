# Sarilumab (Kevzara®)
- Drug ID: 199
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: COVID + with hypoxemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 175 mg/mL
      administration: Further dilution for administration via pump:
1) Add 400 mg to a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
2) To mix, gently invert the bag to avoid foaming
3) Administer IV over 1 hour
4) Finish by rinsing with 20 mL of NaCl 0.9%

• COVID-19 + with hypoxemia under oxygen therapy with CRP > 75 mg/L or high-flow oxygen therapy, non-invasive or invasive mechanical ventilation, in case of a shortage of tocilizumab
      particularity: Contraindications: neutrophils < 0.5 x 10^9/L, platelets < 50 x 10^9/L, septic shock (not due to COVID-19), ALT > 5x upper normal limit, patient immunosuppressed or on immunosuppressive therapy , serious or latent infection (eg: tuberculosis), allergy to sarilumab or one of its components.

Keep refrigerated.
