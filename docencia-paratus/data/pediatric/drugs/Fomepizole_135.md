# Fomepizole (Antizol®)
- Drug ID: 135
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Toxic alcohol intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 1000 mg/mL
      administration: • During methanol poisoning if the serum concentration is greater than or equal to 6 mmol/L (19.2 mg/dL). Folic acid or folinic acid can also be administered as an adjuvant. 
• During ethylene glycol poisoning if the serum concentration is greater than or equal to 3 mmol/L (18.6 mg/dL). Pyridoxine and thiamine can also be administered as adjuvants.
• When compatible history and symptoms AND metabolic acidosis with unexplained osmolar gap > 10, while waiting for a dosage.
 
If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. Incompatible with LR (no data)
• Stable for 24 hours at room temperature or in the refrigerator once diluted
• If the solution has solidified in the vial (<25°C), it should be liquefied by placing the vial under warm running tap  water or warming by holding the vial in the hand.  Solidification does not compromise the efficacy, safety or stability of the product.
• Discuss with your local toxicology center to establish the need for maintenance doses if IV route is not available and need to administer PO.

## Indicación: Severe acetaminophen overdose
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 1000 mg/mL
      administration: Fomepizole is not a routine treatment for acetaminophen poisoning. Consider, after discussion with your local poison control center, in the presence of massive acetaminophen poisoning if hemodialysis is not available or late presentation. 

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. Incompatible with LR (no data)
• Stable for 24 hours at room temperature or in the refrigerator once diluted
• If the solution has solidified in the vial (<25°C), it should be liquefied by placing the vial under warm running tap  water or warming by holding the vial in the hand.  Solidification does not compromise the efficacy, safety or stability of the product.
• Does not replace Acetylcystein (NAC); administer concomitantly.
• Indicated in severe acetaminophen intoxication, usually defined as one of the following: ingestion > 500 mg/Kg or > 30 g, 4 hour post-ingestion level > 5000 micromol/L or > 755 mcg/mL.
• Consider in patients with choc, metabolic acidosis or altered mental status.
