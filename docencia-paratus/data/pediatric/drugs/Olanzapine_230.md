# Olanzapine (Zyprexa®)
- Drug ID: 230
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe agitation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IM  [IM in < 10 seconds]  Administer IM undiluted in the thigh after reconstitution
      concentration: 5 mg
      administration: Reconstitution:
1) Presentation: Vial of Olanzapine 10 mg
2) Add 2 mL of sterile water
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 5 mg/mL
4) Draw the indicated dose
      particularity: • The combination with a benzodiazepine increases the risk of respiratory depression.
• Negligible increase in QTc (4 msec). It is not necessary to do an ECG before administration.
• The risks of extrapyramidal effects and neuroleptic malignant syndrome are less frequent with atypical antipsychotics such as olanzapine than with conventional antipsychotics such as haloperidol.

Contraindications:
Serotonin syndrome → caution in case of poisoning (discuss with Poison Control Center)
Tardive dyskinesia
Congenital long QT syndrome (acceptable if acquired long QTc)

      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: • The injectable formulation of olanzapine is not officially approved for IV administration (like haloperidol), but is reported to be safe, equivalent to haloperidol in efficacy with fewer adverse effects.
• Data on repeated use in children under 10 years of age, particularly with the IV form, are limited. Reassess indication before each dose considering potential accumulation.
