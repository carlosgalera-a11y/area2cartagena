If premixed bag not available
Presentation: Vial or pre-filled Lidocaine syringe 2% = 20 mg/mL (100 mg in 5 mL)
1) Remove 20 mL from a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% = 80 mL
2) Add 20 mL (4 syringes) of Lidocaine 20 mg/mL to the bag
3) Final concentration = 400 mg in 100 mL = 4 mg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration

Suggested titration:
1) Start the infusion at 20 mcg/Kg/min after at least one bolus
2) Administer an additional bolus of 1 mg/Kg as needed before each increase in infusion (Expert opinion)
3) Maximum dose of 50 mcg/Kg/min or 4 mg/min
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
• Adjust the dose of the continuous infusion in heart failure, severe hepatic function, hemodynamic instability.
• No adjustment in renal failure suggested, but potential accumulation of metabolites.
• Monitoring of plasma concentrations (target of 5-21 micromol/L or 1.5-5 mcg/mL) suggested if toxicity is suspected or prolonged infusion.

## Indicación: Intraosseous analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1st dose  [Intraosseous over 30-60 seconds]  Administer Intraosseous undiluted
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: • Caution: Multiple formulations of lidocaine. Use lidocaine 2% (20 mg/mL) WITHOUT epinephrine.
• Use in patient conscious enough to feel pain
      particularity: The dose of lidocaine for intraosseous analgesia is lower than the doses used in Vtach with pulse and IV analgesia (1-1.5 mg/Kg); its use is safe, even for infants.
