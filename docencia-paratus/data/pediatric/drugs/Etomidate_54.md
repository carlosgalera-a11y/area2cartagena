# Etomidate (Amidate®)
- Drug ID: 54
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Intubation - Induction Agent
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Etomidate is not recommended as an induction agent in sepsis or septic shock (possibility of adrenal insufficiency).
      particularity: May induce myoclonus if administered without paralysis, not to be confused with seizures.
