# Penicillin V (Pen VK®)
- Drug ID: 278
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Group A Streptococcus pharyngitis (BID)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: • Oral solution of penicillin V is discontinued in Canada. Use amoxicillin for the treatment of group A Streptococcus pharyngitis if oral solution treatment is desired.

      particularity: Nil
