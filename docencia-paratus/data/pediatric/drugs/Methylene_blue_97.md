# Methylene blue (Methylthioninium chloride)
- Drug ID: 97
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Refractory vasoplegic shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
• Opinion of Pediatric Intensive Care recommended
• Last-line treatment in refractory vasoplegic shock.
• Ideally administered via central venous access, otherwise administration via good quality peripheral access is safe if for a short period of time and with frequent checks of the access site.

Additional information:
• In the presence of a clinical response, consider starting the infusion 1-2 hours after the bolus/test dose.
• Pulse oximetry reading may no longer be reliable after administration of methylene blue.
• Rinse the tubing before and after each injection to reduce the risk of thrombophlebitis.
      particularity: Incompatible with NaCl, LR. Y-site compatible with D5W. 

Avoid if suspicion of pulmonary arterial hypertension.
Pay attention to interactions with serotonergic agents since severe toxicity can occur.

      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
Opinion of Pediatric Intensive Care recommended

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 0.25 mg/Kg/h.
2) Titrate 0.25 mg/Kg/h q1h according to clinical response
3) Maximum dose of 2 mg/Kg/h. (Expert opinion)

Additional information:
• In the presence of a clinical response to the bolus dose, consider starting the infusion 1 hour after the bolus dose
• If a drip is started, administration by central access or intraosseous catheter is preferred (vesicant agent)
• Pulse oximetry reading may no longer be reliable after administration of methylene blue

## Indicación: Methemoglobinemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% by pump or syringe pump
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
• Contact your poison control center
• Rinse with 2.5-5 mL of NaCl 0.9% before and after each injection to reduce the risk of thrombophlebitis.
• Loss of reliability of pulse oximetry for 30 to 45 minutes after administration of methylene blue.

Additional information:
Possibility of diluting to facilitate administration and especially to reduce irritation and local pain.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

The dose can be repeated after 30 to 60 minutes depending on the clinical response.

Maximum of 100 mg/dose.
Maximum dose: 7 mg/kg/day

Risk of serotonergic toxicity when combined with other serotonergics. (e.g. Interaction with SSRI)
