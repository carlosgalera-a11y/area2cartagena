# Trimethoprim / sulfamethoxazole PO (Septra® or Bactrim® PO)
- Drug ID: 269
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage (excluding severe MRSA, Stenotrophomonas and pneumocystis)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: BID  [PO]  BID
      concentration: 8 mg/mL
      concentration note: Dose expressed in mg of TMP
      administration: • Solution at 8 mg/mL of TMP and 40 mg/mL of SMX
• In case the oral solution is back ordered, there is a compound oral solution of TMP/SMX 8/40 mg/mL.
      particularity: • There is no cross-allergy between TMP/SMX and diuretics.
• Multiple potential adverse effects (variable incidence depending on the dose and especially the duration): photosensitivity, severe allergic reaction like SJS, central pancytopenia, nephrotoxicity or type IV tubular acidosis (HyperK), drug fever.
