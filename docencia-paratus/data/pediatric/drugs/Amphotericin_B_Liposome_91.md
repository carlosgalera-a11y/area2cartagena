# Amphotericin B Liposome (Ambisome®)
- Drug ID: 91
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Invasive fungemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Two formulations of amphotericin B are available: deoxycholate solution (Fungizone®) and liposomal suspension (Ambisome®). These formulations are not equivalent: the properties, indications and dosages are not the same from one formulation to another.

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Amphotericin B Liposome 50 mg
1) Add 12 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 4 mg/mL
3) Shake the vial vigorously for about 15 seconds
4) Take the indicated dose WITH THE 5 micron FILTER and add to the indicated volume of D5%

Additional information:
• Final concentration between 0.2 and 2 mg/mL.
• Do NOT filter during administration
• Do NOT administer IV push.

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag.
2) Add the indicated dose.
3) Administer at the indicated rate.

Consider that the use of a bag of 25 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation
      particularity: Incompatible in Y with NaCl, LR or any other solutions. Only compatible with D5%.

Newborns with invasive candidiasis are at high risk for renal damage due to the infection; the deoxycholate solution penetrates the kidneys better than the liposomal formulation. Deoxycholate solution therefore remains the first-line treatment for invasive Candida infections in newborns.

Usual dose: 3-10 mg/Kg/dose IV q24h (No maximum dose in mg)
Dosage adjustment in severe renal impairment.
Nephrotoxic medication
