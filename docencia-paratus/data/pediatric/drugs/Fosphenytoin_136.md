# Fosphenytoin (Cerebyx®)
- Drug ID: 136
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Syringe pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV DILUTED with equal volume of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 50 mg/mL (PE)
      concentration note: Dose expressed in mg of "Phenytoin Equivalent" (PE)
      administration: Caution: 
• Dose expressed in mg of "Phenytoin Equivalent" (PE)
• Presentation: 50 mg/mL in Phenytoin Equivalents (PE) = 75 mg/mL of Fosphenytoin sodium.
• Use Phenytoin OR Fosphenytoin. Do NOT combine the 2 therapies
• If toxic seizures, do NOT use Fosphenytoin; use Phenobarbital or Levetiracetam.

Additional information:
• Does NOT require using a filter.
• Can be administered IM undiluted, but the large volume of administration may require up to 4 administration sites and IM administration is not recommended in status epilepticus. Favor the use of an intraosseous route rather than IM.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
The use of Phenobarbital and Levetiracetam are preferred in status epilepticus in children < 6 months; therapeutic plasma levels are more difficult to achieve with Phenytoin.
• Maximum administration rate: 3 mg PE/Kg/min (maximum 150 mg PE/min).
• Fosphenytoin is rapidly metabolized to Phenytoin; aim for the same serum phenytoin levels and consider the same drug interactions.
