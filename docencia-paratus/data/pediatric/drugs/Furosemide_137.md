# Furosemide (Lasix®)
- Drug ID: 137
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Acute oedema/heart failure
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 10 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9% with syringe pump
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: • In the absence of a syringe pump or if availability of venous access is limited, it is acceptable to administer a first dose of furosemide over 2-5 minutes.
• More risk of ototoxicity when administered in less than 10 min, patient chronically on furosemide or ototoxic drugs.
• Patient naive to furosemide: 0.5-1 mg/Kg IV, max 40 mg
• Patient already on furosemide: give at least the chronic PO dose IV. Maximal dose: 2 mg/Kg IV, max 80 mg except for furosemide challenge during anuria
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR, KCl.
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=20]
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=40]

## Indicación: Subsequent dose in oliguria or anuria
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Over 30 min (Syringe pump)  [IV over 30 min]  Administer IV DILUTED up to 10 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: • Try a classic Furosemide Stress Test (1 mg/Kg IV for patient naive to furosemide, otherwise 1.5 mg/Kg IV) before using dose > 2 mg/Kg. Consult with intensive care or nephrology specialists to evaluate the best choice between higher furosemide dose or renal replacement therapy.
• Administration over 30 min to decrease the risk of ototoxicity
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR, KCl.
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=80]
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=120]
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=200]
