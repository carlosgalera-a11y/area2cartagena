# Lansoprazole (Prevacid Fast-tab®)
- Drug ID: 256
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Gastroesophageal reflux
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      administration: • Place tablet ON tongue and allow to disintegrate until pellets can be swallowed.
OR
• For the 30 mg tablet, put the tablet or part of a tablet in an oral syringe of 10 mL by withdrawing the plunger, draw up 10 mL of water then shake gently. Administer the dose and rinse with 5 mL of water.
• For the 15 mg tablet, put the tablet or part of a tablet in an oral syringe of 5 mL by withdrawing the plunger, draw up 2 mL of water then shake gently. Administer the dose and rinse with 2 mL of water.

• Lansoprazole 3 mg/mL oral solution with sodium bicarbonate is physicochemically stable, but destruction of the enteric coating of the granules in the bicarbonate causes up to 90% loss when administered gastrically. Favor the use of fractions of Fast-tab tablets for optimal use.
• Split tablets are stable for 7 days at room temperature when stored in a dry place.
      particularity: Usual pediatric dose: 1-2 mg/Kg/day PO divided into 1-2 doses
