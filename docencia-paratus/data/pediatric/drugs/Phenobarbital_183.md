# Phenobarbital (Luminal®)
- Drug ID: 183
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Syringe pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 120 mg/mL
      administration: • Levetiracetam and Phenytoin are preferred for status epilepticus in children > 6 months.
• Phenobarbital remains a 2nd line agent for children from 1 month to 6 months, or in the context of intoxication.
• Phenobarbital is preferred in status epilepticus in infants <4 weeks old

Can be administered IM undiluted.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Maximum administration rate :  1 mg/kg/min (maximum 100 mg/min).

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 120 mg/mL
      administration: • Levetiracetam and Phenytoin are preferred for status epilepticus in children > 6 months.
• Phenobarbital remains a 2nd line agent for children from 1 month to 6 months, or in the context of intoxication.
• The use of Phenobarbital is preferred in status epilepticus in infants <4 weeks old.

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag.
2) Add the indicated dose.

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume according to the clinical situation.
