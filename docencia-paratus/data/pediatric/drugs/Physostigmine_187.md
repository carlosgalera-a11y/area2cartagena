# Physostigmine (Anticholium®)
- Drug ID: 187
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Anticholinergic delirium
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  [IV over 1 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 0.4 mg/mL
      administration: • Dose can be repeated every 20 minutes for a maximum of 2 mg in the first hour.
• Do not administer if QRS > 100 msec. 
• Do not administer in mixed intoxications or intoxication with a tricyclic agent.
• May induce bradycardia. Treat severe and symptomatic bradycardia with epinephrine rather than atropine (anticholinergic).
• Discuss with your poison control center. An infusion may be indicated in certain clinical situations. 
• Can be administered IM or subcutaneously
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. Incompatible with LR (no data)
• Antidote available in Canada only through Health Canada's Special Access Program. 
• Unlike neostigmine and pyridostigmine, physostigmine easily crosses the blood-brain barrier.

DILUTED 0.024 mg/mL solution

Further dilution for administration via pump:
Presentation: Vial of Physostigmine 0.4 mg/mL (2 mg in 5 mL).
1) Remove 15 mL from a 250 mL bag of D5% = 235 mL
2) Add 15 mL (6 mg) of Physostigmine 0.4 mg/mL to the bag
3) Final concentration = 6 mg in 250 mL = 0.024 mg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
