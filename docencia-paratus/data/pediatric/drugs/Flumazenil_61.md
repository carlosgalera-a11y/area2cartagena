# Flumazenil (Anexate® or Romazicon®)
- Drug ID: 61
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Iatrogenic benzodiazepine intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 0.1 mg/mL
      administration: Repeat every 60 seconds as needed for 5 doses total (maximum cumulative dose: 1 mg or 0.05 mg/kg).

Relapses in sedation may occur approximately 90 minutes after the first administration.
Repeat bolus or start IV infusion as needed.

Contraindications:
- Coma of unknown origin
- Co-ingestion of another toxic substance
- Epilepsy
- Benzodiazepine dependence
- ECG compatible with sodium channel blocker intoxication
- Intubated patient

 Further dilution for administration via pump:
Presentation: Vial of Flumazenil 0,1 mg/mL (0.5 mg in 5 mL or 1 mg in 10 mL)
1) Remove 20 mL from a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% = 80 mL
2) Add 20 mL (2 mg = 4 vials) of Flumazenil 0.1 mg/mL to the bag
3) Final concentration = 2 mg in 100 mL = 0.02 mg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration 
5) Administer preferably through a proximal peripheral IV access to minimize pain during injection
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5%, NaCl, LR.
