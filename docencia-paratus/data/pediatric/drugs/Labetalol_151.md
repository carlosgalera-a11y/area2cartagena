# Labetalol (Trandate®)
- Drug ID: 151
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Targeted BP control (bolus)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Do not give faster than 0.1 mg/Kg/min or 10 mg/min.
      particularity: Children, especially at a young age, are more sensitive to the hemodynamic effects of beta-blockers because their cardiac output is more dependent on their heart rate than adults. For this reason, labetalol is used on expert advice, and the use of continuous infusion is rarely used in pediatric practice. (demi-vie = 5-8 hours)

Contraindications: Uncompensated chronic heart failure, cardiogenic shock, bradycardia ou heart block, asthma (uncontrolled)

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
The maximal hypotensive effect occurs 5-15 min after administration.
Single dose can be doubled to a maximum of 1 mg/Kg/dose or 80 mg/dose if lack of clinical response.
