# Acyclovir (Zovirax®)
- Drug ID: 86
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: HSV/VZV contact in infant with fever of unknown origin (< 6 weeks)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Empiric coverage of HSV/VZV, without lesions, in the child > 6 weeks post-contact is not necessary unless there is clinical suspicion of viral encephalitis.

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose
3) Administer at the indicated rate

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Maintain adequate hydration during administration; dehydration increases the risk of crystallization in the renal tubules.

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosing: 60 mg/Kg/day divided q8h excluding premature babies and patients with reduced renal function.

## Indicación: Viral encephalitis (≥ 1 year, dose in m2)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 7

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: For patients ≥ 12 years old, Acyclovir 15 mg/Kg/dose = 500 mg/m2/dose.

Consider that the use of a bag of 50-100-250 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Maintain adequate hydration during administration; dehydration increases the risk of crystallization in the kidney tubules.

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosing: 1500 mg/m2/day divided q8h for patients > 1 year excluding patients with decreased renal function.
Dosage adjustment in renal failure.
