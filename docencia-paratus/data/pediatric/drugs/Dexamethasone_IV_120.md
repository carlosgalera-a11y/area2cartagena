# Dexamethasone IV (Decadron®)
- Drug ID: 120
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: COVID + with hypoxemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Nil
      particularity: Recommended dose: 0,15 mg/Kg, max 6 mg daily for 10 days (RECOVERY study). Pediatric doses are extrapolated from adult doses in the RECOVERY study.

Reduced mortality in patients requiring oxygen therapy or invasive or non-invasive mechanical ventilation.

Use with Tocilizumab if possible. If Tocilizumab is not available, consider higher doses of dexamethasone (0,6 mg/Kg, max 20 mg daily).

## Indicación: Acute severe asthma
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Asthma in patients < 9 months of life is very rare.
      particularity: Consider a second dose 24h after the first dose (IV or PO).
The effectiveness of dexamethasone PO is equivalent to IV. (Bioavailability 100%)
Equivalent to 3-5 days of treatment with prednisone/prednisolone.
