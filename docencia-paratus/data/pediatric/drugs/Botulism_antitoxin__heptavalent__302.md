# Botulism antitoxin (heptavalent) (BAT®)
- Drug ID: 302
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Botulism
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [Add to 25 mL of NaCl 0.9% then start IV at 1.5 mL/h]
      concentration note: Vial with variable volume (10-26 mL)
      administration: • Monitor closely for allergic reaction; delayed allergic reaction or serum sickness can occur 10 to 21 days after the administration.
• A history of allergic reaction to equine proteins does not constitute a contraindication; consider administration of pre-medication and have epinephrine 1 mg/mL at the bedside.
• Do not shake
• It is recommended to take samples for toxin detection before giving treatment, but do not wait for confirmation of toxins to begin treatment.
      particularity: • Keep frozen. If thawed, keep refrigerated (stable 36 months after thawing or 48 months from date of manufacture).
• Stable for 1 hour at room temperature
• Y-site compatible with NaCl. No compatibility data with other solutions or other medications.
