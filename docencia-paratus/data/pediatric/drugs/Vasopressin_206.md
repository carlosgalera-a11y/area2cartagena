# Vasopressin (Pressyn®)
- Drug ID: 206
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Refractory anaphylaxis (Bolus)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  [IV push q1-5min PRN]  Administer IV DILUTED directly or via pump
      concentration: 200 milliunités/mL
      concentration note: Same concentration for bolus AND infusion
      administration: Caution:
• For crashing anaphylaxis refractory to epinephrine IV with risk of arresting within the next minutes. Use the infusion without bolus if patient isn't pre-arrest.
• 1 milliunit = 0.001 unit
• Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

Concentration 200 milliunits/mL:

Further dilution for administration via syringe pump:
Presentation: Vial of Vasopressin 20 000 milliunits/mL (20 units/mL) (20 000 milliunits in 1 mL).
1) Withdraw 0.5 mL (1/2 vial) of Vasopressin at 20 000 milliunits/mL with a 50 mL syringe
2) Add 49.5 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe.
3) Final concentration = 10 000 milliunits in 50 mL = 200 milliunits/mL.
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration or draw with syringe.
      particularity: The use of vasopressin bolus (push dose vasopressin) comes from limited anesthesia litterature on anaphylaxis.
      doses:

## Indicación: Shock refractory to vasoconstrictors
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 20000 milliunités/mL
      administration: Caution:
• 1 milliunit = 0,001 unit
• 1 milliunit/Kg/min = 0,001 unit/Kg/min = 60 milliunits/Kg/h 
• Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

Suggested titration:
Increase in steps of 0.1-0.2 milliunits/Kg/min q15min depending on the desired BP target (Expert opinion)

Additional information:
• Do NOT remove volume during preparation
• Rinse tubing with the solution before administration
      particularity: Risk of tissue necrosis and mesenteric ischemia, especially with doses > 4 milliunits/Kg/min or 40 milliunits/min; increased monitoring. (Expert opinion)

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
      doses:
