# Amiodarone (Cordarone®)
- Drug ID: 59
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: VT with pulse/SVT
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV DILUTED
      concentration: 1.74 mg/mL
      administration: DILUTED 1.74 mg/mL solution

Further dilution for administration via pump:
Presentation: Vial of Amiodarone 50 mg/mL (150 mg in 3 mL).
1) Use a 250 mL bag of D5% WITHOUT DEHP
2) Add 9 mL (3 vials) of Amiodarone 50 mg/mL to the bag, WITHOUT removing volume
3) Final concentration = 450 mg in 259 mL = 1.74 mg/mL
4) Install a 0.22 micron filter at the end of the tubing
5) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
6) For a dose of 5 mg/Kg, administer 2.9 mL/Kg of the solution diluted to 1,74 mg/mL over 60 min.

Additional information:
• Except in cardiac arrest, the administration of amiodarone over < 20 min increases the risk of severe hypotension and hepatotoxicity which can be fatal.
• Can be administered over 30 min in patients > 30 kg.
• The use of bags without DEHP is recommended to reduce the release of plasticizers. For this reason, administration via syringe pump is not advised, as syringes are typically made of PVC.
• Solution diluted to 6 mg/mL is possible, but requires a central venous access.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

Repeatable bolus up to a maximum of 15 mg/Kg (3 boluses)
Do not use in case of doubt for Torsades de Pointes and assess risks/benefits with cardiology if QTc> 500 msec.

  - STEP: Infusion
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Caution: 
• Use 250 mL bag of D5% WITHOUT DEHP for preparation of the infusion.
• Administer with a 0.22 micron filter

Further dilution for administration via pump:
1) Prepare a DILUTED 1.74 mg/mL solution
2) Do NOT remove volume during preparation
3) Install a 0.22 micron filter at the end of the tubing
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
5) After the 5 mg/Kg bolus, start the continuous infusion at 5-15 mcg/Kg/min.

Additional information:
• The use of bags without DEHP is recommended to reduce the release of plasticizers. For this reason, administration via syringe pump is not advised, as syringes are typically made of PVC.
• If central venous access not available, can be administered via proximal peripheral venous access of good caliber.
• Solution diluted to 6 mg/mL are possible, but requires a central venous access.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

Repeatable bolus up to a maximum of 15 mg/Kg (3 boluses)
Do not use in case of doubt for Torsades de Pointes and assess risks/benefits with cardiology if QTc> 500 msec.

## Indicación: Pulseless VT/VF
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: According to PALS/ACLS, repeat dose every 2 rhythm analysis/pulse checks/shocks (q4min) up to a maximum of 3 doses or 450 mg.

The 0.22 micron filter can be omitted in cardiac arrest.
      particularity: Nil
