# Doxycycline PO (Vibramycin®)
- Drug ID: 273
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Lyme's disease
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Lyme's disease (BID)  [PO]  BID
      administration: • Use doxycycline tablets or tablet fractions of 100 mg if compound oral solution not available.
• There is a compound oral solution of doxycycline 5 mg/mL
• Administer with food to reduce gastric irritation, but avoid dairy products (decreased absorption).
• Duration of the treatment:• Isolated erythema migrans without systemic symptoms: 10 days
• Systemic involvement excluding meningitis or central nervous system involvement: 14 days
• Lyme's arthritis or recurrence: 28 days

• If cardiac involvement (except if 1st AV block with PR < 300 msec) or if central nervous system involvement, use ceftriaxone.
