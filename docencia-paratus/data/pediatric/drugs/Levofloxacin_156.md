# Levofloxacin (Levaquin®)
- Drug ID: 156
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Severe infection  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Levofloxacin is not a first choice in pediatrics in the empiric treatment of community or hospital-acquired infections or Pseudomonas infections. Consult an infectious disease specialist before initiating.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Administration to immature animals has been associated with arthropathies, which has limited its use in neonatology, pediatrics and obstetrics; arthropathy has not been reported to date in children treated with fluoroquinolones, but clinical follow-up remains limited; muscular, peripheral nervous or central adverse reactions and tendinopathies have been reported in the pediatric and adult population.

Usual dosage: 
6 months to 5 years: 10 mg/Kg/dose IV q12h
> 5 years old: 10 mg/Kg/dose, max 750 mg IV q24h 
excluding patients with reduced renal function.
