# Nicardipine (Cardene®)
- Drug ID: 174
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Premixed bag)
      concentration: 200 mcg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag at 40 mg/200 mL NaCl 0.9% (200 mcg/mL)
      administration: Suggested titration:
1) Start the infusion at 0,5-1 mcg/Kg/min.
2) Titrate by 0.25-0.5 mcg/Kg/min every 5-15 minutes until target BP is achieved (onset of action 5-10 min; rapid titration q5min or gradual titration q15min).
3) Maximum dose of 5 mcg/Kg/min or 250 mcg/min, i.e. 15 mg/h.
*** Caution: BP can drop precipitously after the initial titration ***
4) If titration q5-15min, once target BP is achieved, decrease the infusion by half, then titrate again gradually as needed.
5) If BP falls below target, decrease the infusion to minimum rate or hold entirely, then titrate again gradually as needed.  (Expert opinion)

• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5%, NaCl, LR. 
• Protect solution from light at all times

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: If premixed bag not available: 

Presentation: Vial of Nicardipine 1 mg/mL (10 mg in 10 mL)
1) Remove 20 mL from a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% = 80 mL
2) Add 20 mL (2 vials) of Nicardipine 1 mg/mL to the bag
3) Final concentration = 20 mg in 100 mL = 0.2 mg/mL = 200 mcg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
