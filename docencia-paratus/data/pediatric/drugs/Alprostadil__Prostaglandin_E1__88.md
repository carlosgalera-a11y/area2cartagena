# Alprostadil (Prostaglandin E1) (Prostin®)
- Drug ID: 88
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Duct dependent congenital heart disease
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 0.5 mg/mL
      administration: Suggested titration:
1) Start the infusion at 0.03-0.05 mcg/Kg/min.
2) Titrate by increments of 0.03-0.05 mcg/Kg/min according to the O2 saturation and/or arterial pressure objectives discussed with the experts, according to the suspected cardiopathy, to open or maintain the ductus arteriosus.
3) Once the ductus arteriosus is open and the patient is hemodynamically stabilized, titrate to obtain the minimum effective dose and minimize side effects. (e.g.: Decrease to 0.05 mcg/Kg/min then 0.02 mcg/Kg/min q6h ad 0.01 mcg/Kg/min according to echocardiography) (Expert opinion)

Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
If patient with reduced dextrose intake, very at risk of hypoglycaemia or with a history of hypoglycaemia, dilute in D10% rather than D5%.
Keep refrigerated.

Monitor for hypotension, apnea and fever.
