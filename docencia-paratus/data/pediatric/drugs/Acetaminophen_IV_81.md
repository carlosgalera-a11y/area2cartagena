# Acetaminophen IV (Tylenol® or Ofirmev®)
- Drug ID: 81
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Analgesia (IV)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 15 min]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Risk of hypotension.
Administer the commercial IV 10 mg/mL solution directly without rediluting.
Protect from light.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Relative contraindication in severe active liver disease or severe liver failure.
Usual dosage: 15 mg/Kg/dose, max 1000 mg/dose IV q6h excluding patients <1 month and premature and patients with decreased renal function.

Avir Pharma's Canadian formulation contains povidone, an inactive compound removed by glomerular filtration. Although the potential for accumulation of povidone is unknown, there is a possibility in patients < 1 month of age (including premature infants), as well as in patients with severe renal impairment, where glomerular filtration is reduced. The consequences of this accumulation remain poorly defined for these populations. For repeat doses, carefully weigh the risks vs benefits.
