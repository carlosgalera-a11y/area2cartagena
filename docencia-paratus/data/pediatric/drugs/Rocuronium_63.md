# Rocuronium (Zemuron®)
- Drug ID: 63
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Intubation- Neuromuscular Blocker
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Rapide sequence intubation  [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: For emergency reversal of rocuronium, use Sugammadex 16 mg/Kg.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Stable at room temperature for 6 months.

## Indicación: Neuromuscular Blocker (miscellaneous/non-intubation)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: • Ensure adequate analgo-sedation is in place before administration to prevent a paralyzed patient not receiving analgesia and sedation. 
• The rocuronium dose of 1.5 mg/Kg has not been studied in pediatrics; if the patient is not adequately paralyzed after a first dose, repeat the dose after 90 seconds. 
• If repeated doses or rocuronium, monitor the effect of rocuronium with Train-of-four and the level of analgosedation with a bispectral index (BIS) or other type of electroencephalographic monitoring if available.
• For emergency reversal of rocuronium, use Sugammadex 16 mg/Kg
• Provide passive eyelid closure and eye lubrication for prevention corneal abrasions
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Stable at room temperature for 6 months.
