# Heparin (Unfractionnated; UFH)
- Drug ID: 243
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Therapeutic systemic anticoagulation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV undiluted on volume/time pump
      concentration: 100 unités/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: • Use the premixed heparin bags to administer the bolus; do NOT use concentrated vials at 1000 units/mL or more.
• Administer the bolus after validation with a pediatric expert, if therapeutic anticoagulation is required within the first hour.

• Usual bolus: 75 units/Kg
• Bolus in patient with significant risk of bleeding: 50 units/Kg
• High dose bolus (congenital heart surgery like obstructed BT-shunt or ECMO-VA): 100 units/Kg

• Usual therapeutic target: aPTT 50-70 sec or anti-Xa 0.35-0.7 units/mL
Favor, if available, adjustment according to anti-Xa, because better correlation with heparin levels with less interference from hemolysis, warfarin or liver dysfunction and less variations according to the reagent used in the laboratory.
• Confirm the therapeutic target with the team, ideally before initiating therapy.

• If life-threatening bleeding on heparin, reverse heparin with protamine
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR, KCl.
