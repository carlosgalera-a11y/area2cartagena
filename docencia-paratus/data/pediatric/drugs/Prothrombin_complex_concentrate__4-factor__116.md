# Prothrombin complex concentrate (4-factor) (Beriplex® or Kcentra®)
- Drug ID: 116
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: DOAC reversal in life-threatening bleeding
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer undiluted after reconstitution on volume/time pump
      concentration: 25 unités/mL
      administration: • Pediatric doses of Prothrombin Complex Concentrate (4-Factor) for the reversal of direct oral anticoagulants (including dabigatran if idarucizumab not available) are poorly described in the literature and are mostly extrapolated from adult literature. (Expert opinion)
• In non-life threatening bleeding, consult Pediatric Hematology for management.

Reconstitution:

Presentation: vial of PCC, provided vial of diluent (sterile water for injection) and Mix2Vial® transfer device.
Refer to the monograph for detailed reconstitution.
1) Mix the PCC vial with the diluent using the device provided. DO NOT shake the bottle.
2) Gently swirl the bottle to make sure the product is completely dissolved
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 25 units/mL
      particularity: Do NOT administer in Y with D5%, NaCl, LR or blood products.

Contraindications:
- Prior heparin-induced thrombocytopenia (HIT)
- Heparin allergy

## Indicación: Vitamin K antagonist (VKA) reversal in life-threatening bleeding
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Unkown INR OR 1.6-2  Administer undiluted after reconstitution on volume/time pump
      concentration: 25 unités/mL
      administration: • Must be administered with IV Vitamin K in case of major bleeding.
• Pediatric doses of Prothrombin Complex Concentrate (4-Factor) for the reversal of anti-vitamin K anticoagulants are poorly described in the literature and are mostly extrapolated from adult literature. (Expert opinion)
• In non-life threatening bleeding, consult Pediatric Hematology for management.
• Reversal with Prothrombin Complex Concentrate (4-Factor) is faster and causes less fluid overload than fresh frozen plasma (FFP)
