# Lacosamide (Vimpat®)
- Drug ID: 152
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Syringe pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% by syringe pump
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Despite its favorable side effect profile, lacosamide is not one of the agents traditionally used routinely. Consider when patient chronically under maximum dose of other agents or when intolerance/allergy to these agents. (Levetiracetam/Phenytoin)
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Avoid in patients with symptomatic bradycardia or with 2nd or 3rd degree AV block.

Subsequent doses of 1-8 mg/Kg (max adult 200 mg) IV q12h should be considered depending on the clinical outcome. These doses can also be administered orally for the bioavailability close to 100%. Possible adjustment in renal insufficiency, severe hepatic insufficiency or in patients on drugs inhibitor or highly metabolized by CYP 450 3A4 or 2C9.
