# Clarithromycine (Biaxin®)
- Drug ID: 263
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Atypical bacterial infections (BID)  [PO]  BID
      concentration: 25 mg/mL
      administration: • Drug interactions via CYP 450 3A4 resulting in accumulation of the other drug (weaker substrate)
• For the treatment of B pertussis or Ureaplasma in infants aged < 1 month, use azithromycin instead of clarithromycin. Azithromycin has not been associated with infantile hypertrophic pyloric stenosis.
• Duration of treatment : 7 days
• Consider adding atypical coverage with a macrolide to amoxicillin or amoxicillin/clavulanic acid based on chest X-ray (bilateral focal or interstitial infiltrates) in and if patient or sibling attends school or daycare.
      particularity: Do not refrigerate
