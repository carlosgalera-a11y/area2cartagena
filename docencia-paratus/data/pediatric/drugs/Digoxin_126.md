# Digoxin (Lanoxin®)
- Drug ID: 126
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Atrial fibrillation (AF)/Supraventricular Tachycardia (SVT)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV diluted up to 10 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 0.25 mg/mL
      administration: IV digoxin is not the first choice for treatment of pediatric tachyarrhythmias in general.

IV digoxin is not a first choice in children due to the lack of a pediatric IV formulation. Administer solely under the recommendation of a pediatric cardiologist with expertise in electrophysiology.

The 0.05 mg/mL or 50 mcg/mL pediatric IV formulation is discontinued in Canada; use the 0.25 mg/mL adult IV formulation for adolescents.

Choose an initial bolus of 0.25 mg or 0.5 mg.
      particularity: After the initial bolus, repeat 0.25mg IV q4-6h for a maximum of 1.5mg total for the first 24h. Then, initiate the maintenance dose as clinically indicated.
