# Haloperidol (Haldol®)
- Drug ID: 141
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe agitation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IM  [IM in < 10 seconds]  Administer IM undiluted in the thigh
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: • Extrapyramidal effects with haloperidol are more common in children (Dose dependent). The combination with diphenhydramine to prevent extrapyramidal effects does not clearly reduce its incidence according to the literature, but increases the risk of respiratory depression and lengthens the stay in the emergency room. Unless contraindicated, favor atypical antipsychotics such as olanzapine.
• Repeat as needed up to a maximum cumulative dose of 0.1-0.25 mg/Kg/day or 10 mg/day
• The 5 mg/mL lactate solution can be injected IV.  Do not use the long-acting 50 or 100 mg/mL decanoate solution IV.
      particularity: • In the presence of extrapyramidal effects, treat with diphenhydramine.
• Be careful if combined with another QT prolonging drug or in the presence of electrolyte disorders.
• Depending on the clinical situation, consider the concomitant use of a benzodiazepine. Decrease the dose accordingly: increased risk of respiratory depression.
