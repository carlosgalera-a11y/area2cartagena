# Dantrolene (Dantrium®)
- Drug ID: 117
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Malignant hyperthermia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Maintenance dose  [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 0.33 mg/mL
      administration: Contact your poison control center

Administer preferably through a proximal IV access such as an elbow crease or a central access. (Solution with pH at 9.5)

Diluting and preparing dantrolene can be laborious. This may require extra help and having someone dedicated do the preparation if needed.

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Dantrolene 20 mg
1) Add 60 mL of sterile water
2) Shake the vial until the solution is clear.
3) Concentration after reconstitution =  0.33 mg/mL.
4) Draw the indicated dose and administer IV push undiluted as soon as possible.

This concentration is the only one available on the canadian market and involves a significant quantity of liquid.
      particularity: Incompatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Continue the treatment q6h.

Consult your poison control center for discontinuation of therapy.
