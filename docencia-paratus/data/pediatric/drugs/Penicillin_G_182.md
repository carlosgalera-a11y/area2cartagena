# Penicillin G (Crystapen®)
- Drug ID: 182
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Streptococcus infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Non-meningeal streptococcus
      concentration: 0.25 M unités/mL
      administration: Caution:
Dose in million units (1 M units = 1 000 000 units)

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Penicillin 1 M units
1) Add 3.8 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 0.25 M units/mL
3) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%

Presentation: Vial of Penicillin 5 M units
1) Add 8.2 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 0.5 M units/mL
3) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%

Additional information:
• If different presentation, reconstitute according to the manufacturer's recommendations. 
• The maximum concentration for administration, after secondary dilution, is 0.15 M units/mL (150 000 units/mL).
• IV administration over 15 minutes or more.
• Can be administered IM.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

The first dose of an anti-infective medication should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosing: 0,15-0,4 M units/Kg/day divided q4-6h up to a maximum of 24 M units/day divided q4-6h excluding patients <1 month and premature babies and patients with reduced renal function.
