# Hydroxyzine PO (Atarax® PO)
- Drug ID: 257
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Pruritus secondary to urticaria
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: > 6 months old  [PO]  q6h PRN
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: • Use cetirizine rather than diphenhydramine PO or hydroxyzine to avoid drowsiness and paradoxical reaction. Do not combine anti-H1 drugs; cetirizine is the active, non-drowsy metabolite of hydroxyzine. 
• For anxiolysis or procedural sedation, use ketamine, midazolam or dexmedetomidine instead.
