# Cefuroxime (Zinacef®)
- Drug ID: 108
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 3-5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      administration: Cefuroxime is not a first choice of treatment. It is a possible option when amoxicillin/clavulanic acid or 3rd generation cephalosporins (ceftrixaone or cefotaxime) are not possible options.

Reconstitute according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
IV administration over 3-5 minutes is safe according to the literature. 
It is possible to administer the drug over a longer period according to the hospital protocols in place.
Secondary dilution not necessary.
Can be administered IM.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosing: 150 mg/Kg/day divided q8h up to 1.5g IV q8h (maximum adult dose for severe infection: 1.5g IV q6h) excluding patients <1 month and premature babies and patients with reduced renal function.
Dosage adjustment in renal failure.
