# Ascorbic acid (Vitamin C)
- Drug ID: 83
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Methemoglobinemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 500 mg/mL
      administration: • For patients with severe methemoglobinemia with a contraindication to methylene blue (e.g. suspected or documented G6PD deficiency), discuss with your local poison control center for extracorporal therapy. Consider ascorbic acid for mild cases (methemoglobinemia < 15%) that don't need rapidly acting therapy.
• Usual interval: every 6 hours for up to 4 days or until methemoglobin levels normalize.

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
• The doses studied vary widely in the literature. (5-300 mg/Kg/day)
• Can be administered IM undiluted.
• Protect from light.
