# Hydromorphone (Dilaudid®)
- Drug ID: 215
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Use the minimum required dose for adequate analgesia and repeat as needed.
      particularity: Maximum effect after 10 to 20 minutes
Hydromorphone 1 mg IV = Morphine 5 mg IV
Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

## Indicación: Post-intubation analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: • For initial titration (patient on continuous infusion of hydromorphone or q3h), repeat every 5-10 minutes as needed. 
• If hydromorphone IV q3h regular or PRN, start at 0.005 or 0.01 mg/Kg/dose.
• If breakthrough dose of IV hydromorphone (q1h PRN), administer 1/2 the dose of hydromorphone q3h or the same dose as the hourly dose of the continuous IV infusion. (e.g.: IV hydromorphone at 0.01 mg/Kg/h => breakthrough dose of IV hydromorphone at 0.01 mg/Kg q1h PRN)
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
• Hydromorphone 1 mg IV = Morphine 5 mg IV 
• Use the minimum required dose for adequate analgesia. 
• If possible, adjust analgesia according to validated tools in pediatrics. (numeric rating scale/FACES, FLACC, COMFORT-B)
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0.25]
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0.5]
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=1]

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration. 

1) Start the infusion at a rate of 0.004-0.01 mg/Kg/h
2) Titrate by increments of 0.002 mg/Kg/h q1h until the desired level of analgesia is achieved. (Expert opinion) 

• Before starting the continuous infusion, consider an initial titration with IV hydromorphone boluses repeated every 5-10 minutes as needed. 
• If breakthrough dose of IV hydromorphone (q1h PRN), administer 1/2 the dose of hydromorphone q3h or the same dose as the hourly dose of the continuous IV infusion. (e.g.: IV hydromorphone at 0.01 mg/Kg/h => breakthrough dose of IV hydromorphone at 0.01 mg/Kg q1h PRN)
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
