# Diltiazem (Cardizem®)
- Drug ID: 127
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: SVT (Bolus)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1st Dose  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Avoid if hypotension, hemodynamic instability or heart failure with LVeF < 40%

Suggested titration:
1) 1st dose 0.25 mg/Kg (Max 20 mg)
2) If ineffective after 15 min, administer a 2nd dose of 0.35 mg/Kg (Max 25 mg)
3) If ineffective after 15 min, administer a 3rd dose of 0.35 mg/Kg (Max 25 mg)
4) If no clinical response, consider another molecule
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. No compatibility data with LR.

Keep refrigerated
