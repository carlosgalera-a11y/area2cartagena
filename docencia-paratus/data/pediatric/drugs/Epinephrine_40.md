# Epinephrine (Adrenalin®)
- Drug ID: 40
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis (IM)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IM  [IM in < 10 seconds]  Administer IM undiluted in the thigh
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: Repeat IM injection every 5 minutes if needed x 2-3 doses then switch to the IV infusion if symptoms persist.
If patient is unconscious and requiring assisted ventilation secondary to anaphylaxis, favor IV epinephrine - push dose epinephrine (See EZResus Pediatric).

Dose of 0.1 mg can be used for weights of 3-9 Kg if 1 mL syringe not available.
Dose of 0.15 mg acceptable for patient <15 Kg if auto-injector is the only epinephrine available.
      particularity: Avoid 1:1000 nomenclature as it is associated with more medication errors.

## Indicación: Push-Dose pressor
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push q1-5min PRN]  Administer IV DILUTED
      concentration: 10 mcg/mL
      administration: Presentation: Pre-filled Syringe of Epinephrine 0.1 mg/mL (100 mcg/mL)
1) Withdraw 1 mL of Epinephrine 100 mcg/mL with a 10 mL syringe
2) Add 9 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 100 mcg dans 10 mL = 10 mcg/mL
4) Draw the required dose using a syringe
5) Clearly identify syringes with the correct concentration
6) Administer IV push q1-5min PRN to a target blood pressure or heart rate
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

## Indicación: Acute severe asthma
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: Caution: Several concentrations at 16, 20 and 40 mcg/mL pending standardization according to ASHP standards.

Use if IV salbutamol is not available or if the patient remains symptomatic despite high dose of IV salbutamol.
 
Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 0.02-0.1 mcg/kg/min.
2) Titrate by increments of 0.01-0.02 mcg/kg/min q5min depending on clinical response. (Expert Opinion)

Additional information:
Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
• Use the lowest concentration if a peripheral venous access is used.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR, MgSO4.
Avoid 1:1000 nomenclature as it is associated with more medication errors.

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

## Indicación: Cardiogenic and distributive shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: Caution: Several concentrations at 16, 20 and 40 mcg/mL pending standardization according to ASHP standards.

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 0.05 mcg/kg/min
2) Titrate by increments of 0.01-0.02 mcg/kg/min q5min depending on clinical response. (Expert Opinion)

Additional information:
• Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
• In cardiogenic shock, the reported doses are 0.02-0.1 mcg/Kg/min. In distributive septic shock, the reported doses are 0.05-1 mcg/Kg/min. In practice, the addition of the vasoconstrictor effect (alpha) to the inotropic effect (beta) is not precise and absolute.
• Use the lowest concentration if a peripheral venous access is used.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Avoid 1:1000 nomenclature as it is associated with more medication errors.

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.
