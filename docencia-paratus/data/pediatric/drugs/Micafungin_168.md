# Micafungin (Mycamine®)
- Drug ID: 168
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Invasive candidemia (Less than 4 months)
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: DILUTED solution at 1 mg/mL:

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Micafungin 100 mg
1) Add 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Shake gently, avoiding foaming the solution
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 20 mg/mL

Further dilution for administration via pump:
1) Draw 5 mL of DILUTED Micafungin at 20 mg/mL
2) Withdraw 5 mL from a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% or D5W
3) Add 5 mL of DILUTED Micafungin to the 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% or D5W
4) Final concentration after further dilution = 1 mg/mL
5) Administer the indicated dose via pump (Volume/time) or draw the indicated dose and administer via syringe pump. 

Additional information:
DILUTED 1 mg/mL solution favored, because solution > 1.5 mg/mL requires central venous access
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Usual dosage for < 4 months of life: 2-10 mg/Kg IV q24h based on indication.
No adjustment required in renal failure.
