# Isoproterenol (Isuprel®)
- Drug ID: 150
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Bradyarrhythmia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 0.2 mg/mL
      administration: Additional information:
• If central venous access is not available, may be administered via proximal peripheral venous access.
• Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
      particularity: If bradycardia with pulse or high-grade AV block with hemodynamic instability, favor epinephrine by infusion and pacing as first-line. (PALS 2020)
Risk of hypotension

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
