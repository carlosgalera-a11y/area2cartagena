# Ipratropium (Atrovent®)
- Drug ID: 148
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Acute Severe Asthma
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Inhalation  [q20min x 3 doses]
      concentration: 20 mcg/inhalation
      administration: Asthma in patients < 9 months of life is very rare.

Use Aerochamber® for optimal administration.

If the patient is on CPAP or BiPAP, favor nebulized administration to allow the patient to retain the benefits of PEEP.
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
        • 4 inhalations (80 mcg) [weight_min=2, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 8 inhalations (160 mcg) [weight_min=30, weight=0, min=0, max=0]

  - STEP: Nebulization  [q20min x 3 doses]  Administer by nebulizer diluted up to 3-5 mL of 0.9% NaCl
      concentration: 125 mcg/mL
      administration: Caution: 
• Asthma in patients < 9 months of life is very rare.

Additional information:
• Nebulize with an O2 flow rate of 6-8 L/min when possible.
• If the patient is on CPAP or BiPAP, favor nebulized administration to allow the patient to retain the benefits of PEEP.
• Administer q30-60min if clinical improvement after 3 treatments q20min.
