- Weight type: 1

      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: The 500 mcg/kg bolus is not generally used in pediatrics as children are more susceptible to the hemodynamic effects of beta-blockers.

## Indicación: TET spells in Tetralogy of Fallot
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Use after fluid bolus and phenylephrine in the Tetralogy of Fallot patient with hypoxia and tachycardia.

The 500 mcg/kg bolus is not generally used in pediatrics as children are more susceptible to the hemodynamic effects of beta-blockers.

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 50 mcg/kg/min
2) Titrate infusion by 25 mcg/kg/min q5min as needed (Expert opinion)

Additional information:
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
      particularity: The decrease in heart rate as well as infundibular relaxation due to beta-blocker reduces the right-left shunt and improves pulmonary blood flow in Tetralogy of Fallot.

Contraindications: uncompensated chronic heart failure, cardiogenic shock, bradycardia or heart block, asthma (uncontrolled)

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Pediatric Intensive Care opinion recommended for children under 6 years old. For patients < 1 year, use another agent such as Nicardipine or Nitroprusside.

The 500 mcg/kg bolus is not generally used in pediatrics as children are more susceptible to the hemodynamic effects of beta-blockers.

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 50 mcg/kg/min
2) Titrate infusion by 25 mcg/kg/min q5min as needed (Expert opinion)

Additional information:
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
      particularity: Children, especially at a young age, are more sensitive to the hemodynamic effects of beta-blockers because their cardiac output is more dependent on their heart rate than adults. For this reason, esmolol is used only with expert advice.

Contraindications: uncompensated chronic heart failure, cardiogenic shock, bradycardia or heart block, asthma (uncontrolled)

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

Short half-life (9 min)
