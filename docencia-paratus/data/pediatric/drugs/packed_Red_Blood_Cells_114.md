# packed Red Blood Cells (pRBC)
- Drug ID: 114
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Massive hemorrhage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Rate of administration varies depending on the clinical indication
      administration: Additional information:
• Repeat according to clinical response
• In early massive hemorrhage, favor transfusing blood products at a ratio of 1:1:1 (Plasma: Platelets: RBCs)
• The sodium citrate in pRBC lowers calcium; monitor ionized/mesured calcium if repeated administration of pRBC
• If low Hb AND absence of bleeding, opinion of pediatric hematology or pediatric intensive care recommended
• 1 PRBC unit = approximately 320 mL
• May be administered over 3 hours if patient is stable
