# Dexmedetomidine (Precedex®)
- Drug ID: 121
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Sedation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted directly from the vial
      concentration: 4 mcg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial vial at 4 mcg/mL
      administration: Caution:
• Pediatric Critical Care opinion recommended
• Do not bolus, risk of severe bradycardia
 
If 4 mcg/mL commercial premixed vial not available: 
DILUTED solution at 4 mcg/mL:

 Further dilution for administration via pump:
Presentation: Vial of Dexmedetomidine 100 mcg/mL (200 mcg in 2 mL) 
1) Remove 2 mL from a 50 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% = 48 mL
2) Add 2 mL (1 vial) of Dexmedetomidine 100 mcg/mL to the bag
3) Final concentration = 200 mcg in 50 mL = 4 mcg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration 

Further dilution for administration via syringe pump:
Presentation: Vial of Dexmedetomidine 100 mcg/mL (200 mcg in 2 mL)
1) Withdraw 2 mL (1 vial) of Dexmedetomidine 100 mcg/mL with a 50 mL syringe
2) Add 48 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 200 mcg in 50 mL = 4 mcg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Monitor for bradycardia and hypotension
Dose up to 3 mcg/Kg/h possible under expert advice and continuous hemodynamic monitoring
