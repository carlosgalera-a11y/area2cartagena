# Famotidine (Pepcid®)
- Drug ID: 133
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: The documented daily dose for patients < 3 months old is usually less (0.25 mg/Kg) in the context of repeated administration (e.g. stress ulcer prophylaxis), because elimination is slower in this population. The administration of a single dose of 0.5 mg/Kg for anaphylaxis is safe for children < 3 months excluding newborns and premature babies.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated
