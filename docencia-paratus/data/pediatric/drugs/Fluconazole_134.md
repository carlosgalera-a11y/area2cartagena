# Fluconazole (Diflucan®)
- Drug ID: 134
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed vial
      administration: Fluconazole is not a first choice in the empiric treatment of invasive candidemia. See Caspofungin or Micafungin for empiric coverage of invasive candidemia.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
Multiple drug interactions (CYP 450 2C9, 2C19 and 3A4), increased ALT and increased QTc.

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosage: Loading dose of 12 mg/Kg/dose, max 400 mg IV followed by 6-12 mg/Kg/day, max 400 mg IV q24h, excluding patients <1 month and premature and patients with reduced renal function.
Dosage adjustment in renal failure and dialysis.
