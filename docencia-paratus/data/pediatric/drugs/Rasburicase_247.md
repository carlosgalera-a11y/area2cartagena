# Rasburicase (Fasturtec®)
- Drug ID: 247
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Tumor lysis syndrome
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer DILUTED after reconstitution
      concentration: 1.5 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:

Presentation: vial of Rasburicase 1.5 mg and provided vial of diluent (sterile water for injection)
1) Add 1 mL of sterile water
2) Mix the solution with a gentle swirl to make sure the product is completely dissolved. DO NOT shake the vial.
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 1.5 mg/mL
4) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%
      particularity: • Incompatible in Y with D5W, LR or any other solutions. Only compatible with NaCl 0.9%.
• Keep refrigerated.
• Rasburicase continues to break down uric acid after blood is drawn; make sure to put the sample on ice until analysis in the laboratory.
• Do NOT administer simultaneously with allopurinol for optimal transformation of uric acid into allantoin.
• Rasburicase only treats the systemic and renal component related to uric acid; in the presence of hyperkalaemia, hyperphosphoraemia or hypocalcaemia, treat in parallel according to the usual algorithms.
• Contraindicated if known G6PD deficiency. (methemoglobinemia)
