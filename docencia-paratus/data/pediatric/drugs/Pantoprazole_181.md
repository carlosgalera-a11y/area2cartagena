# Pantoprazole (Pantoloc®)
- Drug ID: 181
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Upper GI bleeding (bolus strategy)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  [IV over 2 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      concentration: 4 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:

Presentation: vial of Pantoprazole 40 mg 
1) Add 10 mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 4 mg/mL
3) Draw the indicated dose

Pantoprazole can also be administered diluted to 0.4 or 0.8 mg/mL IV over 15 minutes.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR, Plasmalyte, KCl. Incompatible with octreotide.
Administer maintenance dose 12h after loading dose.

In the context of the paucity of pediatric data with continuous IV infusion in the literature, the adult literature which demonstrates the non-inferiority of q12h boluses compared to continuous infusion, as well as the difficulty of quickly setting up multiple IV accesses in pediatrics, particularly in patients with hepatic impairment (routes for blood products, infusion of pantoprazole, other medications), the administration of pantoprazole as a bolus q12h is preferred.
