# Cetirizine PO (Reactine® or Zyrtec®)
- Drug ID: 227
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Pruritus secondary to anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 6 months to < 2 years  [PO]
      administration: Favor the administration of cetirizine rather than diphenhydramine so as not to alter the assessment of the state of consciousness.
For administration via NG tube, dilute the tablet in 5 mL of water.
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
        • 2.5 mg [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
