# Norepinephrine (Levophed®)
- Drug ID: 177
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Premixed bag)
      concentration: 16 mcg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag at 16 or 32 mcg/mL (250 mL D5W)
      administration: • For concentration of norepinephrine 64 mcg/mL, see other tabs (Infusion by syringe pump or by pump) for dilution; not available in premixed commercial bags at 64 mcg/mL.
• Use the lowest concentration if a peripheral venous access is used.
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration 

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 0.05 mcg/kg/min
2) Titrate by increments of 0.02 mcg/kg/min q5min depending on clinical response. (Expert Opinion)
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.

• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration
• Use the lowest concentration if a peripheral venous access is used
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
