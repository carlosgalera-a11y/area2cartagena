# Tetanus Immune Globulin (TIg) (HyperTET S/D®)
- Drug ID: 275
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IM in < 10 seconds]  Administer IM undiluted in the thigh or deltoid muscle
      administration: Validate the indication with the Decision support tool for tetanus prophylaxis:
• Wound at high risk of Clostridium tetani infection
WITH
• < 3 doses of tetanus vaccines in patient > 1 year of life
OR
• < 2 doses of tetanus vaccines in patients < 1 year of life
OR
• Immunosuppressed patient

• Do not administer tetanus vaccine and immunoglobulins in the same syringe
      particularity: Apply pressure to the injection site for a few minutes if the patient is bleeding or at risk of bleeding. The IM route is not contraindicated in urgent situations when indicated.
      doses:
        • 250 units (1 mL) [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
