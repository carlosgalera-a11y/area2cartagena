# Hydrocortisone (Solucortef®)
- Drug ID: 143
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 30-60 seconds]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution :
Presentation: Vial of Hydrocortisone 100 mg
1) Add 1.8 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 50 mg/mL

Presentation: Vial of Hydrocortisone 250 mg
1) Add 1.8 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 125 mg/mL
      particularity: There is lack of evidence for the use of steroids in the acute management of anaphylaxis. Their use should not be systematic and could be reserved for certain cases (eg bronchospasm, upper airway obstruction, shock). The main treatment remains epinephrine.

## Indicación: Septic/Adrenal crisis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 7

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Hydrocortisone Act-O-Vial 100 mg
1) Reconstitute the powder from the Act-O-Vial vial with the provided diluent
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 50 mg/mL

Presentation: Standard vial of Hydrocortisone 100 mg
1) Add 1.8 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 50 mg/mL

If different presentation, reconstitute according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
      particularity: If septic shock refractory to volume bolus and 2 inotropes/vasopressors.
If adrenal shock is suspected, make sure to draw the endocrine tests necessary for the diagnosis before administering. (Validate with Endocrinology)

Loading dose at 50-100 mg/m2 followed by 25 mg/m2 IV q6h
Hydrocortisone 50 mg/m2 ≈ Hydrocortisone 2 mg/Kg

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
