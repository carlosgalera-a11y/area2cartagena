# Azithromycin IV (Zithromax®)
- Drug ID: 94
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Atypical bacterial infections
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Less than 1 month  Administer DILUTED by pump or syringe pump
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: DILUTED solution at 1 mg/mL:

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Azithromycin 500 mg
1) Add 4.8 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 100 mg/mL

Further dilution for administration via pump or syringe pump:
1) Draw 5 mL of DILUTED Azithromycin at 100 mg/mL
2) Withdraw 5 mL from a 500 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
3) Add 5 mL of DILUTED Azithromycin to the 500 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
4) Final concentration after further dilution = 1 mg/mL
5) Administer the indicated dose via pump (Volume/time) or draw the indicated dose and administer via syringe pump.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Usual dosage: 10 mg/Kg, max 500 mg IV q24h.
No adjustment required in renal failure

Possible increase in QTc without an increase in the incidence of Torsade de Pointes.
