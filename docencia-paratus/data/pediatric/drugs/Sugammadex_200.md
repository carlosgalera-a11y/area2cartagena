# Sugammadex (Bridion®)
- Drug ID: 200
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Urgent reversal of rocuronium
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 10 seconds]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: • Does NOT replace specialist in difficult airway management
• For reversal of rocuronium and vecuronium only. Does not reverse the effect of depolarizing agents (succinylcholine) or cisatracurium.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

Adverse reactions (bradycardia, anaphylaxis)
Interferes with aPTT and PT tests without causing coagulopathy
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=2, weight=0, min=0, max=1000]
