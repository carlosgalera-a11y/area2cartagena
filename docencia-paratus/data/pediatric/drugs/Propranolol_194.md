# Propranolol (Inderal®)
- Drug ID: 194
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: TET spells in Tetralogy of Fallot
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: • Use after fluid bolus and phenylephrine in Tetralogy of Fallot patient with hypoxia and tachycardia.
• The decrease in heart rate as well as infundibular relaxation due to beta-blocker reduces the right-left shunt and improves pulmonary blood flow in the tetralogy of Fallot.

Caution:
• Administer manually or via syringe pump over 10 minutes or until control of the TET spell. 
• Avoid administration via volumetric pump (minimal volume of 25 mL), as it is too imprecise.
• Start at 0.01-0.02 mg/Kg/dose and titrate according to the clinical effect (heart rate and O2 saturation) by doubling the dose q6-8h up to a maximum dose of 0.1 mg/Kg/dose or 1 mg/dose.
      particularity: Contraindications: Uncompensated chronic heart failure, cardiogenic shock, bradycardia ou heart block, asthma (uncontrolled)

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
