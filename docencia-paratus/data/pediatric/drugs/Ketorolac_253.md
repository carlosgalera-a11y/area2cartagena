# Ketorolac (Toradol®)
- Drug ID: 253
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 1 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 30 mg/mL
      administration: • Do not use under 6 months of age, because of immaturity of platelet function and risk of closure of the arterial duct in newborns.
• Contraindicated for patients with allergy to NSAIDs, moderate to severe dehydration or shock, renal impairment, severe heart or liver impairment, active bleeding, gastrointestinal bleeding or ulceration, surgery or invasive procedure planned within 24 hours, coagulopathy, thrombocytopenia ( < 100 x 10^9/L), active varicella, anticoagulant treatment or chemotherapy in progress.
• Can be administered IM.
•  The solution for IM injection can be used safely for IV administration.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5%, NaCl, LR and KCl.
• The dose of 0.5 mg/Kg, maximum 15 mg/dose, can be repeated q6h.
• The analgesic ceiling dose in adult is around 10 to 15 mg; higher doses do not provide more analgesia and cause more side effects. The analgesic ceiling dose in pediatric is not defined.
