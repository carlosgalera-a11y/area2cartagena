# Ampicillin (Penbritine®)
- Drug ID: 92
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Non-meningeal infection  [IV over 3-5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      administration: Reconstitute according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
IV administration over 3-5 minutes is safe according to the literature.
It is possible to administer the drug over a longer period according to the hospital protocols in place.
Secondary dilution not necessary.
Can be administered IM.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosing: 200 mg/Kg/day divided q6h up to 2g IV q6h (maximum adult dose for severe infection: 2g IV q4h) excluding patients <1 month and premature babies and patients with reduced renal function.
Dosage adjustment in renal failure.
