## Indicación: Intubation - Co-Induction Agent
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Co-Induction Agent (In combination)  [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: • Midazolam is not a first choice as an induction or co-induction agent in pediatrics.
• The Canadian Pediatric Society does not recommend midazolam for intubation in newborns and favors fentanyl 3-5 mcg/Kg.
• In children, use ketamine, etomidate or propofol
• Consider another induction agent in case of hemodynamic instability
• Dose used when combined with fentanyl. If used as the only induction agent, use 0.2 mg/Kg, maximum 20 mg.
      particularity: Risk of hypotension; promote the early use of vasopressor agents (Push dose pressor such as epinephrine or phenylephrine or a temporary drip of norepinephrine) rather than the administration of fluid boluses only.

## Indicación: Severe agitation and sympathomimetic toxidrome (IV)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 30-60 seconds]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: • Use 1 mg/mL concentration
• Start at 0.02 or 0.05 mg/Kg, except in severe situations of sympathomimetic toxidrome where 0.1 mg/Kg may be appropriate. 
• Repeat q5min as needed until anxiety/agitation is controlled or a maximum cumulative dose of 0.2 mg/Kg or 20 mg. (Expert opinion)
• Risk of paradoxical reaction in preschool aged children
      particularity: • Consider reducing dose if used in combination with another agent (ex: antipsychotic): increased risk of respiratory depression.
• In the presence of a sympathomimetic toxidrome, contact your poison control center.

## Indicación: Refractory Status Epilepticus (infusion)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Premixed bag)
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag at 100 mg/100 mL NaCl 0.9% (1 mg/mL)
      administration: Suggested titration:
1) Bolus of 0.1 mg/Kg
2) Start the infusion at 0.1 mg/Kg/h
3) Additional bolus of 0.1 mg/Kg between each increase in the infusion
4) Increase the infusion by 0.1 mg/Kg/h q15min until the convulsions stop. (Expert opinion)

• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration
      particularity: Under EEG monitoring.
Have equipment and personnel ready for airway management.
