# Sotalol PO (Sotacor® PO)
- Drug ID: 270
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: SVT
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 7

  - STEP: 90 mg/m2/day (TID)  [PO]  TID
      administration: • Use sotalol tablets or tablet fractions of 80 mg if compound oral solution not available.
• There is a compound oral solution of sotalol 5 mg/mL
• Dose up to 180 mg/m2/day; the dose increase adds more class III effect (K+ blockade) to the beta-blocking effect (class II)
• 12-lead ECG for QTc measurement (max 500 msec)
• Torsade de pointes is significantly less common in pediatrics and is often associated with medications combined with electrolyte disorders.
• Adjust in renal insufficiency.
• Avoid if LVEF < 30%
