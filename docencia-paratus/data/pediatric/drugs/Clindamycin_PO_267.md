# Clindamycin PO (Dalacin® or Cleocin® PO)
- Drug ID: 267
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 30 mg/Kg/day (TID)  [PO]  TID
      concentration: 15 mg/mL
      administration: • May be indicated if group A Streptococcus pharyngitis in patients 3 years of age or older, with documented anaphylaxis to penicillins.
• Administration TID (vs. QID) facilitates compliance with treatment while maintaining efficacy according to references.
      particularity: • The incidence of symptomatic C. difficile infection (due to clindamycin or not) is very rare in the first years of life, even with prolonged hospitalization; the absence of toxin receptors would be the mechanism to explain the low incidence.
