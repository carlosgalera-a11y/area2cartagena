# Methylprednisolone (Solumedrol®)
- Drug ID: 165
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      concentration: 40 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Methylprednisolone 40 mg
1) Add 1 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 40 mg/mL

Presentation: Vial of Methylprednisolone 125 mg
1) Add 2 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 62.5 mg/mL
      particularity: • There is lack of evidence for the use of steroids in the acute management of anaphylaxis. Their use should not be systematic and could be reserved for certain cases (eg bronchospasm, upper airway obstruction, shock). The main treatment remains epinephrine.
• Solumedrol Act-o-vial 40mg is contraindicated in patients with a known or suspected hypersensitivity to cow's milk.

## Indicación: Acute severe asthma
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 40 mg/mL
      administration: • Asthma in patients < 9 months of life is very rare.

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Methylprednisolone 40 mg
1) Add 1 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 40 mg/mL

Presentation: Vial of Methylprednisolone 125 mg
1) Add 2 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 62.5 mg/mL 

• If the patient has received and tolerated a dose of dexamethasone of 0.6 mg/kg, max 16 mg, start methylprednisolone as soon as IV access established at a dose of 1 mg/kg/dose, max 40 mg.
      particularity: Solumedrol Act-o-vial 40mg is contraindicated in patients with a known or suspected hypersensitivity to cow's milk.
