# Succinylcholine (Anectine®)
- Drug ID: 52
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Intubation- Neuromuscular Blocker
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Rapid sequence intubation (< 12 years)  [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: In neonatal patients or < 3 kg, favor succinylcholine as a neuromuscular blocker in the absence of contraindications.

Absolute contraindications
• History of malignant hyperthermia
• History of pseudocholinesterase deficiency
• Significant tissue damage (Rhabdomyolysis, Severe burn > 24-48h, Abdominal sepsis >5 days)
• Neuromuscular disease
• Hyperkalemia > 5.5 mmol/L
• Renal failure
• Stroke / Spinal cord injury > 5 days - 6 months
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Stable at room temperature for 6 months.

With dose of 1.5-2 mg/Kg:
Onset of action: 15 seconds
Duration of action: 4-6 minutes
