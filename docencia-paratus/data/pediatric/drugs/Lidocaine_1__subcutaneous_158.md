# Lidocaine 1% subcutaneous (Xylocaine®)
- Drug ID: 158
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Local anesthesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Without epinephrine (≥ 6 months)
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: • Reduce the dose of subcutaneous lidocaine by half if procedure with liposomal lidocaine 4% or LET gel (lidocaine 4%/epinephrine 0.1%/tetracaine 0.5%) (expert's opinion)

• To reduce pain when injecting lidocaine:• Add 0.1 mL of 8.4% sodium bicarbonate (1 mmol/mL) to each mL of lidocaine (Mixture 1:10 to reduce pain secondary to the acid pH of lidocaine)
• Warm the solution to body temperature with your hands
• Use a small needle like 27G or 30G
