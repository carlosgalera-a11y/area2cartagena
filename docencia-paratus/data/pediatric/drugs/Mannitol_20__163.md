# Mannitol 20% (Osmitrol®)
- Drug ID: 163
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Intracranial Hypertension
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted with a 0.22 micron filter
      concentration: 0.2 g/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Additional information:
• Administer preferably via proximal peripheral venous access if central access not available (hyperosmolar solution).
• If osmotherapy is indicated to treat intracranial hypertension, favor the use of NaCl 3% as first line. Administration of mannitol is not recommended in the latest guidelines for the management of pediatric severe traumatic brain injury. (No studies with inclusion criteria)
• Premixed commercial bag at 100 g/500 mL (20% = 0.2 g/mL)
• Rinse the tubing with the solution before administration.
• Mannitol solutions are stable at room temperature, but mannitol may crystallize when exposed to temperatures < 20 degrees C. Warm the solution to at least 25 degrees C before administration.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR
• Monitor for hypotension
• Do not administer if blood osmolarity > 320 mOsm/L
• It is recommended to install a urinary catheter to follow induced diuresis.
