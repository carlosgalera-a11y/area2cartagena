# Hydroxocobalamin (Cyanokit® or Hydroxy-Cobal®)
- Drug ID: 144
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Cyanide poisoning
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted after reconstitution with the provided 15 micron filter
      concentration: 25 mg/mL
      administration: Repeat dose after 15 minutes if no improvement or partial improvement.

Presentation:
- Vial: 5 g hydroxocobalamin
- Sterile transfer device
- Cardboard box to hang the vial
- IV infusion kit with tubing and integrated 15 micron filter 

Reconsitution:
1) Prepare 4 syringes of 50 mL of NaCl 0.9%
2) Reconstitute the hydroxocobalamin vial with this 200 mL of NaCl 0.9% WITHOUT using the sterile transfer device provided 
3) Swing or flip the flask for at least 60 seconds to mix the solution (DO NOT shake)
4) Final concentration: 25 mg/mL
5) Hang the vial using the provided box
6) Use tubing and filter provided in the box
7) Administer via pump or gravity over 15 minutes

If the supplied filter is not compatible with your institution's pumps, administer by gravity or via DEHP-free tubing + 15 micron filter kit. (Do NOT delay administration. Confirm with your poison control center if in doubt)
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. LR
• Stable for 6 hours at room temperature after reconstitution 
• The vial should not be shaken as this may cause foaming and make it difficult to control reconstitution. Since the reconstituted solution is dark red, some insoluble particles may not be seen.
• If hydroxocobalamin is not available, administer sodium thiosulfate in combination with sodium nitrite.
• Interference with certain laboratory analyses using colorimetry (creatinine, bilirubin, etc.) may last 24 to 72h. In addition, blood samples can be falsely interpreted as hemolyzed.
• Alteration (lower) of carboxyhemoglobin has also been reported.
