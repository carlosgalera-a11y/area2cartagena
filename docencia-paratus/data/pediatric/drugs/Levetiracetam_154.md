# Levetiracetam (Keppra®)
- Drug ID: 154
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: For doses < 375 mg, dilution with a 25 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% gives a concentration < 15 mg/mL (minimal documented concentration in the literature), but allows administration in the absence of a syringe pump. If syringe pump available, favor this method of administration for patients < 6.25 kg. 

Administer doses < 3000 mg over 5 minutes and doses between 3000 and 4500 mg over 10 minutes.

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume according to the clinical situation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

The use of Phenobarbital is preferred in status epilepticus in infants <4 weeks old.

Subsequent doses of 10-30 mg/Kg (Adult max 1500 mg) IV q12h can be administered if required. Adjust according to renal function. These doses can also be administered orally as bioavailability is close to 100%.

## Indicación: Seizure prophylaxis in traumatic brain injury (Syringe pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV DILUTED with equal volume of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      concentration note: Loading dose
      administration: Dilute the dose to equal volume with  NaCl 0.9% to obtain a final concentration of 50 mg/mL.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

The loading dose in anticonvulsant prophylaxis in head trauma is lower than the loading dose in status epilepticus.

Subsequent doses of 10-20 mg/Kg (Adult max 1500 mg) IV q12h can be administered if required. Adjust according to renal function. These doses can also be administered orally as bioavailability is close to 100%.
