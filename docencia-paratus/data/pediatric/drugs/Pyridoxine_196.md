# Pyridoxine (Vitamin B6)
- Drug ID: 196
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Metabolic disorder)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: Pyridoxine is not a routine treatment in status epilepticus but may be indicated in infants with suspected underlying metabolic disorder.

Can be administered IM.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
• Ideally administer under EEG monitoring to objectify efficacy; do not delay treatment if EEG not available.
• If seizures decrease/stop with Pyridoxine IV, consider Pyridoxine 50 mg PO BID.
• Pyridoxine-dependent seizures are a result of defective binding of pyridoxine in the formation of GABA. They typically present in the neonatal period or early infancy.
      doses:
        • 100 mg (1 mL) [weight_min=2, weight=0, min=0, max=0]

## Indicación: Ethylene glycol intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 1 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: • Administer only in the presence of metabolic acidosis.
• Repeat the dose every 6 hours until resolution of intoxication and resolution of acidosis.
• Contact your local poison control center if hemodialysis, as recommendations on continuing or stopping pyridoxine during renal replacement therapy are unclear in the literature.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR
      doses:
        • 50 mg (0.5 mL) [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]

## Indicación: Isoniazide, hydrazine or gyromitre mushrooms intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Seizure prevention
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: In prevention/absence of seizures, administer the dose over 4 hours. If seizures occur during the administration of the infusion, increase the rate to give the remainder of the dose in less than 10 minutes and see "active seizures" entry for further instructions.
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR
• Treat seizures concomitantly with benzodiazepines.

  - STEP: Active seizures
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: If the seizures stop during the 10 minute infusion, administer the remainder of the infusion over 4 hours. If the seizures start again during these 4 hours, increase the rate again and administer the remainder of the infusion quickly (less than 10 minutes).

If the seizures continue or recur after the administration of the first dose, administer a second dose in the same manner. 

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose
