# Diphenhydramine (Benadryl®)
- Drug ID: 128
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Pruritus secondary to anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Do NOT use as initial therapy for anaphylaxis; epinephrine IM administration should be prioritized.
      particularity: Nil

## Indicación: Treatment of extrapyramidal symptoms
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IM  [IM in < 10 seconds]  Administer IM undiluted in the thigh
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Extrapyramidal effects are more common in children. Diphenhydramine to prevent extrapyramidal symptoms does not clearly reduce its incidence according to the literature, but increases the risk of respiratory depression and lengthens the stay in the emergency room when combined with antipsychotic.
      particularity: Nil
