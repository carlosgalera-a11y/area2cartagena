# Vancomycin (Vancocin®)
- Drug ID: 209
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection including meningitis (syringe pump/fluid restriction)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer DILUTED
      concentration: 5 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
A faster administration can lead to a Vancomycin Infusion Reaction (formely known as "Red Man Syndrome"). Consider giving over 90 min if history of reaction or dose of 20 mg/Kg.

DILUTED solution at 5 mg/mL
Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Vancomycin 500 mg
1) Add 10 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 50 mg/mL

Further dilution for administration via pump:
1) Draw 10 mL of DILUTED Vancomycin at 50 mg/mL
2) Withdraw 10 mL from a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
3) Add 10 mL of DILUTED Vancomycin to the 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
4) Final concentration after further dilution = 5 mg/mL
5) Administer the indicated dose via pump (Volume/time) or Draw the indicated dose and administer via syringe pump.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.

Usual dosage: 15-20 mg/Kg/dose IV q6-8h excluding patients <1 month and premature and patients with reduced renal function. Adjust the dose and the interval according to the indication and the blood levels, preferably targeting an AUC of 400-600 mg/L x hour or a trough of 10-20 mg/L.

## Indicación: Severe infection including meningitis (pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
• A faster administration can lead to a Vancomycin Infusion Reaction (formely known as "Red Man Syndrome"). Consider giving over 90 min if history of reaction or dose of 20 mg/Kg.

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of vancomycin 500 mg
1) Add 10 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 50 mg/mL
3) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50-100-250 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
