# Acetylcysteine (Mucomyst®)
- Drug ID: 82
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Acetaminophen intoxication, Quebec poison control protocol
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: First hour
      concentration: 200 mg/mL
      administration: This is the protocol of the Quebec Poison Control Center

Administer 150 mg/Kg over 1 hour (first hour) then 15 mg/Kg/h for at least 20 hours or as recommended by the poison control center (see "infusion" step).

Usual dilution with D5W, but also compatible with NaCl 0.9% and NaCl 0.45%.
Do NOT remove volume during preparation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. No compatibility data with LR.

To be started and stopped according to the criteria of your poison control center.
In exceptional cases, the poison control center may recommend doubling the infusion. It is then necessary to double the concentration of the solute, and not to double the flow rate of the infusion.

Watch for the risk of an anaphylactoid reaction.
