# Azithromycin PO (Zithromax® PO)
- Drug ID: 264
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Atypical bacterial infections
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose (Day 1)  [PO]  x 1 dose (then see dose Day 2-5)
      concentration: 40 mg/mL
      administration: • Drugs interactions via CYP 450 3A4 are less than with clarithromycine.
• For the treatment of B pertussis or Ureaplasma in infants aged < 1 month, use azithromycin instead of clarithromycin. Azithromycin has not been associated with infantile hypertrophic pyloric stenosis.
• Consider adding atypical coverage with macrolide to amoxicillin or amoxicillin/clavulanic acid based on chest X-ray (bilateral focal or interstitial infiltrates) and if patient or sibling attends school or daycare.
      particularity: Possible increase in QTc without an increase in the incidence of Torsade de Pointes.
