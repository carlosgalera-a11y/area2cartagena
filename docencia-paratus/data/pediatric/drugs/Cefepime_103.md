# Cefepime (Maxipime®)
- Drug ID: 103
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 3-5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      administration: Cefepime is not a first choice in the empiric treatment of hospital-acquired infections or Pseudomonas infections. Consult an infectious disease specialist before initiating.

Reconstitute according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
IV administration over 3-5 minutes is safe according to the literature.
It is possible to administer the drug over a longer period according to the hospital protocols in place.
The maximum concentration for administration, after secondary dilution, is 100 mg/mL. Secondary dilution not necessary. 
Can be administered IM.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Accumulation when CrCl <60 mL/min with increased risk of severe neurological adverse effects. Dosage adjustment in renal failure. 

Usual dosing: 50 mg/Kg/Dose q8-12h up to a maximum of 2g IV q8h excluding patients <1 month and premature babies and patients with reduced renal function.
