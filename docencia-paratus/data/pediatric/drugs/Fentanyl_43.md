# Fentanyl (Sublimaze®)
- Drug ID: 43
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IV  [IV over 3-5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 50 mcg/mL
      administration: IV over 3-5 minutes to minimize adverse effects.
      particularity: Wait 3 minutes for optimal analgesic effect.
Protect ampoule from light.

## Indicación: Intubation - Induction Agent
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Induction agent (Monotherapy)  [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 50 mcg/mL
      administration: Caution: 
Increased risk of chest wall rigidity in neonates linked to the rate of administration and high doses. In the context of intubation, the use of a neuromuscular blocker treats chest wall rigidity.

Additional information:
• Dose 3-5 mcg/kg if used as the ONLY induction agent in rapid sequence intubation 
• Fentanyl is not a first choice as a sole induction agent EXCEPT in neonatology.

## Indicación: Premedication in rapid sequence intubation
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 1-2 min]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 50 mcg/mL
      administration: Ideally, if fentanyl is used for premedication, administer at least 3 minutes before induction, over 1-2 minutes.
      particularity: Increased risk of chest wall rigidity in neonates linked to the rate of administration and high doses. In the context of rapid sequence intubation, the use of a neuromuscular blocker treats chest wall rigidity.
