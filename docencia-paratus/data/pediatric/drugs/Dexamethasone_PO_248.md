- Weight type: 1

## Indicación: Mild to severe acute asthma
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [PO x 1 dose]
      administration: • The effectiveness of dexamethasone PO is equivalent to IV. (Bioavailability 100%) For patients with PRAM > 10, intolerant to dexamethasone PO or if in doubt about systemic absorption, because they are very symptomatic, switch to methylprednisolone IV.
• Do not use commercial dexamethasone elixir because of high alcohol content.
• If oral solution not available:• The 4 mg/mL IV formulation can be given PO. (Caution, PO formulation at 1 mg/mL and IV formulation at 4 mg/mL)
• There is a compound oral solution of dexamethasone 1 mg/mL

• Consider a second dose 24h after the first dose.
      particularity: Equivalent to 3-5 days of treatment with prednisone/prednisolone.

## Indicación: Anaphylaxis
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [PO x 1 dose]
      administration: • Do not use commercial dexamethasone elixir because of high alcohol content.
• If oral solution not available:• The 4 mg/mL IV formulation can be given PO. (Caution, PO formulation at 1 mg/mL and IV formulation at 4 mg/mL)
• There is a compound oral solution of dexamethasone 1 mg/mL

• Consider a second dose 24h after the first dose if symptoms remains.
      particularity: • There is lack of evidence for the use of steroids in the acute management of anaphylaxis. Their use should not be systematic and could be reserved for certain cases (eg bronchospasm, upper airway obstruction, shock). The main treatment remains epinephrine.
• The use of corticosteroids in anaphylaxis comes from evidence in asthma and the most severe and frequent clinical manifestation in pediatrics remains bronchospasm. The use of dexamethasone is based on the lack of evidence in anaphylaxis of corticosteroids in general, the evidence for dexamethasone in severe acute asthma, as well as the practicality of its administration compared to prednisone/prednisolone.
