# Naproxen (Naprosyn®)
- Drug ID: 252
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Anti-inflammatory/Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: q12h  [PO or PR]  q12h reg or PRN
      concentration: 25 mg/mL
      administration: • Do not use under 6 months of age, because of immaturity of platelet function and risk of closure of the arterial duct in newborns.
• Intra-rectal administration should only be used as a last resort, as absorption is erratic
• Contraindicated for patients with allergy to NSAIDs, moderate to severe dehydration, renal impairment, severe heart or liver impairment, active bleeding, gastrointestinal bleeding or ulceration, surgery or invasive procedure planned within 24 hours, coagulopathy, thrombocytopenia ( < 100 x 10^9/L), active varicella, anticoagulant treatment or chemotherapy in progress
      particularity: Maximum dose of 20 mg/Kg/day to a maximum of 1250 mg/day
      doses:
