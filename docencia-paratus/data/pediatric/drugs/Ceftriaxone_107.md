# Ceftriaxone (Rocephin®)
- Drug ID: 107
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Non-meningeal infection  [IV over 3-5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9% after reconstitution
      administration: Reconstitute according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
IV administration over 3-5 minutes is safe according to the literature. 
It is possible to administer the drug over a longer period of time according to the hospital protocols in place.
Secondary dilution not necessary.

Can be administered IM. To reduce pain associated with IM administration, ceftriaxone can be reconstituted by diluting with Lidocaine 1% without epinephrine.
      particularity: Incompatible with LR and calcium. Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl. 

No dosage adjustment in renal impairment.

Usual dosing: 50-100 mg/Kg/day divided q12-24h up to a maximum of 2g IV q24h excluding patients < 1 month old and premature babies.

## Indicación: Usual dosage (IM)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Non-meningeal infection (IM)  [IM in < 10 seconds]  Administer deep IM undiluted into a large muscle
      concentration: 250 mg/mL
      administration: • To reduce pain associated with IM administration, ceftriaxone can be reconstituted by diluting with Lidocaine 1% without epinephrine.

• Presentation: Vial of Ceftriaxone 250 mg
1) Add 0.9 mL of sterile water or lidocaine 1% without epinephrine 
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 250 mg/mL

• Presentation: Vial of Ceftriaxone 500 mg
1) Add 1.1 mL of sterile water or lidocaine 1% without epinephrine 
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 350 mg/mL

• Presentation: Vial of Ceftriaxone 1000 mg
1) Add 2.2 mL of sterile water or lidocaine 1% without epinephrine 
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 350 mg/mL

• Presentation: Vial of Ceftriaxone 2000 mg
1) Add 4.4 mL of sterile water or lidocaine 1% without epinephrine 
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 350 mg/mL

• IM administration technique• < 1 year old• 25 gauge (≥ 2.2 cm (⅞ in))
• Dorsogluteus muscle (max 2-5 mL depending on muscle size)  
• Vastus lateralis or gluteus anterior muscle (max 1 mL/injection site)

• 1 year and over• 22 to 25 gauge (≥ 2.5 cm (1-1 ½ in))
• Dorsogluteal muscle (max 2-5 mL/injection site depending on muscle size)
• Deltoid, vastus lateralis or gluteus anterior muscle (max 1-3 mL/injection site depending on the size of the muscle)
      particularity: No dosage adjustment in renal impairment.

Usual dosing: 50-100 mg/Kg/day divided q12-24h up to a maximum of 2g IV q24h excluding patients < 1 month old and premature babies.
