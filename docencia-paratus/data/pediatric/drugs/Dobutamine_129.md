# Dobutamine (Dobutrex®)
- Drug ID: 129
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Cardiogenic shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 12.5 mg/mL
      administration: Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 5 mcg/kg/min
2) Titrate by increments of 2.5 mcg/kg/min q5-10min depending on clinical response. (Expert Opinion)

Additional information:
• N.B. Dobutamine is a pure inotrope. Therefore, its efficacy cannot be evaluated with blood pressure changes, but rather by measuring cardiac output or by evaluating indirect signs of cardiac output.
• Do NOT remove volume during preparation
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Monitor for hypotension.

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.
