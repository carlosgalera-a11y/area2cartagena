# Ondansetron IV (Zofran®)
- Drug ID: 231
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Nausea/vomiting (Syringe pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 15 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 5 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: • Administration over 15 min to decrease risk of arrhythmia
• Can be given in 2 to 5 min if availability of the venous line is limited
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR and KCl.
ECG not required prior to administration unless underlying electrophysiological condition
      doses:
        •  [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=8]

## Indicación: Nausea/vomiting (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 2 mg/mL
      administration: • Administration over 15 min to decrease risk of arrhythmia
• Can be given in 2 to 5 min if availability of the venous line is limited

If 25 mL bag not available
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL NaCl 0.9% bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR and KCl.
ECG no required before administration unless underlying electrophysiological condition
