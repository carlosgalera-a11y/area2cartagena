# Nitroprusside (Nipride®)
- Drug ID: 176
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Targeted BP control
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Infusion (Pump)
      concentration: 25 mg/mL
      administration: Additional information:
• Do NOT remove volume during preparation
• Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration
• Protect solution from light at all times

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 0.5 mcg/kg/min.
2) Titrate by increments of 0.25-0.5 mcg/kg/min q5min until target BP is reached or a maximum dose of 5 mcg/Kg/min or 200 mcg/min. (Expert Opinion)
      particularity: The risk of cyanide toxicity increases with the use of doses greater than 2 mcg/kg/min over more than 24-48 hours or renal or hepatic failure.

Avoid in patients with intracranial hypertension.

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
