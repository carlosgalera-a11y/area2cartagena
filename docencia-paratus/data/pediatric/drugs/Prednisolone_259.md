# Prednisolone (Pediapred®)
- Drug ID: 259
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Usual dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Other indications (than asthma or ITP)  [PO]  Once daily
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: • Other indications for prednisone or prednisolone are usually Bell's palsy, Henoch-Schönlein purpura or nephrotic syndrome.
• The palatability (taste) of prednisone and prednisolone is very poor and may interfere with compliance; when indicated favor dexamethasone.
• For doses over 20 mg, consider that administering more than 20 mL of prednisolone may be too much for some children. Favor the use of PredniSONE in tablets or tablet fractions of 5 or 50 mg.
