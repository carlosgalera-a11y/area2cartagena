# Voriconazole (Vfend®)
- Drug ID: 204
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Severe infection
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Voriconazole 200 mg
1) Add 19 mL of sterile water
2) Concentration after reconstitution = 10 mg/mL
3) Draw the indicated dose and add to the indicated volume of NaCl 0.9%

Additional information:
• Final concentration after further dilution between 0.5 and 5 mg/mL
• Maximum rate of administration = 3 mg/Kg/hr.
• Do NOT administer rapid IV push
      particularity: • Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR
• Usual dose: 2 loading doses of 9 mg/Kg/dose, max 400 mg IV q12h followed by 4-6 mg/Kg/dose, max 200 mg IV q12h
• Multiple drug interactions (CYP 450 2C9, 2C19 and 3A4), increased ALT and increased QTc.
• Adjust according to blood dosages (trough), aiming for 1 to 5.5 mg/L.
• No dosage adjustment in renal impairment. In patients with CLcr <50 mL/min, the monograph does not recommend the use of IV voriconazole due to the potential accumulation of sodium sulfobutyl ether beta-cyclodextrin (SBECD), but the use <7 days in renal failure has not demonstrated any harmful effects.
