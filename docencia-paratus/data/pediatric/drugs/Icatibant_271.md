# Icatibant (Firazyr® or Sajazir®)
- Drug ID: 271
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Hereditary angioedema (treatment)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [Administer subcutaneous over 30 sec in the abdominal region]
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: • Administer subcutaneous over 30 seconds to the abdominal area. Inject 5 to 10 cm below the belly button, avoiding scarred or inflamed areas.
• Visually inspect the solution for particulate matter and discoloration prior to administration. 
• For doses < 30 mg, transfer the exact volume needed from the commercial syringe into a graduated syringe for greater precision.
      particularity: • Repeatable every 6 hours if response is inadequate or symptoms recur for a maximum of 3 doses.
• Onset of action: 30-60 min. 
• Therapies for angioedema should never delay securing the airway.
