# Liposomal lidocaine 4% (cream) (Maxilene®)
- Drug ID: 276
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Local anesthesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [Local application, at procedure sites]  Cover with transparent film or massage in. Remove the cream after 30 min.
      administration: Children 3 months or younger:
• Max 4 applications/day
• Max 2 sites at a time
• Space at least one hour between the first 2 applications and the following ones
• Maximum duration of application: 1 hour
Children over 3 months:
• Max 8 applications/day
• Max 4 sites at a time
• Space at least one hour between the first 4 applications and the following ones
• Maximum duration of application: 2 hours
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
        • 0.5 g/site (Canadian 5-cent coin or "nickel") [weight_min=2, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
        • 1 g/site (Canadian 2-dollar coin or "toonie") [weight_min=6, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
