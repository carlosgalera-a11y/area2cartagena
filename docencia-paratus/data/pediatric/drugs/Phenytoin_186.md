# Phenytoin (Dilantin®)
- Drug ID: 186
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Syringe pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  Administer IV DILUTED with 0.22 micron filter
      concentration: 8.33 mg/mL
      administration: • Use phenytoin OR fosphenytoin. Do NOT combine the 2 therapies
• If toxic seizures, do NOT use phenytoin; use phenobarbital or levetiracetam
• The use of phenobarbital and levetiracetam are preferred in status epilepticus in children < 6 months; therapeutic plasma levels are more difficult to achieve with phenytoin.

DILUTED solution at 8.33 mg/mL:

Further dilution for administration via syringe pump:
Presentation: 250 mg in 5 mL vial (50 mg/mL)
1) Use a 100 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
2) Add 1000 mg of Phenytoin (20 mL of 50 mg/mL) to the bag, WITHOUT removing the volume
3) Final concentration = 1000 mg in 120 mL = 8.33 mg/mL
4) Flush tubing with dilute phenytoin solution
5) Install a 0.22 micron filter at the end of the tubing
6) Withdraw the indicated dose and administer via syringe pump
7) Rinse tubing with NaCl 0.9% after administration. (Compatible only with NaCl 0.9%.)

Additional information:
• DILUTED phenytoin solution stable between 5 and 10 mg/mL in NaCl 0.9%
• Maximum administration rate: 1 mg/Kg/min (maximum 50 mg/min). In pediatric patients between 50 and 60 kg, administration of the loading dose over 20 minutes (rate up to 60 mg/min) is acceptable to reduce errors, since hypotension is rare. (Expert advice)
      particularity: Incompatible with D5W, LR. Y-site compatible with NaCl.

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus (Pump)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  Administer IV with a 0.22 micron filter
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: • Use phenytoin OR fosphenytoin. Do NOT combine the 2 therapies
• If toxic seizures, do NOT use phenytoin; use phenobarbital or levetiracetam
• The use of phenobarbital and levetiracetam are preferred in status epilepticus in children < 6 months; therapeutic plasma levels are more difficult to achieve with phenytoin.

Additional information:
• Compatible only with NaCl 0.9%
• Use with 0.22 micron filter
• Rinse tubing with NaCl 0.9% after administration
• DILUTED phenytoin solution stable between 5 and 10 mg/mL in NaCl 0.9%
• Maximum administration rate: 1 mg/Kg/min (maximum 50 mg/min). In pediatric patients between 50 and 60 kg, administration of the loading dose over 20 minutes (rate up to 60 mg/min) is acceptable to reduce errors, since hypotension is rare. (Expert advice)

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
