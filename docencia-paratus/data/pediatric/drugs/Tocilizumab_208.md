# Tocilizumab (Actemra®)
- Drug ID: 208
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: COVID + with hypoxemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: < 30kg
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      administration: Further dilution for administration via pump:
1) Choose the vials corresponding to the desired dose and according to availability (80 mg/4mL, 200 mg/10 mL or 400 mg/20 mL are all at a concentration of 20 mg/mL)
2) Add the total dose to a 50 mL bag of NaCl 0.9%
3) To mix the solution, gently invert the bag to avoid foaming
4) Administer IV over 1 hour
5) Finish by rinsing with 10 mL of NaCl 0.9%.

• Combined with dexamethasone, in patients with standard or high flow oxygen therapy, non-invasive or invasive mechanical ventilation with CRP > 75 mg/L. (RECOVERY and REMAP-CAP) (The benefits in mortality reduction have not been demonstrated with tocilizumab as monotherapy despite several studies.)
      particularity: Tocilizumab has not been studied in randomized controlled studies in pediatrics for the treatment of acute COVID-19; its use is extrapolated from adult data and should be considered on a case-by-case basis, balancing the risks/benefits.

Contraindications: neutrophils < 0.5 x 10^9/L, platelets < 50 x 10^9/L, septic shock (not due to COVID-19), ALT > 5x upper normal limit, patient immunosuppressed or on immunosuppressive therapy , serious or latent infection (eg: tuberculosis), allergy to tocilizumab or one of its components.

Keep refrigerated.
