# Levocarnitine (Carnitor®)
- Drug ID: 155
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Valproic acid intoxication
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose  [IV over 2-3 min]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      administration: • To be considered as an adjuvant in acute valproic acid poisoning in a symptomatic patient (encephalopathy or presence of hepatoxicity), not undergoing hemodialysis, with hyperammonemia.
• Follow the loading dose with maintenance doses; start 4 hours after the loading dose. 
• Levocarnitine is dialyzable; its use for patients who have already started hemodialysis is less relevant.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with NaCl, LR. Incompatible with D5%
