# Maintenance fluids (IV fluids)
- Drug ID: 250
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Maintenance fluids
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: All critically ill children unless dehydrated (75%)
      administration: Caution: Choice of IV solution to be determined

• Dextrose• Newborn < 72 hours of life: See maintenance D10% in paratus Neonatal
• Term neonate 3-28 days old: D10%-LR or D10%-NaCl 0.9% (if NPO or at risk of hypoglycemia with low intake) OR D5%-LR or D5%-NaCl 0.9% (monitor closely glycemia and maintain > 3.3 mmol/L)
• Patient 1 month of life up to puberty: D5%-LR or D5%-NaCl 0.9%
• Pubescent patient (excluding hepatic impairment and prolonged fasting. Check blood sugar if in doubt): LR, Plasmalyte or NaCl 0.9%

• NaCl 0.9% vs Lactated Ringer's• Patient > 1 month old: do NOT use hypotonic solutions such as NaCl 0.45%
• If metabolic acidosis, hyperchloremia or hyperkalemia: Lactate Ringer's or Plasmalyte. Hyperlactatemia is not a contraindication to the use of Lactate Ringer; 30 mL/Kg might possibly raise the lactate level by around 0.5 mmol/L.
• If intracranial hypertension, hypercalcemia or metabolic alkalosis: NaCl 0.9% or Plasmalyte

• KCl 20 mmol/L• Not necessary in initial solute
• Caution in oliguria, anuria, renal failure or acidosis
• If in doubt, measure serum potassium before changing to a solution with KCl.

• Avoid manual additions of concentrated electrolytes, especially KCl in bag of IV solution. Use commercially available solutions.
