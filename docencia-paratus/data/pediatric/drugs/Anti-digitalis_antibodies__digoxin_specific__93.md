# Anti-digitalis antibodies (digoxin specific) (DigiFab®)
- Drug ID: 93
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Cardiac arrest imminent
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 2-3 min]  Administer IV undiluted, without a filter
      administration: Repeat 5-10 vials as needed after 3-5 minutes
 
See the indications in Particularites

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Anti-digitalis antibodies 40 mg
1) Add 4 mL of sterile water
2) Mix gently, roll between hands until completely dissolved to avoid bubble formation 
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 10 mg/mL
      particularity: Keep refrigerated
Maximum total dose : 20 vials

For poisoning by digitalis derivatives other than digoxin, consult your poison control center. 

For potentially lethal poisoning with digoxin or other substances containing cardiac glycosides (plants, digitoxin, bufo venom, etc.) with the following symptoms:

• Symptomatic progressive bradyarrhythmia
• Atropine-resistant 2nd or 3rd degree AV block
• Ventricular tachycardia or fibrillation
• Digoxinemia > 12.8 nmol/L or > 10 ng/mL, more than 6 hours following acute ingestion without cardiotoxic coingestion for asymptomatic or midly symptomatic patients.
• K+ ≥ 5 mmol/L during acute poisoning

## Indicación: UNSTABLE patient, digoxin level unavailable
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Administer IV undiluted or diluted in 25 mL of NaCl 0.9%, without a filter
      administration: See the indications in Particularities 

Reconstitution:
Presentation: Vial of Anti-digitalis antibodies 40 mg
1) Add 4 mL of sterile water
2) Mix gently, roll between hands until completely dissolved to avoid bubble formation 
3) Concentration after reconstitution = 10 mg/mL

If 25 mL bag not available:
1) Remove 25 mL from a 50 mL bag
2) Add the indicated dose

Consider that the use of a bag of 25-50 mL can represent a significant amount of volume depending on the clinical situation.
      particularity: For poisoning by digitalis derivatives other than digoxin, consult your poison control center.

• K+ ≥ 5 mmol/L during acute poisoning
