## Indicación: Simple periorbital cellulitis (TID)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Simple periorbital cellulitis (TID)  [PO]  TID
      concentration: 80 mg/mL
      administration: • PO treatment of periorbital cellulitis is recommended only in patients:• ≥ 6 months of life
• Without signs of systemic toxicity
• Immunocompetent
• Controlled fever

• Criteria for mild periorbital cellulitis, without orbital involvement:• Mild edema and erythema
• absence of significant pain
• good eyelid opening
• normal, non-painful extraocular movements
• normal vision
• absence of proptosis and chemosis

• If in doubt of orbital involvement or meningeal complication, consult a specialist and refer for hospitalization.
• Requires follow-up 24-36 hours with hospitalization if no improvement
• Duration of therapy: 10-14 days
      particularity: Keep refrigerated
