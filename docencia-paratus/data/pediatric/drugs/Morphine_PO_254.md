# Morphine PO (Statex®)
- Drug ID: 254
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Analgesia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: q3h  [PO]  q3h reg or PRN
      concentration: 1 mg/mL
      administration: • If the oral solution not available, the 10 mg/mL IV formulation can be given PO. (Caution, dilute 1 mL of IV morphine 10 mg/mL with 9 mL of water to obtain a morphine 1 mg/mL PO formulation. Shake well before before withdrawing the PO dose.) 
• Use the minimum required dose for adequate analgesia and repeat as needed.
      particularity: Maximum effect after 30 minutes
Adjustment necessary in renal failure if repeated dose (favor hydromorphone)
Morphine 1 mg IV = Morphine 2-3 mg PO
