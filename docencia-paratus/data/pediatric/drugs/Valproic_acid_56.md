# Valproic acid (Epiject®)
- Drug ID: 56
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Status Epilepticus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Loading dose
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      concentration note: Opinion of Pediatric Neurology recommended
      administration: Avoid in patients with suspected metabolic disorders, hepatic insufficiency, coagulopathy or women who are pregnant or of childbearing age. Warning, drug interactions involving multiple CYP 450.

No data to justify dilution in less than 50 mL and concentrated administration via syringe pump.
      particularity: Drug available through Health Canada's Special Access Program; not commercially available in Canada.

Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Maximum speed of administration of 6 mg/kg/min.

Subsequent doses of 5-30 mg/Kg (max adult 1500 mg) IV q12h should be considered depending on the clinical outcome. These doses can also be administered orally for the bioavailability close to 100%. Adjust dosage according to serum levels.
