# Ondansetron PO (Zofran®)
- Drug ID: 222
- Type: drug
- Entorno: pediatric

## Indicación: Nausea and vomiting
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [PO q8h PRN]
      concentration: 0.8 mg/mL
      administration: In gastroenteritis, administrer ondansetron for one dose only. Repeated dose q8h are not indicated.

The 2 mg/mL IV formulation can be given PO if no oral formulation is available. (Caution, PO formulation at 0.8 mg/mL and IV formulation at 2 mg/mL)

If only tablets are available:
< 15kg: 2mg
15kg to < 30kg: 4mg
30kg and more: 8mg
      particularity: ECG not required prior to administration unless underlying electrophysiological condition
