## Indicación: Torsade de pointes (Polymorphic VT with pulse and prolonged QT)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Bolus  Administer IV DILUTED
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      concentration note: Dose expressed in mg of MgSO4
      administration: Caution:
Opinion of Pediatric Intensive Care recommended

If pre-mixed bag not available
DILUTED solution at 100 mg/mL

Further dilution for administration via syringe pump:
Presentation: Vial of MgSO4 500 mg/mL (5000 mg in 10 mL)
1) Withdraw 10 mL (1 vial) of MgSO4 500 mg/mL with a 50 mL syringe
2) Add 40 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 5000 mg in 50 mL = 100 mg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration

Further dilution for administration via pump:
Presentation: Vial of MgSO4 500 mg/mL (5000 mg in 10 mL)
1) Remove 10 mL from a 50 mL bag of NaCl 0.9% = 40 mL
2) Add 10 mL (1 vial) of MgSO4 500 mg/mL to the bag
3) Final concentration = 5000 mg in 50 mL = 100 mg/mL
4) Rinse the tubing with the infusion before starting the administration

Caution:
• Risk of hypotension related to the rate of administration; start the bolus in 10 minutes and, if hypotension, reduce the rate by half. Use push dose phenylephrine rather than push dose epinephrine if congenital long QT syndrome cannot be excluded.
• If recurrence of Torsades de Pointes, repeat the bolus and start a continuous infusion.
• The DILUTED solution at 100 mg/mL allows the administration of boluses and continuous infusion via the same solution.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.

Suggested titration:
1) Start continuous infusion if arrhythmia persists despite 1 or 2 boluses of MgSO4 in the last hour. Avoid continuous infusion in the presence of renal impairment (CLcr <30 mL/min)
2) Start at 25 mg/Kg/h aiming for magnesemia of 1.5-2 mmol/L
3) Increase to 50 mg/Kg/h if target not reached after 6 hrs.
4) If magnesemia 2.1-2.9 mmol/L, decrease the infusion by 50%.
5) If magnesemia ≥ 3 mmol/L, stop continuous infusion
6) If weakness, drowsiness, bradycardia or dyspnea, sample STAT magnesemia and reassess continuous infusion.
7) Stop the continuous infusion after 24 hours.
      particularity: The MgSO4 infusion can be prepared in NaCl 0,9%, D5W or LR.
Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR.
