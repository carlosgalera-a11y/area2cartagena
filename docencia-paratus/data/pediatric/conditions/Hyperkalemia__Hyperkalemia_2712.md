# Hyperkalemia — Hyperkalemia

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Metabolic
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2712


## Tab: Default
## Initial medical management
    Stop all K+ intake and medications that can increase K+
    12-lead ECG
    Confirm that non-hemolyzed sample and by macromethod Start treatments simultaneously
## ECG changes or severe hyperkalemia
  ### Peripheral venous access
    [DRUG] Calcium (Gluconate) (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
  ### Central venous access
    [DRUG] Calcium (Chloride) (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
## Bradycardia with hemodynamic instability
    [DRUG] Epinephrine (Infusion (Pump)) — Cardiogenic and distributive shock
      IV infusion
## Intracellular potassium shifting
  ### Dextrose and insulin
    [DRUG] D10% (N/A) — Adjuvant to IV insulin in hyperkalemia
    [DRUG] D25% (N/A) — Adjuvant to IV insulin in hyperkalemia
    [DRUG] Insulin regular (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
      IV Bolus
  ### Salbutamol
    [DRUG] Salbutamol (Inhalation) — Hyperkalemia
      Inhalation
    [DRUG] Salbutamol (Nebulization) — Hyperkalemia
      Nebulization
## Hypovolemia/Euvolemia WITHOUT metabolic acidosis
    Avoid NaCl 0.9% Increases K+ vs balanced solute
    [DRUG] Lactate Ringer's (Non-cardiogenic shock) — Shock
## Hypovolemia/Euvolemia WITH metabolic acidosis
    Avoid hypertonic/undiluted sodium bicarbonate (4.2 or 8.4%) Ineffective in hyperK, favor the diluted solution if acidosis
    [DRUG] Sodium bicarbonate (Hemodynamically stable) — Hyperkalemia with acidosis
      Bolus
## Kaliuresis induction
  ### Hypervolemia/Euvolemia with fluid bolus
    [DRUG] Furosemide (N/A) — Acute oedema/heart failure
      Initial dose
    [DRUG] Furosemide (Over 30 min (Pump)) — Subsequent dose in oliguria or anuria
      Subsequent dose
    [DRUG] Metolazone (N/A) — Hyperkalemia
      PO
    Consider hemodialysis
## Congenital adrenal hyperplasia
  ### Consider if infant
    Draw the different adrenal hormones before starting treatment
    [DRUG] Hydrocortisone (N/A) — Septic/Adrenal crisis
