# Hypoglycemia — Hypoglycemia

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Metabolic
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2713


## Tab: Default
## Before treating the hypoglycemia
  ### If newborn < 72 hours old or criticall ill
    If glycemia < 2.8 mmol/L, consider collecting a critical sample for exploration of endocrine and metabolic causes
## Dextrose PO (Without altered mental state)
  ### Infant
    Mother's milk or milk formula followed by a feed
    Dextrose gel 40 % (e.g.: Dex4® or equivalent) followed by a feed
  ### Child and adolescent
    120 mL (4 oz) of juice
    Dextrose gel 40% (e.g.: Dex4® or equivalent) 1 gel of 38 mL (15 g)
## IV Dextrose
    [DRUG] D10% (N/A) — Hypoglycemia
    [DRUG] D25% (N/A) — Hypoglycemia
    [DRUG] Maintenance fluids (All healthy or dehydrated children (100%)) — Maintenance fluids
      Maintenance with dextrose (D5% or D10% depending on age)
