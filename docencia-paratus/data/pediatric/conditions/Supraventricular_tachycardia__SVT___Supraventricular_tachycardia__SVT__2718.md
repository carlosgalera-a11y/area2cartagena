# Supraventricular tachycardia (SVT) — Supraventricular tachycardia (SVT)

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Cardiovascular
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2718


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    ABC + 12-lead ECG
    Assessment for signs of hypoperfusion (altered mental status, hypotension, peripheral hypoperfusion)
## Unstable
## Stable
    Consider vagal maneuvers without delaying adenosine E.g.: Ice bag over the face if infant or blow into a syringe then legs raised at 45° for older children
    [DRUG] Adenosine (1st dose) — Supraventricular Tachycardia (SVT)
    [DRUG] Adenosine (2nd dose) — Supraventricular Tachycardia (SVT)
## Refractory or recurrent
  ### Consult pediatric intensive care or cardiology
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (Bolus) — VT with pulse/SVT (Pump)
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Amiodarone (Drip) — VT with pulse/SVT (Pump)
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Procainamide (Bolus) — VT with pulse/SVT (Pump)
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Procainamide (Drip) — VT with pulse/SVT (Pump)
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Esmolol (N/A) — SVT
      IV infusion
    Treatments not recommended in pediatrics: Diltiazem IV, Metoprolol IV, Digoxine IV
