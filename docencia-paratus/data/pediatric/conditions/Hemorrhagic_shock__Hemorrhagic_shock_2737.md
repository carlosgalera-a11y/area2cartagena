## General measures
  ### Favor rapid administration of blood products as soon as possible
    Limit crystalloids as much as possible to avoid coagulopathy
    Isotonic crystalloid bolus
    Consider activating massive hemorrhage protocol
    [DRUG] packed Red Blood Cells (N/A) — Massive hemorrhage
    [DRUG] Fresh Frozen Plasma (N/A) — Massive hemorrhage
    [DRUG] Platelets (N/A) — Massive hemorrhage
      1 platelets unit (apheresis) (approximately 250 mL)
    [DRUG] Platelets (N/A) — Massive hemorrhage
      1 platelets unit (apheresis) (approximately 250 mL)
    [DRUG] Cryoprecipitate (N/A) — Massive hemorrhage
      1 unit (approximately 15 mL)
    Control the source of bleeding
    Favor balanced transfusions (Ratio 2:1:1 or 1:1:1) according to local protocol
    Hypothermia prevention
  ### Calcium repletion
    [DRUG] Calcium (Gluconate) (N/A) — Hemorrhagic shock
    [DRUG] Calcium (Chloride) (N/A) — Hemorrhagic shock
      Central line
  ### Anticoagulation reversal
