# Analgosedation - Invasive ventilation — Analgosedation - Invasive ventilation

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Respiratory
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2726


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Consider the presence of parents to reassure the child
    If available, evaluate and adjust the level of analgosedation with tools validated in pediatrics (e.g.: numeric rating scale/FACES, FLACC, COMFORT-B, RASS)
    [DRUG] Fentanyl (Bolus) — Post-intubation analgesia
      Pre-aspiration or pre-procedure as needed
    [DRUG] Ketamine (Bolus) — Post-intubation sedation
      Rescue bolus if agitated
    [DRUG] Rocuronium (N/A) — Neuromuscular Blocker (miscellaneous/non-intubation)
      After repeated rescue boluses of analgosedation if risk of extubation
## Analgesia
    [DRUG] Acetaminophen PO (15 mg/Kg q6h) — Pain or fever
    [DRUG] Acetaminophen PO (12.5 mg/Kg q4h) — Pain or fever
    [DRUG] Acetaminophen IV (N/A) — Analgesia (IV)
  ### Validate the absence of contraindications to NSAIDs
    [DRUG] Ibuprofen PO (N/A) — Pain or fever
    [DRUG] Ketorolac (N/A) — Analgesia
  ### Mild to moderate analgosedation or extubation < 24h
    [DRUG] Morphine IV (Bolus) — Post-intubation analgesia
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Fentanyl (Bolus) — Post-intubation analgesia
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Ketamine (Infusion (Pump)) — Co-analgesia
      IV infusion
  ### Moderate to profound analgosedation or extubation > 24h
    All agents used for mild to moderate analgesia can usually be used for deep analgesia, in the higher dose range, depending on clinical response
    [DRUG] Morphine IV (Infusion (Pump)) — Post-intubation analgesia
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Fentanyl (Infusion (Pump)) — Post-intubation analgesia
      IV infusion
## Sedation
  ### Mild to moderate analgosedation (RASS -1 to +1) or extubation < 24h
    [DRUG] Dexmedetomidine (N/A) — Sedation
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Ketamine (Infusion (Pump)) — Post-intubation sedation
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Propofol (Bolus PRN) — Post-intubation sedation
      Bolus PRN
    [DRUG] Propofol (mcg/kg/min) — Post-intubation sedation
    [DRUG] Propofol (mg/kg/h) — Post-intubation sedation
    [DRUG] Midazolam (Bolus) — Post-intubation sedation
      Bolus
  ### Moderate to profound analgosedation (RASS ≥-3) or extubation > 24h
    All agents used for mild to moderate sedation can usually be used for deep sedation, in the higher dose range, depending on clinical response. Dexmedetomidine sedation alone will not achieve a RASS of -2 or less, but can be used in combination.
    [DRUG] Midazolam (Infusion (Premixed bag)) — Post-intubation sedation
    [DRUG] Midazolam (Infusion (Pump)) — Post-intubation sedation
      If premixed bag not available
