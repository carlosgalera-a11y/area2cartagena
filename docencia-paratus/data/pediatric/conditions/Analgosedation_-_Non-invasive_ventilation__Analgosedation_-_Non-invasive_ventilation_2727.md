# Analgosedation - Non-invasive ventilation — Analgosedation - Non-invasive ventilation

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Respiratory
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2727


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Consider the presence of parents to reassure the child
    If available, evaluate and adjust the level of analgosedation with validated tools in pediatrics (e.g.: numeric rating scale/FACES, FLACC, COMFORT-B, RASS)
    [DRUG] Acetaminophen PO (15 mg/Kg q6h) — Pain or fever
    [DRUG] Acetaminophen PO (12.5 mg/Kg q4h) — Pain or fever
    [DRUG] Acetaminophen IV (N/A) — Analgesia (IV)
  ### Validate the absence of contraindications to NSAIDs
    [DRUG] Ibuprofen PO (N/A) — Pain or fever
    [DRUG] Ketorolac (N/A) — Analgesia
## Analgesia
    [DRUG] Morphine IV (Bolus) — Analgesia
      Bolus
    [DRUG] Ketamine (Infusion (Pump)) — Co-analgesia
      IV infusion
## Sedation
    [DRUG] Dexmedetomidine (N/A) — Sedation
      IV infusion
    Avoid benzodiazepines if possible (pro-delirium, paradoxical agitation in pre-school children, reduce respiratory drive)
