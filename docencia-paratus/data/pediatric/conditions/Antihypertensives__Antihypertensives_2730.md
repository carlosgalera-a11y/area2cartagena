## Calcium channel blockers
    [DRUG] Nicardipine (Infusion (Premixed bag)) — Targeted BP control
    [DRUG] Nicardipine (Infusion (Pump)) — Targeted BP control

    [DRUG] Clevidipine (N/A) — Targeted BP control
      IV infusion
## Nitrates
    [DRUG] Nitroprusside (Infusion (Pump)) — Targeted BP control
    [DRUG] Nitroglycerin (Infusion (Premixed bag)) — Heart failure with coronary artery disease
    [DRUG] Nitroglycerin (Infusion (Pump)) — Heart failure with coronary artery disease
      If premixed bag not available
## Adrenergic blockers
    [DRUG] Esmolol (N/A) — Targeted BP control
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Labetalol (N/A) — Targeted BP control (bolus)
      Bolus
    Avoid IV continuous infusion of labetalol in children (risk of accumulation with long t1/2)
    [DRUG] Phentolamine (Infusion (Pump)) — Targeted BP control
      IV infusion
## Others
    Avoid IV hydralazine because the dose-response effect is unpredictable
    Avoid IV enalaprilat because the dose-response effect is unpredictable in children and pediatric data are limited
