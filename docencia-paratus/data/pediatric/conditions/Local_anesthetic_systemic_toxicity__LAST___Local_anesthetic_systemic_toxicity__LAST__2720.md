# Local anesthetic systemic toxicity (LAST) — Local anesthetic systemic toxicity (LAST)

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Neurological
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2720


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Stop all local anesthetic administration
    Consider contacting your poison center
## Specific antidotes
    [DRUG] Lipid emulsion 20% (N/A) — Local anesthetic systemic toxicity

    [DRUG] Midazolam (IM) — Status Epilepticus

    [DRUG] Lorazepam (N/A) — Status Epilepticus

    [DRUG] Levetiracetam (N/A) — Status Epilepticus (Pump)
