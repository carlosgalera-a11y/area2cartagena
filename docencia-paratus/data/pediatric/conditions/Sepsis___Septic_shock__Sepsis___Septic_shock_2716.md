# Sepsis / Septic shock — Sepsis / Septic shock

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Infectious
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2716


## Tab: Default
## General measures
    Obtain cultures and prioritize source control
## Empiric antibiotics
    Start IV antibiotics as soon as possible (<1h if patient in shock)
    [DRUG] Ceftriaxone (Non-meningeal infection) — Usual dosage
    [DRUG] Cefotaxime (Non-meningeal infection) — Usual dosage
  ### MRSA coverage
    [DRUG] Vancomycin (N/A) — Severe infection including meningitis (pump)
  ### Pseudomonas coverage or intra-abdominal infection
    [DRUG] Piperacillin-tazobactam (N/A) — Severe infection
    [DRUG] Meropenem (Non-meningeal infection) — Usual dosage
## Volume repletion
    Bolus cristalloïde isotonique, solution balancée préférablement
    Frequent clinical reassessment between boluses with particular attention to peripheral perfusion, capillary refill time, and hepatomegaly.
## Vasopressors and inotropes
    [DRUG] Epinephrine (Infusion (Pump)) — Cardiogenic and distributive shock
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Norepinephrine (Infusion (Premixed bag)) — Shock
      Premixed bag (IV infusion)
    [DRUG] Norepinephrine (Infusion (Pump)) — Shock
      Premixed bag not available (IV infusion)
  ### Vasopressor refractory shock
    [DRUG] Vasopressin (Infusion (Pump)) — Shock refractory to vasoconstrictors
      IV infusion
    [DRUG] Hydrocortisone (N/A) — Septic/Adrenal crisis
