# Laryngitis — Laryngitis

- Tipo: condition
- Categoría: Respiratory
- Entorno: pediatric
- Indication ID: 2715


## Tab: Default
## General
    Acute episode of barking cough or inspiratory ± expiratory stridor: Westley croup score (0-17)
    Administer O₂ while avoiding agitating the child (ex.: Blow-by method with kid sitting on the parent)
    Rule out foreign body, anaphylaxis, history of laryngomalacia
## Stable (Westley 1-7)
    [DRUG] Dexamethasone PO (N/A) — Laryngitis
  ### Westley 5-7
    [DRUG] Epinephrine (N/A) — Nebulization
      Nebulization
## Unstable (Westley ≥ 8)
  ### Respiratory failure
    Consult ENT and anesthesia STAT
    Difficult airway equipement bedside with ETT 0.5 mm smaller
    Start CPAP with PEEP at 7 cmH₂O
    [DRUG] Dexamethasone IV (N/A) — Laryngitis/Laryngeal edema
    [DRUG] Epinephrine (N/A) — Nebulization
      Nebulization
    Consider Heliox (70% or 60% in He) if O₂ needs < 40%
    X-ray of soft tissue of the neck (rule out epiglottitis)
