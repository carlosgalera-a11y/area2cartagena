=== neonatal INDICATION -> DRUGS ===

• Anemia
    - packed Red Blood Cells
• Cardiac arrest
    - Epinephrine
• Coagulopathy
    - Cryoprecipitate
    - Fresh Frozen Plasma
• Duct dependent congenital heart disease
    - Alprostadil (Prostaglandin E1)
• Fluid bolus
    - NaCl 0.9%
• Hemorrhagic shock
    - Calcium (Gluconate)
• Hyperkalemia or Hemorrhage
    - Calcium (Chloride)
• Hyperkalemia with acidosis
    - Sodium bicarbonate
• Hyperkaliemia
    - Calcium (Gluconate)
• Hypoglycemia
    - D10%
• Intubation - Induction Agent​
    - Fentanyl
    - Ketamine
• Intubation- Bradycardia
    - Atropine
• Intubation- Neuromuscular Blocker
    - Rocuronium
    - Succinylcholine
• Maintenance fluids
    - D10% infusion
• Prophylaxis for vitamin K deficiency bleeding
    - Vitamin K
• Pulmonary hypertension with acidosis
    - Sodium bicarbonate
• Pulseless VT/VF
    - Amiodarone
    - Lidocaine IV
• Pulseless VT/VF associated with torsade de pointes
    - Magnesium (Sulfate)
• Respiratory distress syndrome of the newborn
    - Beractant
    - Bovine lipid extract surfactant
    - Poractant alpha
• Severe infection including meningitis
    - Cloxacillin
• Shock
    - Dopamine
    - Epinephrine
    - Norepinephrine
• Supraventricular Tachycardia (SVT)
    - Adenosine
• Thrombocytopenia
    - Platelets
• Urgent reversal of rocuronium
    - Sugammadex
• Usual Dosage
    - Gentamicin
    - Tobramycin
• Usual dosage
    - Ampicillin
    - Cefotaxime
