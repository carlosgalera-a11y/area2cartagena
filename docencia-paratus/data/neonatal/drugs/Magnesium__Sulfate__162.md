# Magnesium (Sulfate) (MgSO₄)
- Drug ID: 162
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Pulseless VT/VF associated with torsade de pointes
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV DILUTED
      concentration: 20 mg/mL
      concentration note: Dose expressed in mg of MgSO4
      administration: Do NOT use MgSO4 500 mg/mL undiluted; unmeasurable volumes for neonatal doses
Further dilution for final concentration at 20 mg/mL (If prediluted concentration is unavailable):
Presentation : Vial of MgSO4 500 mg/mL
1) Withdraw 1 mL of MgSO4 500 mg/mL and transfer to a 30 or 50 mL syringe
2) Add to 24 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 500 mg in 25 mL = 20 mg/mL
      particularity: The dose is expressed in mg of MgSO4, NOT in mg of elemental magnesium.
