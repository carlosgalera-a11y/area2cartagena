# Cryoprecipitate ([])
- Drug ID: 115
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Coagulopathy
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Rate of administration varies depending on the clinical indication
      administration: Repeat according to clinical response.

Transfusion threshold : fibrinogen < 1.5-2 g/L. (Adapt to the clinical situation with the recommendations of neonatology.)

1 bag of cryoprecipitate = 1 unit (approximately 15 mL)
      particularity: Nil
      doses:
        • 1 unit (≈ 15 mL) IV [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]
