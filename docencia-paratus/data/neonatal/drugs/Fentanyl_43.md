# Fentanyl (Sublimaze®)
- Drug ID: 43
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Intubation - Induction Agent​
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: Induction agent- Monotherapy  [IV over 5 minutes]  Administer IV diluted up to 1 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 50 mcg/mL
      administration: Increased risk of chest wall rigidity in neonates linked to the rate of administration and high doses. In the context of intubation, the use of a neuromuscular blocker treats chest wall rigidity.

Consider dose adjustment according to hemodynamic status

Dose: 3-5 mcg/kg (usually 4 mcg/Kg) if used as the ONLY induction agent in rapid sequence intubation
