# Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP)
- Drug ID: 189
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Coagulopathy
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   Rate varies according to indication
      administration: Repeat according to clinical response.

Transfusion threshold: INR > 1.8. (Adapt to the clinical situation with the recommendations of neonatology.) 

In neonatology, prescribe transfusions in mL rather than units.
1 bag of FFP = approximately 250 mL
