# Ketamine (Ketalar®)
- Drug ID: 62
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Intubation - Induction Agent​
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 2 minutes]  Administer IV diluted up to 1 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Caution:
• Consider dose adjustment according to hemodynamic status
• Ketamine may be favored in patients kept with spontaneous breathing (ex.: administration of surfactant by less invasive method) or with hemodynamic instability.

Do NOT use ketamine 50 mg/mL undiluted; unmeasurable volumes for neonatal doses
If 10 mg/mL concentration is unavailable:
Presentation : Vial of Ketamine 50 mg/mL
1) Withdraw 2 mL (1 vial) of Ketamine 50 mg/mL with a 10 mL syringe
2) Add to 8 mL of NaCl 0.9% to the syringe
3) Final concentration = 100 mg in 10 mL = 10 mg/mL
      particularity: Laryngospasm risk < 1%
