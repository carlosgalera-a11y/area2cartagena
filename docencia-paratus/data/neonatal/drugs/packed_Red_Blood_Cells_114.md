# packed Red Blood Cells (pRBC)
- Drug ID: 114
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Anemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 5 min if massive hemorrhage]  Rate of administration varies depending on the clinical indication
      administration: Repeat according to clinical response.

Transfusion threshold for newborn < 1 week of life: Hemoglobin < 90 g/L. (Adapt to the clinical situation with the recommendations of neonatology.)

May be administered over 3 hours if patient is stable.

In neonatology, prescribe blood transfusions in mL rather than units.
1 PRBC unit = approximately 320 mL

The sodium citrate in pRBC lowers calcium; monitor ionized/mesured calcium if repeated administration of pRBC.
