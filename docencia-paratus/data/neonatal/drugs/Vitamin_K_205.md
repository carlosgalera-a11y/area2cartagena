# Vitamin K (Phytonadione)
- Drug ID: 205
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Prophylaxis for vitamin K deficiency bleeding
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: IM  Administer IM undiluted in the thigh
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Oral administration of vitamin K is less effective than IM administration.
      particularity: Protect from light
      doses:
        • 1 mg (0.1 mL) IM [weight_min=0, weight=0, min=0, max=0]

  - STEP: IV  [IV over 30 minutes]  Add to 2mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Rapid IV administration may cause anaphylactoid reactions with possible hypotension. In the context of prophylaxis, administer over 30 minutes.

Despite the Canadian Pediatric Society's lack of position on IV vitamin K, consider IV administration in premature infants less than 28 weeks of gestation. IM administration in the thigh is difficult due to low muscle mass.
      particularity: Protect from light
