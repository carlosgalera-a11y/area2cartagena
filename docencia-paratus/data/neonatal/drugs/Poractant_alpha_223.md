# Poractant alpha (Curosurf®)
- Drug ID: 223
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Respiratory distress syndrome of the newborn
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1st dose  [Administer endotracheal undiluted over 5 sec]  By multi-access endotracheal catheter by classic or less invasive method
      concentration: 80 mg/mL
      administration: Attention:
• Opinion of Neonatology recommended
• Administration with respiratory therapist
• Avoid endotracheal aspiration within 2 hours after administration.

Technique:
1) Warm the vial at room temperature for 20 minutes or by placing it in the hands for 5 minutes, 
2) Gently swirl, without shaking, to obtain a uniform whitish suspension.
3) Gently flush the endotracheal catheter with 1 mL of air after administration to avoid risk of obstruction of the endotracheal tube. 
4) Adjust ventilatory parameters : Lung compliance and oxygenation may improve very quickly after instillation; it is essential to quickly adjust the ventilatory parameters to avoid hyperoxia and pulmonary distension. Risk of pneumothorax if pressure ventilation.

Additional informations:
• 1st dose of 200 mg/Kg, repeat a 2nd and 3rd dose of 100 mg/Kg every 12 hours if necessary.
• Natural porcine lung surfactant
• Salvage treatment of idiopathic respiratory distress syndrome (RDS) of premature newborns (> 25 weeks), neonatal RDS associated with meconium aspiration, pulmonary hamorrhage or pneumonia
      particularity: Keep refrigerated
Protect from light
Unopened vial can be stored for a maximum of 24 hours at room temperature and can be refrigerated again (once only)
