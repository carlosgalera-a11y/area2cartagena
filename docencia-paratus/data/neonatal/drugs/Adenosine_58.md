# Adenosine (Adenocard®)
- Drug ID: 58
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Supraventricular Tachycardia (SVT)
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 1st dose  [IV over 1-3 seconds]
      concentration: 3 mg/mL
      administration: Use a good caliber proximal venous access such as the right upper extremity elbow crease.
Administration via a distal peripheral route (eg: access on the foot) decreases the chances of success and may require higher doses (eg: 0.3 mg/Kg).

Ideally administer while recording a 12-lead ECG.
Defibrillator and resuscitation cart nearby.

Double-syringe or 3-way stopcock administration technique:

Preparation:
1) Withdraw the dose of adenosine, with an additional 0.3 mL, with a 1 or 3 mL syringe
2) Open the valve to inlet 1 (inlet 2 blocked, patient outlet open)
3) Screw the adenosine syringe onto inlet 1 and purge the tap with 0.3 mL of adenosine
4) Adjust the volume of adenosine to the desired dose in the 1 or 3 mL syringe
5) Block entry 1 (adenosine)
6) Screw a 5 mL syringe of NaCl 0.9% onto inlet 2
7) Purge the 3-way stopcock with approximately 2 mL of NaCl 0.9%

Administration:
1) Connect the output of the device to the patient
2) Open the valve to inlet 1 (adenosine) and administer the adenosine in 1-3 seconds
3) Open the valve to inlet 2 (NaCl 0.9%) and rinse QUICKLY with 1-3 mL of NaCl 0.9%
      particularity: Consider reducing the dose by 50% if: 
- Administrated via central venous access
