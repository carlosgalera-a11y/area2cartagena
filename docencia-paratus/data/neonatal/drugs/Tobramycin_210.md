# Tobramycin (Nebcin®)
- Drug ID: 210
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Usual Dosage
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 3-5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 1 mL of NaCl 0,9%
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Attention:
• Different concentrations available
• Usual neonatal dosing according to postmenstrual and postnatal age: (Postmenstrual age = gestational age + postnatal age)

Neonatal dosing:
Postmenstrual age < 30 weeks:
0 to 28 days : 3.8 mg/Kg q36h
More than 28 days : 3.2 mg/Kg q24h

Postmenstrual age 30 à 39 weeks:
0 to 28 days : 3.2 mg/Kg q24h
More than 28 days : 3 mg/Kg q18h

Postmenstrual age ≥ 40 weeks:
0 to 7 days : 3 mg/Kg q18h
7 to 28 days : 3 mg/Kg q12h
For infants over 28 days, see dosing in Pediatric section

Additional information:
• IV administration over 3-5 minutes is safe according to the literature.
• It is possible to administer the drug over a longer period according to the hospital protocols in place.
• Secondary dilution not necessary.
• Can be administered IM.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 

Because of the risk of nephrotoxicity, consider another class of antibiotics in patients with CrCl less than 30 mL/min or in acute renal failure.

The first dose of an anti-infective medication (mg) should not be influenced by the presence or absence of renal insufficiency in order to achieve an effective concentration quickly. If adjustments are necessary, these should be applied at subsequent doses.
