# Dopamine (Intropin®)
- Drug ID: 65
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Shock
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP: 
      concentration: 800 mcg/mL
      concentration note: Premixed commercial bag
      administration: Caution:
Use the lowest concentration if a peripheral venous access is used.

Preferred concentration:
• Under 1 Kg = 800 mcg/mL
• 1 and 3 Kg = 1600 mcg/mL
• Over 3 Kg or in fluid restriction = 3200 mcg/mL. 

Suggested titration:
1) Start infusion at 5 mcg/kg/min.
2) Titrate by increments of 2.5-5 mcg/kg/min q5min until target BP is reached or a maximum dose of 20 mcg/Kg/min. (Expert Opinion)

Additional information:
Rinse the tubing with the infusion before administration.
      particularity: Y-site compatible with D5W, NaCl, LR. 
Starting dose 5 mcg/Kg/min ("renal" dose of 2-3 mcg/Kg/min not effective according to the literature).

Although the preferred route of administration of vasopressors is via central venous access, multiple studies demonstrate that administration via a good quality peripheral access is safe if used for a short period of time and with frequent verifications of the access site.
