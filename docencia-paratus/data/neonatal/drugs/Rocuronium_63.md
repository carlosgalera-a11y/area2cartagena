# Rocuronium (Zemuron®)
- Drug ID: 63
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Intubation- Neuromuscular Blocker
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 10 mg/mL
      administration: Rocuronium can also be used as an alternative neuromuscular blocking agent.

For emergency reversal of rocuronium, use Sugammadex 16 mg/Kg.
      particularity: Keep refrigerated.
Stable at room temperature for 6 months.

With dose of 1-1.2 mg/Kg:
Onset of action: 45-60 seconds
Duration of action: 30-45 minutes
