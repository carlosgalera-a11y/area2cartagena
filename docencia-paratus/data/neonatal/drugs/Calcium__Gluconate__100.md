# Calcium (Gluconate) (CaGluc)
- Drug ID: 100
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Hyperkaliemia
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 5 min]  Administer IV undiluted or diluted up to 1mL of NaCl 0.9%
      concentration: 100 mg/mL
      concentration note: Dose expressed in mg of Calcium Gluconate
      administration: IV push in cardiac arrest
May be administered diluted IV over 15-30 minutes if patient is stable.

Administer preferably via proximal peripheral venous access if central access not available (hyperosmolar solution).

Hyperkaliemia in neonatology is defined as K+> 6.5-7.5 mmol/L. Neonates often tolerate blood levels of K+ higher than children and adults. In doubt, control the K+ level and look for anomaly on the 12-lead EKG.
      particularity: In elemental calcium equivalents, 1 g of calcium chloride = 3 g calcium gluconate.
