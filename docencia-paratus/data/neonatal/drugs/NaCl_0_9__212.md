# NaCl 0.9% (Bolus)
- Drug ID: 212
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Fluid bolus
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV over 5-10 minutes]  Administer on volume/time pump
      administration: For patients < 32 weeks, if the clinical condition allows, favor administration over at least 10 minutes to reduce the risk of intraventricular hemorrhage.

Repeat as needed with clinical reassessment between each bolus. In the absence of confirmed neonatal anemia, consider early administration of vasopressors after 1 bolus.
