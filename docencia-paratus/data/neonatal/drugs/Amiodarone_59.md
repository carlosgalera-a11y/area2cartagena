# Amiodarone (Cordarone®)
- Drug ID: 59
- Type: drug
- Entorno: neonatal

## Indicación: Pulseless VT/VF
- Categoría: Uncategorized
- Weight type: 1

  - STEP:   [IV push]  Administer IV undiluted
      concentration: 50 mg/mL
      administration: According to PALS, repeat dose up to a maximum of 3 doses every 2 rhythm analysis/pulse checks/shocks (q4min).

The 0.22 micron filter can be omitted in cardiac arrest
