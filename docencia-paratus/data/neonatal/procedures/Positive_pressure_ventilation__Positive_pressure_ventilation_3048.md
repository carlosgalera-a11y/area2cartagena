## Tab: Preparation
## Indications
    Newborn requiring positive pressure ventilation (PPV)
## Contraindications
  ### Absolute
    Suspected or confirmed congenital diaphragmatic hernia
## Equipment preparation
    
    Recommended device for neonatal resuscitation (pressure stability and lung protection) Allows for: Requires a source of compressed gas Ensure the availability of a self-inflating bag in case of failure of the compressed gas source

## 5
    Place a pulse oximeter on the right hand or wrist (Pre-ductal) Adjust the FiO₂ as needed to achieve saturation targets
## 6
    
    Occlude the T-piece with the thumb or index to deliver ventilation Respiratory rate 40-60 breaths per minute Ventilate...Two...Three
