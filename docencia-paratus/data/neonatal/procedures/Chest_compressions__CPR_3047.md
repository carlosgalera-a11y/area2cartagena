# Chest compressions — CPR

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Circulation & Access
- Entorno: neonatal
- Indication ID: 3047


## Tab: Default
## Indications
    Initiate if heart rate < 60 / min despite optimal ventilation (advanced airway management)
    [PROCEDURE] Neonatal resuscitation — Resuscitation
## 1
    
    Place thumbs at the level of the sternum below the inter-nipple line The remaining fingers are under the back as support
## 2
    
    Compress to a depth of approximately 1/3 of the anteroposterior diameter of the chest The compression rate should be 90 / min Keep hands in constant contact with the chest at all time:
## 3
    Total of 120 events per minute (Compression + Ventilation) 3 compressions + 1 ventilation every 2 seconds One-and-Two-and-Three-and-Breathe-and...
