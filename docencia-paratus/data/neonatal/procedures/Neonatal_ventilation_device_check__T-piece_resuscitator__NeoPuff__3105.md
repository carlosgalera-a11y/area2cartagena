# Neonatal ventilation device check — T-piece resuscitator (NeoPuff®)

- Tipo: procedure
- Categoría: Airway & Ventilation
- Entorno: neonatal
- Indication ID: 3105


## Tab: Preparation
## Equipment preparation
    
    Recommended device for neonatal resuscitation (pressure stability and lung protection) Allows for: Requires a source of compressed gas Ensure the availability of a self-inflating bag in case of failure of the compressed gas source

## Tab: Execution
## 1
    
    Connect the device to the compressed gas source (air–O₂ blender) Attach the oxygen tubing to the flowmeter of the blender and to the gas inlet of the device Set the oxygen flow rate to 10 L/min Connect the ventilation tubing to the gas outlet of the device
## 2
    
    Block both ends of the T-piece (using a cap or by cleanly covering them with your hands)
## 3
    
    Turn the PIP (Peak Inspiratory Pressure) knob fully clockwise Check that the safety pressure shown on the manometer is between 30–40 cmH₂O If the maximum pressure needs to be adjusted, use the Max-Pressure knob located behind the removable cover
## 4
    
    Keep both ends of the T-piece blocked Adjust to achieve a PIP (Peak Inspiratory Pressure) of 20–25 cmH₂O
## 5
    
    Keep the circuit blocked, but release the top of the T-piece (rotary valve) Use the valve to adjust the PEEP to 5 cmH₂O
